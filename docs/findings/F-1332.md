## Finding

The binder shard (`binder.md` plus four sub-pages) carries no Non-goals (or scope) section anywhere. The two features the binder defers past loom 1.0 — the **binder refinement loop** and **automatic context escalation** — are only discoverable from inside the shard by following two `> **loom 1.0 seam — …**` blockquotes mid-prose (anchors `loom-1-0-seam-binder-refinement-loop` on `binder-bypass-and-envelope.md` and `loom-1-0-seam-automatic-context-escalation` on `binder-model-and-context.md`). The canonical inventory lives out-of-shard at `future-considerations/surface-extensions.md`, which a reader scanning the binder spec has no reason to open.

The binder hub `binder.md` is the natural place to surface this: it opens with a positioning paragraph framing what the binder *is*, but says nothing about what loom 1.0's binder deliberately omits. The two deferred features are categorically distinct from the project-wide loom 1.0 *non-goals* (which reserve no seam) — the refinement loop and automatic context escalation both reserve a seam (carriers pinned with `MUST` modality, e.g. BNDR-1/2/3, the re-entrancy invariant). The section to add is a binder-scoped *deferrals* index forward-linking the canonical owner, not a copy of the project-wide non-goals.

## Solution Space

**Shape:** single

### Recommendation

Add a `## Non-goals for loom 1.0` section to `docs/spec_topics/binder.md` (the hub), after the opening paragraphs and before `## Contents`. Enumerate exactly the two deferred features that carry binder-shard seam carriers, with anchored cross-references to the canonical owner sub-buckets on `future-considerations/surface-extensions.md`:

- **Binder refinement loop** — multi-turn `needs_info` negotiation instead of single-shot "system note then stop". Link to the surface-extensions bucket and note the in-shard seam carriers are BNDR-1/2/3.
- **Automatic context escalation** — re-issuing a failed binder call with `bind_context: session`. Link to the *gated on an open design decision* bucket; carry the *Decision required* status so it is not mistaken for a committed deferral.

Framing: state these are *binder-shard deferrals that reserve a forward-compatibility seam* (category 2), distinct from the project-wide *loom 1.0 non-goals* (which reserve no seam). The section is orientation, not normative — no new obligation, no REQ-ID, no anchor other pages cite (keeps GOV-30/GOV-31 satisfied). Do not add the section to the four sub-pages; leave the seam blockquotes in place.

## Consequence

**Severity:** advisory

A reader doing a scope sweep of the binder spec cannot confirm what loom 1.0 deliberately leaves out without opening a sibling shard; an implementer who never opens `surface-extensions.md` may not realise that BNDR-1/2/3, the three-arm envelope discriminator, the `ambiguous.candidates` field, and the re-entrancy invariant are forward-compatibility seams. Nothing breaks at runtime.
