## Problem

That home (`#err-18`) covers the operand-type precondition, the `question-outside-result-fn` scope precondition, implicit-return inference, and the no-rollback guarantee (`#err-13`), but never states the discarded-tail interaction. A reader auditing `?` semantics outward from `error-model.md` never encounters the rule, and sibling pages must link `grammar.md#block-expressions` to cite a `?` behavioural rule that has no anchor of its own. This breaches the one-canonical-home-per-concept discipline (GOV-30).
