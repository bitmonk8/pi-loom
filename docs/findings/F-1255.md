## Problem

The `V13c-T` tests leaf omits the `ERR-13` delegated live-carrier witness bullet and the `V4f` dependency that its paired impl leaf `V13c` carries: its Tests list stops after the depth-6 co-fire bullet and its **Deps** are `V13b, V9c, V16a, V5e, V8c, H4b`. `V4f` delegates the live-surface re-assertion of its completed-callee-finality vector to `V13c` (among others), and `coverage-matrix.md`'s `ERR-13` row lists `V13c` as a co-witness. Under the per-phase TDD ritual, an implementer authors the failing tests from `V13c-T` first; because the `ERR-13` bullet is absent there, the delegated witness is never authored red and `V13c` can ship green without verifying it, while `V13c-T` stays pickable without `V4f` complete.
