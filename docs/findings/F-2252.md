## Problem

The Pi version-bump procedure is a numbered 1–7 sequence. The *Editorial-review checklist for unpinned host presuppositions* (items (a)–(aq)) is rendered as an unnumbered, indented preamble at the top of `version-bump-step2.md`, sitting between numbered step 1 and the numbered `2. Re-run the SDK surface-inventory tests.` line, and is never assigned a position in the 1–7 sequence. The wayfinding contradicts itself: `version-bump-intro.md`'s non-goals call the checklist a "step 2 audit" item, yet the numbered `2.` in the file is a different obligation (the build-time SDK surface-inventory tests at `#bump-step-2-positive` / `#bump-step-2-negative`), and every checklist item cites "step 2(a) above" — pointing backwards to text that physically appears after the checklist in reading order. A contributor cannot tell whether the checklist is the step-2 audit, a separate unnumbered step, or where in the execution order it runs.

## Issue introduction
**Verdict:** introduced by a specific later commit (not present at the procedure's inception)
**Introducing commit:** `52b9101` — *pi-loom spec: resolve "T24 — Fork-reason watcher closure leaves the extension in an unspecified, silently degraded state"* (2026-05-11)
**History:** The spec corpus is git-tracked. The numbered version-bump procedure was introduced at `b0cdf1b` (2026-05-06) as a clean numbered list (steps 1–6 at the time, later 1–7) with no editorial-review checklist — `git show b0cdf1b -- spec_topics/pi-integration-contract.md` shows step 2 as the bare "Re-run the H1 SDK surface-inventory test", so no orphaned block existed. `git log -S "Editorial-review checklist for unpinned host presuppositions"` and `git log -S "audit the candidate Pi minor against"` both first surface the checklist at `52b9101`, where it was inserted as a 4-space-indented, unnumbered preamble immediately after numbered step 1 and before numbered step 2 — the exact orphaned structure present today. `git log -S "step 1.5"` over the file's history (and its pre-split monolith `spec_topics/pi-integration-contract.md`) returns nothing, confirming the checklist was never assigned an intermediate step number. The subsequent item expansions ((f)…(aq)) and the `81bf52a` file split (`spec/` → `spec_topics/`) carried the unnumbered structure forward unchanged. The defect therefore entered at `52b9101` when the checklist was first added, and has persisted unmodified since.

## Solution approach

Restructure step 2 in `version-bump-step2.md` so its opening declares both arms it owns — the editorial-review checklist (items (a)–(aq)) and the build-time SDK surface-inventory tests (`#bump-step-2-positive` / `#bump-step-2-negative`) — positioning the checklist as the editorial-review arm of step 2 rather than as an unnumbered preamble, matching the "step 2 audit" phrasing already in `version-bump-intro.md`'s non-goals. State the checklist's execution order within the 1–7 sequence explicitly. Repair the checklist items' "step 2(a) above" references so they resolve under the rendered order.
