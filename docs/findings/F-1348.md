## Problem

`host-prerequisites.md` carries normative MUSTs that lack co-located `PIC-N` REQ-ID anchors: the `typebox` `"*"` MUST-NOT-be-collapsed rule (cited by `cka-21`), the `peerDependencies` lock-step co-move MUST, and the deliberate-deviation re-write-on-Pi-minor-change MUST. The page already owns `PIC-7`, but these sites are un-anchored, so GOV-9's `#prefix-n` cross-link contract is unsatisfiable for them. Two readers can disagree on an obligation's scope and per-facet citing tests cannot bind to a stable cite-handle.

Update `cka-21`'s citation to point at the coined closed set, and leave `PIC-7` untouched.
