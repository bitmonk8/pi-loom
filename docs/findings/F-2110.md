## Problem

The *Hint* column of the `loom/load/typed-query-unsupported-provider` row in `code-registry-load.md` is normative author-facing text — `diagnostic-shape.md`'s column legend defines it as the hint rendered verbatim to authors at diagnostic time. Its third remediation arm tells authors to "wait for the deferred JSON-mode fallback," a feature with no loom 1.0 availability, so the guidance is non-actionable. The arm also couples the hint to a deferred future-work item (catalogued in `future-considerations/surface-extensions.md`) that will go stale if the deferral status changes, while the two other arms (switch provider; drop the typed-query expressions) stand on their own.

## Issue introduction
**Verdict:** introduced-by-commit
**Introducing commits:** `82be125` (2026-05-04, "spec: adopt P1 typed-query two-phase loop (Option A)")
**History:** `git log -S 'deferred JSON-mode fallback'` and `git log -G 'wait for the deferred JSON-mode fallback'` (with `--follow` across the later `spec/` → `spec_topics/` collapse and the `diagnostics.md` → `code-registry-load.md` split) trace the clause to a single origin: the same commit that first introduced the `loom/load/typed-query-unsupported-provider` row added the *Hint* text already carrying the "or wait for the deferred JSON-mode fallback in [Future Considerations]" arm. The defect was present from the row's inception, not the product of a later interaction; subsequent commits (the V1 → loom 1.0 rename, the diagnostics-file split under the size-cap commit `f5e89f4`) preserved the clause verbatim.

## Solution approach

Delete the trailing "wait for the deferred JSON-mode fallback" arm and its `[Future Considerations]` link from the *Hint* cell of the `loom/load/typed-query-unsupported-provider` row in `code-registry-load.md`, and repair the list grammar of the two surviving arms. The future widening remains documented in `future-considerations/surface-extensions.md`.
