## Problem

`defaulting-system-note-echo.md`'s H1 `# Defaulting system note echo` concatenates the page's three H2 topics (`Defaulting`, `System-note rendering`, `Echo policy`) with no punctuation, so it reads as one garbled phrase rather than a list of the page's subjects.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** single-commit
**Introducing commits:** 7a6cf96 — pi-loom spec: resolve "V1 seam-preservation MUSTs hidden inside the deferred-features narrative page" (2026-05-07, Thomas Andersen); f5e89f4 — docs: enforce 100KB hard / 30KB recommended size cap on spec set (2026-06-04, Thomas Andersen)
**History:** The "carrier" coinage entered at 7a6cf96 as "V1 carriers" when the deferred seam-preservation MUSTs were lifted into their own normative obligations; the V1→loom 1.0 prose rename (a6d82db, 2026-05-29) rephrased it to "loom 1.0 carriers" without defining it, and f5e89f4 carried it verbatim into the new `binder/` subtree. The two defects have distinct single-commit origins and do not interact.

## Solution approach

Rename the H1 of `defaulting-system-note-echo.md` to an enumerated form matching its three H2 sections, e.g. `# Defaulting, system-note rendering, and echo`.
