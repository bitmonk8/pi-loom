## Problem

`frontmatter-fields-a.md#argument-hint-binder-only` is the spec's declared single owner of the `argument-hint` autocomplete contract, and `pi-integration.md` and `slash-invocation.md` both forward-link to it rather than restating its rationale. `registration-steps.md` step 3's `pi.registerCommand` `description`-field paragraph instead restates that owned rationale inline — the clause asserting Pi's extension-command autocomplete UI has no `argumentHint` slot. This is a third statement of a contract the owning paragraph reserves to itself, so a future edit to the owner can leave step 3 to drift into a divergent statement of the same contract.

## Issue introduction
**Verdict:** multi-commit-interaction
**Introducing commits:** 1e103d5 — spec: rescope argument-hint to binder grounding only (Decision #7) (2026-05-05, Thomas Andersen); 4da6c6c — pi-loom spec: resolve "argument-hint field contract is defined once and duplicated" (2026-06-06, Thomas Andersen)
**History:** Commit 1e103d5 introduced the `argument-hint`-not-concatenated rationale into the registration paragraph of the then-monolithic `pi-integration-contract.md` (carried verbatim into `registration-steps.md` by the f5e89f4 size-cap split) at the same time it added the parallel restatements in `pi-integration.md` and `slash-invocation.md`. The later de-duplication pass 4da6c6c designated `frontmatter-fields-a.md#argument-hint-binder-only` as the single owner and converted the `pi-integration.md` and `slash-invocation.md` restatements to forward-links — but left the `registration-steps.md` restatement untouched, so the defect is the residue of an incomplete corrective pass rather than either commit alone.

## Solution approach

In `registration-steps.md` step 3's `#slash-handler-registration` paragraph, keep the registration-specific assertion that `pi.registerCommand`'s `description` argument carries `frontmatter.description` verbatim and that `argument-hint` is not concatenated. Replace the trailing autocomplete-rationale clause (why Pi's extension-command UI has no `argumentHint` slot) with a forward-link to the owning definition at `frontmatter-fields-a.md#argument-hint-binder-only`, mirroring the forward-link convention `pi-integration.md` and `slash-invocation.md` already use.

## Solution constraints

- The existing `binder.md` binder-grounding reference is a distinct contract from the `argument-hint` autocomplete contract owned by `#argument-hint-binder-only`; it remains and is not folded into the new forward-link.
