## Problem

`V15a`'s **Ships when** asserts that `npm test` "resolves and spawns an invoke across the fresh-vs-attach cross-mode matrix", but spawning a live invoke across that matrix requires the subagent `AgentSession` spawn sequence (owned by `V9i`) and the prompt-mode conversation drive (owned by `V9c`), plus the end-to-end harness `H4a`/`H4b`. Neither `V15a` nor `V15a-T` declares any of that machinery in **Deps**, so the **Ships when** is unsatisfiable from the leaf's declared collaborators. `V15a`'s **Adds** scopes the leaf to cross-mode matrix *selection*, so the **Ships when** overclaims relative to the leaf's own scope. A contributor can pick `V15a`, then either invent a stand-in spawn/attach surface that may diverge from the real `V9i`/`V9c` surfaces or stall.
