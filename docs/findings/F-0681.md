## Problem

The opening paragraph of `docs/spec_topics/diagnostics.md` claims Pi "would otherwise push" factory-time bootstrap registration / subscription failures into `LoadExtensionsResult.errors` while loom emits its own `loom/load/extension-bootstrap-failed` instead тАФ with no anchor citation and no cross-reference to the no-double-report invariant that makes the claim hold. Every other concrete Pi-shape claim in the corpus is pinned to an anchor; this one stands alone, leaving nothing to re-validate on a Pi minor bump. The same paragraph also asserts the diagnostics defined here "all fire after the extension is already live", which two rows contradict: `loom/load/extension-bootstrap-failed` and `loom/load/host-incompatible` are both emitted at factory / capability-probe time, before any scan.

## Solution approach

Replace the universal "all fire after the extension is already live" clause with a two-window statement separating the factory-time / `session_start`-time codes тАФ `loom/load/host-incompatible` and `loom/load/extension-bootstrap-failed` тАФ from the post-bootstrap codes that fire during scan, watcher reload, or slash-command execution.
