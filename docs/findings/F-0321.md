## Problem

The §Pass/fail criterion paragraph of `real-host-smoke-gate.md` says the live smoke run is scored on model-output-invariant observables "not on exact reproduction of `H7a`'s double-recorded goldens". The compound term `double-recorded goldens` is introduced without definition; its referent — the committed golden transcript and golden diagnostics list `H7a` records against the in-process Pi session double — lives in `H7a-integration-acceptance.md` and cannot be recovered from the smoke note alone. The modifier `double-recorded` reads plausibly as "recorded twice" rather than "recorded against the in-process double", blurring the very contrast the sentence draws. The note is a self-contained governance protocol whose audience (the bump/merge contributor and the `H6a` release-gate auditor) is distinct from the `H7a` implementer, so it should not silently depend on an `H7a`-internal term for its scoring boundary.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** present-since-inception
**Introducing commits:** fd22a3d
**History:** `real-host-smoke-gate.md` was added as a new file (`git log --follow --name-status` shows a single `A` status, no rename predecessor) in commit fd22a3d (2026-06-17), with `double-recorded goldens` already present in the §Pass/fail criterion. `git log -S"double-recorded"` and `git log -G"double-recorded goldens"` over the file both return only fd22a3d, confirming the undefined compound term has existed since the file's inception; no earlier revision defined the term and later orphaned its definition.

## Solution approach

In `real-host-smoke-gate.md` §Pass/fail criterion, rewrite the phrase `` `H7a`'s double-recorded goldens `` to name its explicit referent inline — `H7a`'s committed golden transcript and golden diagnostics list recorded against the in-process Pi session double — so the scoring contrast is legible without opening `H7a`. The other two `goldens` uses in the same paragraph (`deterministic goldens` in criterion (b), `exact-match goldens` in the closing in-process-gate sentence) become unambiguous once the lead-in names the referent and need no separate rewrite.

## Solution constraints

- Out of scope: `H7a-integration-acceptance.md` is the read-only definition source for these terms — do not edit it.
