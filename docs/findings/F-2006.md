## Finding

"The candidate patch" is ambiguous: it could refer to (a) a candidate new Pi patch version (the usual sense in this section, where skew arises from a Pi patch) or (b) a candidate loom patch version. The section consistently discusses Pi patch releases, but "candidate patch" modifying loom-contributor tooling (`npm run typecheck`) could lead a reader to infer it means a loom contributor is evaluating a loom patch rather than a Pi package update.
