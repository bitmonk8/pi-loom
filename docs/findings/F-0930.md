## Finding

`V9i`'s **Ships when** line reads `npm test` spawns an isolated subagent session and asserts `dispose()`-in-`finally` idempotency. The leaf's **Tests** block, however, gates substantially more: `PIC-9` (single-owner session + idempotent dispose), `PIC-22` (N=3 parallel-spawn — all `createAgentSession` calls dispatched before any returns), the RET final-value propagation contract against the `V3d` function-result seam, the `loom/runtime/subagent-model-unresolved` / `loom/runtime/subagent-dispose-failure` diagnostics, and `ERR-8` (mid-stream cancellation isolation, the delegated live-carrier witness `V4c` defers).

Per `conventions.md` §Leaf format, the binding obligations of a leaf are its **Tests** REQ-ID bullets *and* its **Ships when** gate, so the parallel-spawn and cancellation-isolation contracts are not actually ungated. The defect is that **Ships when** — the field a reader scans as the leaf's acceptance summary and the phase-exit gate keys on (§Per-phase TDD ritual, exit-gate step) — under-represents the surface. A reader treating the line as the authoritative acceptance criterion can conclude `PIC-22` parallel-spawn and `ERR-8` cancellation isolation are out of the leaf's closing scope.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** multi-commit-interaction
**Introducing commits:** `7a3b907` (2026-06-10, added the `PIC-22` parallel-spawn Tests bullet), `7a8565a` (2026-06-11, added the RET final-value Tests bullet), `7bd65a7` (2026-06-18, added the `ERR-8` cancellation-isolation Tests bullet); `6d7661d` (2026-06-17) trimmed **Ships when** back to its inception scope.
**History:** The plan corpus is git-tracked. At inception (`c6a664e`, 2026-06-10) `Ships when` already omitted the model-guard diagnostics relative to the Tests block, so a minor under-sell was present-since-inception. The gap then widened across three independent Tests-widening commits (`7a3b907`, `7a8565a`, `7bd65a7`), none of which re-synced the **Ships when** summary. The only commit that touched **Ships when** after inception (`75a9bcd` expanded it to mention the swallowing-handler facet; `6d7661d` then trimmed that facet out when the concern split to `V9o`) returned the line to its inception scope rather than reconciling it against the now-larger Tests block. The current under-sell is therefore the cumulative product of Tests growth never being mirrored into **Ships when**, not a single edit.

## Solution Space

**Shape:** single

### Recommendation

In `docs/plan_topics/V9i-subagent-isolation.md`, broaden the **Ships when** field so its enumerated acceptance surface matches the gated Tests block. Add references to the `PIC-22` parallel-spawn assertion and the `ERR-8` cancellation-isolation assertion alongside the existing isolated-session-spawn and `dispose()`-in-`finally` idempotency clauses; optionally also name the RET final-value propagation and the `subagent-model-unresolved` model-guard diagnostic so every Tests REQ-ID has a corresponding acceptance clause. Keep the line a concise observable-change summary that points at the Tests bullets rather than restating their assertion text (contrast `V9g`, which over-restates).

## Consequence

**Severity:** advisory

The Tests bullets remain binding regardless of the thin **Ships when** line, so no contract is silently dropped. The risk is narrative: a reader scanning the acceptance summary may treat the `PIC-22` parallel-spawn and `ERR-8` cancellation-isolation contracts as outside the leaf's closing scope and under-prioritise them when judging completeness.
