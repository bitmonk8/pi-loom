## Problem

The `masked` field paragraph in `diagnostic-shape.md` (`#masked-field-hard-ceiling-co-fire`) never directly states the field's loom 1.0 disposition: that `masked` is never populated on any diagnostic-shape surface. The reader must assemble that conclusion from a lead sentence that reads as "`loom/runtime/invoke-depth-exceeded` is the diagnostic on which `masked` can be populated" and a much later parenthetical noting that surface's reachable mask domain is empty. An implementer who anchors on the lead sentence can build a co-fire detection probe at the `invoke-depth-exceeded` site, when the correct behaviour for every diagnostic-shape surface in loom 1.0 is unconditional omission.

## Issue introduction
**Verdict:** multi-commit-interaction
**Introducing commits:** 4ccc072, 7598f50
**History:** The `masked` field was introduced wholesale in `4ccc072` ("Masked ceilings: no observability requirement for the unreported breach"). That commit authored the `diagnostics.md` paragraph with the misreadable framing — "It is populated only on diagnostics … in V1 this is the `loom/runtime/invoke-depth-exceeded` row" — while deferring the per-site reachable mask domain to the spec.md interaction paragraph ("The per-site reachable mask domain is fixed by the interaction paragraph in `spec.md`"), so the empty-domain fact for `invoke-depth-exceeded` lived on a different page from the "populated only" framing. `7598f50` ("CIO-6 hard-ceiling co-fire: predicate, test vector, and normative ownership") moved domain ownership to PIC-1 and inlined the parenthetical "(in V1: empty for `loom/runtime/invoke-depth-exceeded`, so this diagnostic always omits `masked`)" into the same paragraph — co-locating both halves without ever collapsing them into a single direct "never populated in loom 1.0" statement. The ambiguity is the joint product of the two edits: the first supplied the misleading lead sentence, the second appended the corrective clause but left the lead intact.

## Solution approach

Clarify the `masked` field paragraph in `diagnostic-shape.md` so it states directly, before the PIC-1 delegation, that `masked` is never populated on any diagnostic-shape surface in loom 1.0 — the only such hard-ceiling surface, `loom/runtime/invoke-depth-exceeded`, has an empty reachable mask domain and always omits the field, and the only non-empty `masked` enumeration occurs on the runtime-event channel (`event.masked`), not on a diagnostic. Rewrite the lead sentence so `invoke-depth-exceeded` reads as the sole diagnostic-shape co-fire-eligible surface whose reachable domain is empty, rather than as the surface that carries the field.

## Solution constraints

- Out of scope: the per-site reachable mask domain, the V1 reachable predicate, and the masking-probe MUST remain owned by PIC-1 — do not relocate or restate that normative ownership.
- Preserve the existing `MUST NOT emit masked: []` omission rule.
