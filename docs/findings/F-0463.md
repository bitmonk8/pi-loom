## Problem

In `real-host-smoke-gate.md`, trigger-(1) (a Pi version bump) has a concrete pre-merge recovery — a confirmed divergence blocks the bump and `V18d` restores the prior Pi-SDK pin — but trigger-(2) (a merge whose diff touches the four fidelity-contract axes) has no equivalent. Recognising a trigger-(2) event is a manual contributor obligation with no automatic detector, and the note states an unrecognised divergence is "detectable only after the fact" via the `H6a` release-gate commit-message audit but never says what to do once an already-merged divergence is found. Separately, a trigger event firing before `H7a` lands is "deferred" and recorded in the triggering commit message, but nothing reconciles recorded deferrals against recorded re-runs, so a deferred trigger-(2) event can be recorded and then never re-run with no signal at `H6a`. The combined asymmetry lets a real double-vs-real-host divergence reach `main` with no documented recovery and no audit reconciliation.

## Solution approach

In `real-host-smoke-gate.md`, bound the trigger-(2) clause with a concrete touch-list naming the production-seam and double-modelling hints for each of the four fidelity-contract axes, so a contributor checks their diff against a named list rather than re-deriving "touches the four axes" from prose. Add after-the-fact remediation prose: when the `H6a` audit (or any later observation) surfaces an unrecorded or skipped trigger-(2) run, re-run the smoke against the merged change and, on a confirmed divergence, land a revert or follow-up fix. Add deferred-run reconciliation prose stating that recorded deferrals carry forward and are reconciled against recorded re-runs at the `H6a` release-gate audit, reusing the existing triggering-commit-message evidence record. Land the recovery prose on the bounded trigger definition.

## Solution constraints

- Out of scope: `V18d` owns trigger-(1)'s pre-merge pin restore; trigger-(2) recovery is a post-merge re-run plus revert/follow-up and must stay distinct from a pin restore.
- Route deferred-run reconciliation through the existing triggering-commit-message evidence record `H6a` already audits; do not introduce a separate artefact file (the spec's "produces no separate artefact file" convention).
- The smoke remains a manual contributor obligation; do not add a CI hook or automatic diff detector.
