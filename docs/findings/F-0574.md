## Problem

`V17a` (with its `V17a-T` pair) carries two independently-shippable cancellation mechanisms in one leaf. The first is the forwarding contract — `loomAbort` construction, forwarding the three entry-point signals into `loomAbort`, downward-only propagation, and `CNCL-4` reason propagation. The second is the race-semantics surface — the `CNCL-1`/`CNCL-2`/`CNCL-3` late-settlement discard rules, the `CNCL-5`/`CNCL-6` no-rewrite and no-tail-synthesis rules, and the three-side-channel swallowing-handler suppression at the `V8a` `Checkpoint`-seam substrate. The forwarding contract is observable through the three entry-point harness paths and the race-semantics surface through the `Checkpoint`-seam substrate; they share only the `loomAbort` primitive, so one leaf collapses two cohesion units into a single TDD red-green batch contrary to `conventions.md` §3's smallest-shippable-feature rule.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** present-since-inception (narrowed by later peels, not eliminated)
**Introducing commit:** `c6a664e` (2026-06-10, "pi-loom plan: build/update plan for spec.md + review") — `V17a` was created as a single leaf carrying the entire cancellation contract.
**History:** Two subsequent atomicity peels narrowed the leaf without separating the two surfaces this finding targets: `0bc84dd` (2026-06-11) peeled the forwarding-listener throw-trap into `V17b`, and `5b95143` (2026-06-18, "resolve 'V17a bundles forwarding contract and checkpoint-site wiring'") peeled the checkpoint-granularity surface into `V17c`. The forwarding-contract and race-semantics surfaces have been co-located in `V17a` continuously since `c6a664e`; the residual bundle is original plan structure, not a regression introduced by a later edit. The repeated peeling trajectory is the corpus's established response pattern to `V17a` over-bundling.

## Solution approach

Split `V17a` so the forwarding contract and the race-semantics surface ship as two leaves. Keep `V17a`/`V17a-T` as the forwarding-contract core (`loomAbort` construction, three-path forwarding, downward-only propagation, `CNCL-4`) and author a new V17-series leaf with its `-T` partner carrying the race-semantics surface (`CNCL-1`/`CNCL-2`/`CNCL-3`, `CNCL-5`/`CNCL-6`, and the swallowing-handler substrate suppression). Move the matching `Adds`/`Tests`/`Ships when` clauses, the `coverage-matrix.md` `CNCL-1,2,3` and `CNCL-5,6` closing-leaf tokens, and the `cka-33` swallowing-substrate row onto the new leaf, re-deriving its `Deps` from the surface it consumes rather than copying `V17a`'s, and retarget the race-dependent dependents and `H5b`'s `Deps` accordingly.
