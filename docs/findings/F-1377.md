## Finding

`V9b`'s **Ships when** gate is meant to be a concrete, externally observable green-gate summary (per `conventions.md` §Leaf format: "**Ships when.** A concrete, externally observable change."). Its final clause instead restates, at near-verbatim length, the entire mechanism already carried by the last **Tests** bullet ("model-reference-matcher production wiring"): construction of the exact-match resolver over `ctx.modelRegistry.getAvailable()`, injection of that single instance into the `V6a` parser seam, and the binding of `V6a`'s `loom/load/model-unresolved` resolution.

It also carries over the observation-method qualifier `(instance identity, not equivalence-of-outcome)`. The observation method describes *how the test observes the behaviour* and belongs in the Tests bullet (where it appears, fuller, as "observed by single-source-of-construction (instance identity), not equivalence-of-outcome against a shared fake"). Ships-when adds no gating criterion beyond "the cited test passes"; the duplicated qualifier is cruft, and a second copy of the mechanism invites the two from drifting out of sync on future edits.

Later commits (`cb8737a` predates it; `b722ce7`, `3424d54`, `c1fa860` follow) reworded the surrounding swap/note clauses and carried the duplicated qualifier through the rename to `V9b-registration-reload-wiring.md` unchanged.

## Solution Space

**Shape:** single

### Recommendation

In `V9b`'s **Ships when**, collapse the final matcher clause so it references the closing Tests bullet instead of re-stating the mechanism and observation method. Strike the verbatim restatement "constructs the model-reference matcher from `ctx.modelRegistry.getAvailable()` once at the load pass, injecting that single instance into the `V6a` parser seam so `V6a`'s `loom/load/model-unresolved` resolution binds that instance (instance identity, not equivalence-of-outcome)" and replace it with a brief pointer such as "wires the model-reference matcher production covered by the last Tests bullet". Leave the last Tests bullet (which carries the full mechanism and the "single-source-of-construction (instance identity), not equivalence-of-outcome against a shared fake" observation method) untouched. The earlier Ships-when clauses (registry swap, `registry-swap-failed`, snapshot read-only, empty-window suppression / same-window rename emission) are unaffected.

## Consequence

**Severity:** cosmetic

The duplicated mechanism and observation-method qualifier add no gating criterion — the gate reduces to "the last Tests bullet's test passes" — and bloat the green-gate summary. The only practical risk is the two copies drifting apart on a later edit; no implementer is misled or blocked.
