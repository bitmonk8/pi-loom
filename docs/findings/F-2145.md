## Problem

`errors-and-results/error-model.md` opens with the complete normative specification of two general-purpose language constructs — the `match` expression (Pattern grammar through arm syntax and exhaustiveness) and the postfix `?` operator (Ok-unwrap / Err early-return semantics and the ERR-18 operand-type precondition) — before any error-model content appears. Neither construct is error-specific: `match` destructures arbitrary arrays, objects, literals, and schema-typed values and is the structural-branch peer of `if` / `for` / `while` owned by `control-flow.md`, and `?` is the expression sublanguage's propagation operator. The language-semantics cluster (`expressions.md`, `control-flow.md`, `grammar.md`) holds only forward-references into the error page: the `expr?` Supported-forms bullet, the operator-precedence postfix-`?` row, and the `match`-arm-body note all defer their normative definition into `error-model.md`. A reader or maintainer seeking the `match` grammar or `?` semantics among the language constructs finds pointers, not definitions.

## Issue introduction
**Verdict:** single-commit
**Introducing commits:** 53f5831 — spec: rewrite query primitive, system prompts, and schema syntax (2026-05-03, Thomas Andersen)
**History:** The initial scaffold (9a53d9c, 2026-05-01) had no `match` or `?` construct. Both were first specified in the spec rewrite 53f5831 (2026-05-03), where they were placed under the monolithic `spec.md` "Errors and Results" section (the `match` pattern grammar appears after the `### Errors and Results` heading, not under `### Control Flow`). The later topic split (fecb504, 2026-05-04) and size-cap split (f5e89f4, 2026-06-04) preserved that grouping by carving `error-model.md` out of the errors cluster with the constructs intact. The placement has therefore existed since the constructs were first written; no later commit relocated correctly-placed text.

## Solution approach

Move the `match` and `?` normative specifications — the `match` content from **Pattern grammar (loom 1.0)** through the `?` operator's ERR-18 precondition — out of `error-model.md` into the language-semantics cluster (a new `docs/spec_topics/match.md`, or appended to `expressions.md`). Leave a forward-reference at the top of `error-model.md` naming `match` and `?` as the `Result`-destructuring constructs and pointing to their new home. Repoint the existing `expressions.md` deferrals (the `expr?` Supported-forms bullet, the operator-precedence postfix-`?` row, the `match`-arm-body note) and any `grammar.md` cross-reference at the new location, and convert `error-model.md`'s in-page references to relocated `match` / `?` text (e.g. in the terminal-outcomes prose) into cross-links.

## Solution constraints

- The `err-18` REQ-ID anchor MUST remain resolvable after the move — either preserved at the relocated text's new location, or every citer updated in the same commit (lock-step anchor governance).
