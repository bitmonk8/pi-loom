## Problem

`V15j` (and its paired `V15j-T`) is the delegated live-carrier witness for `V5e`'s ceiling-#4 routing at the runtime `invoke` boundary, but it appears in no `coverage-matrix.md` row and both Tests bullets cite the obligation only through a prose section link (`ceilings-3-and-4.md — Per-boundary destination/surface table (ceiling #4)`) with no machine-resolvable `cka-N` token. As a result the invoke-boundary ceiling-#4 routing witness is untrackable by the closing-gate machinery: `H5a`/`H5f` have no token to resolve, `H5d` has no row to verify `V15j`'s reachability, and `H5e`'s un-anchored-MUST scan surfaces the obligation with no closing-leaf cell to satisfy it. This diverges from `V15j`'s code-driven peer `V14e`, which is tracked under `cka-13`.
