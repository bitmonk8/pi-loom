## Problem

`V9b` owns the model-reference-matcher production wiring point, and its **Adds**, **Tests**, and **Ships when** (with the bullet mirrored in `V9b-T`'s **Tests**) assert by instance identity that the single matcher instance services both `V6a`'s load-time `loom/load/model-unresolved` resolution and `V11a`'s binder-model resolution. But `V11a` declares `V9b` in its **Deps** and is built strictly after `V9b`, so at `V9b`'s acceptance point `V11a`'s binder-model resolution path does not yet exist — the `V11a` half of the instance-identity witness cannot be exercised red/green at `V9b` time without faking or over-building `V11a`. What `V9b` can legitimately observe is bounded to constructing one matcher, injecting it into the `V6a` seam, and `V6a`'s resolution binding that instance.

**Introducing commit:** `ccedadf` — "pi-loom plan: resolve \"V9b model-reference-matcher wiring untested\"" (2026-06-16)
**History:** The plan corpus is git-tracked. `git log --follow -S` over the leaf (carried under the prior name `V9b-registration-drain-state.md`, renamed to `V9b-registration-reload-wiring.md` in `44a96f5`) shows the matcher-witness bullet and Ships-when clause naming "`V11a`'s binder-model resolution" were net-added in `ccedadf`; the parent tree has zero occurrences. That commit resolved the earlier "V9b model-reference-matcher wiring untested" finding by adding the matcher production-wiring witness to both `V9b` and `V9b-T`, but worded the instance-identity reconciliation to name `V11a`'s resolution path — a path `V9b`'s build point predates. The defect is a side-effect of that fix, not present since the leaf's inception.

## Solution approach

Narrow `V9b`'s model-reference-matcher witness (and the mirrored bullet in `V9b-T`'s **Tests**) to what is observable at `V9b`'s build point — one `findExactModelReferenceMatch` matcher constructed from `ctx.modelRegistry.getAvailable()`, injected into the `V6a` injection seam, with `V6a`'s `loom/load/model-unresolved` resolution binding that instance — and strike the clause naming `V11a`'s binder-model resolution from `V9b`'s **Adds**, **Tests**, and **Ships when**. Add the cross-resolution instance-identity witness to `V11a` (and a paired failing test in `V11a-T`), where both `V6a`'s and `V11a`'s resolution paths co-exist and the single-instance reconciliation can actually be witnessed.
