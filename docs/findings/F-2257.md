## Problem

The step-2 editorial-review checklist in `version-bump-step2.md` is a single flat enumeration of items `(a)`–`(aq)`. Items `(b)`–`(aq)` are recurring per-bump audits sharing a `pass`/`fail`/`N/A` recording shape; item `(a)` is a one-shot, dispositive pre-implementation determination that MUST be resolved once before SM-4/5/6 are implemented and records one of two authoritative verdicts rather than a per-bump row. Item `(a)` nonetheless occupies a peer-bullet position in the same enumeration as the recurring items, so its categorically different obligation class, recording shape, and one-time cadence are conveyed only by inline bold prose, not by structure.

## Issue introduction
**Verdict:** single-commit-introduced
**Introducing commit:** `80fcd63` — "pi-loom spec-plan: fix F-0718+F-0823 — item (a) authoritative-determination evidentiary standard + recording shape" (2026-06-20)
**History:** The checklist has carried item `(a)` as the leading bullet of the `(a)`–`(e)` host-presupposition list since the file was split out of the parent PIC document (`f5e89f4`); at that point item `(a)` was a recurring per-bump audit on the same footing as `(b)`–`(e)`, so its peer-bullet placement was correct and no defect existed. `git log -S` over `version-bump-step2.md` shows the one-shot framing — the "item (a) is the exception" preamble clause and the dedicated "Item (a) authoritative-determination recording shape" sub-block ("one-shot, dispositive open question") — first entering at `80fcd63`. That commit reclassified item `(a)` from a recurring audit to a one-shot dispositive determination while leaving it structurally in place as the leading per-bump bullet; the structural/semantic mismatch this finding describes dates from that reclassification. A later commit, `27a023e` (F-1535), added the bold "Open at the loom 1.0 pin, not merely a future-minor re-audit:" lead-in inside the bullet, reinforcing the one-shot semantics in prose but not resolving the structural placement.

## Solution approach

Promote item `(a)` out of the `(a)`–`(aq)` enumeration into its own subsection placed above the per-bump editorial-review checklist, leaving the recurring checklist running `(b)`–`(aq)`. Retain item `(a)`'s `(a)` label and its `#bump-checklist-instance-survival` anchor so existing cross-references continue to resolve. Reword the per-bump checklist preamble so its recurring-audit scope and `pass`/`fail`/`N/A` recording rule range over `(b)`–`(aq)`.

## Solution constraints

- Do not re-letter items `(b)`–`(aq)`; their labels are referenced by anchor across the corpus (`version-bump-step2b.md`, the plan).
- Item `(a)` must retain its MUST-level editorial-review detection routing after it leaves the items `(a)`–`(e)` grouping in the preamble.
