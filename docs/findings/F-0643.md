## Problem

The wire contract for the `masked` field is split across two owner pages in two different subtrees. The `## masked field` section in `docs/spec_topics/hard-ceilings/ceilings-3-and-4.md` (anchor `#masked-field`) owns the closed identifier set (`"ceiling#1"`…`"ceiling#4"`) with its canonical-absence rule and the wire-location split (`details.masked` vs `details.event.masked`); PIC-1 in `docs/spec_topics/pi-integration-contract/runtime-event-channel.md` (anchor `#pic-1`) owns every other element — the per-site reachable mask domain, the V1 reachable predicate, the pure-read MUST, the verbatim-copy obligation, and the dedup-key non-inclusion rule. An implementer assembling the on-the-wire shape of `masked` for a `Diagnostic` or `RuntimeEvent` must read both pages to obtain the full contract.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** single-commit-cause
**Introducing commit:** `7598f50` — *pi-loom spec: resolve "CIO-6 hard-ceiling co-fire: predicate, test vector, and normative ownership"* (2026-05-07)
**History:** The `## masked field` section was created in `1c7f550` (2026-05-07) holding the full wire contract — closed identifier set, per-site reachable mask domain bullets, pure-read MUST, and wire-location split — as a single block on `hard-ceilings.md`. Hours later, `7598f50` introduced the current split: it moved the per-site reachable mask domain, V1 reachable predicate, pure-read MUST, verbatim-copy obligation, and dedup-key non-inclusion rule into a new PIC-1 block in `pi-integration-contract.md`, deliberately leaving the closed identifier set and the wire-location split behind on `hard-ceilings.md` and reciprocally cross-linking the two pages. Subsequent commits `81bf52a` (collapse into `spec_topics/`) and `d75f2a8` (added the PIC-1 forward-link at the top of `## Interaction between ceilings`) refined navigation but did not change the split. The defect is the choice of split made by `7598f50`; the later commits inherit and patch around it.

## Solution approach

Move the closed identifier set (with the canonical-absence rule) and the wire-location split prose from the `ceilings-3-and-4.md` `#masked-field` section into PIC-1, joining the rest of the wire contract PIC-1 already owns. Collapse the `ceilings-3-and-4.md` `## masked field` section to a stub that forward-links to PIC-1 as the owner. Re-point the closed-set / wire-location citations in `diagnostics/diagnostic-shape.md` and the normative-ownership summaries on `hard-ceilings.md` and the `ceilings-3-and-4.md` CIO-6 bullet at PIC-1.
