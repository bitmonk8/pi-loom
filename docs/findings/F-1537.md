## Problem

`binder-inference.md` (anchor `binder-inference-call`) is the canonical owner of the per-binder-attempt `complete(model, context, options)` payload layout and presents `options` population as a closed bulleted enumeration that omits `StreamOptions.maxRetries` and `StreamOptions.maxRetryDelayMs`. The section's closing delegation sentence reads as loom populating "this call's `StreamOptions.maxRetries` / `StreamOptions.maxRetryDelayMs`", yet no enumeration bullet sets a value or states the fields are left at the library default. A reader confined to `binder-inference.md` cannot tell whether loom passes specific values: the enumeration implies loom never touches the fields while the closing prose implies loom sets them. The authoritative resolution — loom redefines neither field — exists only on `determinism-cancellation-failure.md#per-invocation-retry-budget`.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** introduced-by-fix
**Introducing commits:** `d0bcdb2` (2026-06-05) "pi-loom spec: resolve "binder per-invocation retry inter-attempt timing""
**History:** Before `d0bcdb2`, the binder-inference page closed its `complete()` paragraph with "inter-attempt timing is out of scope here" and made no reference to `maxRetries`/`maxRetryDelayMs`; `determinism-cancellation-failure.md` similarly made no claim about per-attempt backoff. The HC3-d wording "3 binder LLM calls per slash invocation (1 initial attempt + 1 transport-class retry + 1 malformed-envelope-class retry)" was introduced at the same time the per-class retry budget was first authored and remained unchanged through `1f4f536` (2026-06-07), which only re-anchored the HC3-a..HC3-e labels. `d0bcdb2` then resolved a different finding ("binder per-invocation retry inter-attempt timing") by delegating per-attempt backoff and `Retry-After` to `StreamOptions.maxRetries`/`StreamOptions.maxRetryDelayMs` in both pages — adding the dependency on those fields without adding them to the enumerated `options` bullet list and without revisiting whether HC3-d's "binder LLM calls" now counts loom invocations or HTTP attempts. The defect is the unfollowed-through edit: a fix that introduced a new SDK-layer behaviour into the contract without pinning loom's values for it or reconciling it with the pre-existing ceiling label.

## Solution approach

Add an entry to the binder `complete()` options enumeration in `binder-inference.md` stating that `StreamOptions.maxRetries` and `StreamOptions.maxRetryDelayMs` are left unset so the `@earendil-works/pi-ai` defaults apply, and that loom imposes no per-attempt timeout on the binder call beyond `loomAbort.signal` cancellation. Add a forward-link from that entry to `determinism-cancellation-failure.md#per-invocation-retry-budget` so the loom-level vs. provider-level retry layering is explicit at the enumeration site. Rewrite the closing delegation sentence so it no longer reads as loom setting "this call's" fields, keeping it consistent with the determinism page's "loom redefines neither field."

## Solution constraints

- Do not introduce a new loom-owned timeout/backoff knob over the external `StreamOptions` surface; that bound is owned by `@earendil-works/pi-ai`.
