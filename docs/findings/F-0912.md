## Finding

The `docs/plan.md` §Blocked obligations bullet for `V9l` states that the clause-(a) gating is "tracked in plan terms by `V18c`'s **Deps** edge." The gating edge does not live in `V18c`'s Deps. `V9l`'s Deps are `V9l-T, V9h, V9b, V18c` — the blocking edge is the `V18c` entry **on V9l**, naming V18c as the clause-(a) resolution owner. `V18c`'s own Deps are `V18c-T, V18a, V18b`, which contain nothing related to clause (a) or to V9l.

A reader who follows the §Blocked obligations sentence and cross-checks `V18c`'s Deps to confirm the gating finds no such edge there. The two authoritative locations both place the edge correctly on V9l: the `V9l` leaf's Deps line ("`V18c` is the clause-(a) resolution owner: this leaf is **blocked** until …"), and `conventions.md` §Deps pickability predicate ("a leaf flagged blocked (e.g. `V9l`) stays gated through its declared `Deps.` edge on the resolution-owner leaf"). Only the §Blocked obligations narrative misattributes it. The plan's §V9 Interleave note also describes the dependency correctly ("`V9l` … also depends on `V18c`").

Execution is unaffected — V9l's actual Deps edge is present and correct, so the leaf remains gated as intended. The defect is a cross-reference inconsistency in the master-index narrative that misdirects anyone auditing the gating mechanism.

## Solution Space

**Shape:** single

### Recommendation

In `docs/plan.md` §Blocked obligations, in the `V9l` bullet, replace the clause "tracked in plan terms by `V18c`'s **Deps** edge" with "tracked in plan terms by `V9l`'s **Deps** edge on `V18c`". No mechanism change — the gating edge already exists in `V9l`'s Deps; only the narrative attribution is corrected so it agrees with the `V9l` leaf and the `conventions.md` pickability predicate. Leave `V9l`'s and `V18c`'s Deps lines and the §V9 Interleave note untouched (all three are already correct).

## Consequence

**Severity:** advisory

An auditor verifying the V9l block by cross-checking `V18c`'s Deps finds no clause-(a) edge and may conclude the block is untracked, or "fix" it by adding a spurious edge to V18c's Deps. The gating itself operates correctly via V9l's existing Deps edge, so no implementer produces a wrong leaf.
