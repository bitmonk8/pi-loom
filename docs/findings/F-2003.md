## Finding

INV-3 exists solely to document a deferred feature (per-call timeout) and the open-struct convention that reserves space for it. The normative claim — "the internal options record is an open struct; consumers tolerate unknown fields" — is an implementation-internal constraint invisible to loom authors. The "Per-call-timeout seam" heading and blockquote format present it as a named, citable requirement (hence the `<a id="inv-3">` anchor), but it constrains only future extension authors, not loom language implementers in loom 1.0.
