## Problem

The `**Host prerequisites for the degraded-state branch.**` paragraph (anchor `#degraded-state-host-prerequisites`) presents sub-clauses (a)–(d) as a homogeneous four-item "presuppositions" list, but clause (a) is not a presupposition: it is the load-bearing record of an open, blocking contradiction at the loom 1.0 Pi-SDK pin between loom's session-only degraded-state premise and Pi's documented teardown-and-rebind extension lifecycle, carrying the two dispositive verdicts (`governed-by-rebind` / `provably-differs`), the evidentiary standard, and the SM-4 / SM-5 / SM-6 / SM-3b implementation gate. The canonical `## Open contradictions (blocking, not deferrals)` register in `model-changes-and-non-goals.md` — the discoverable home for blocking contradictions — carries only a one-bullet pointer back into clause (a), so the topology is inverted: the canonical register holds a stub while the substance lives off-canonical as a sub-clause of an unrelated presupposition list. A reader arriving at the register cannot read the contradiction without a second hop, and a reader of the host-prerequisites paragraph meets a ~580-word essay that breaks the symmetric four-presupposition framing the paragraph announces.

## Solution approach

Move the open-contradiction substance — the contradiction statement against Pi's documented teardown-and-rebind lifecycle, the two dispositive verdicts (`governed-by-rebind` / `provably-differs`), the evidentiary standard, and the SM-4 / SM-5 / SM-6 / SM-3b gate — from `host-prerequisites.md` clause (a) into the *Extension-instance survival across session-only `session_shutdown`* bullet of `model-changes-and-non-goals.md` §`## Open contradictions (blocking, not deferrals)`. Rewrite clause (a) at `#degraded-state-host-prerequisites` to a one-sentence normative gate that names the contradiction and forward-links to the relocated bullet, and adjust the umbrella paragraph's "Clause (a) is the exception" sentence to forward-link the relocated bullet rather than the in-place text. Narrow the "Open at the loom 1.0 pin" prose of version-bump item (a) at `#bump-checklist-instance-survival` to a cross-reference into the relocated bullet, leaving the checklist-procedural content in place.
