## Finding

`match`'s grammar is currently bisected. The left-hand side тАФ the normative six-form Pattern table (wildcard / identifier / literal / constructor / object-schema / array, plus the disambiguation note and the deferred-features paragraph) тАФ lives in `errors-and-results/error-model.md` interleaved with the `Result` / `?` / `MatchError` material. The right-hand side тАФ the `MatchArm` / `ArmBody` productions and the statement-in-arm-body diagnostic тАФ lives in `grammar.md` under `## match arm body`. The `MatchArm ::= Pattern "=>" ArmBody` production cites a non-terminal `Pattern` that the same file does not define, and the grammar appendix's only mention of pattern grammar appears as an aside under `LetStmt` ("full destructuring patterns appear only inside `match` arms (see Errors and Results тАФ Pattern grammar)"), not at the `MatchArm` production where a reader would look. Going the other direction, the Pattern grammar table in `error-model.md` carries no pointer to the grammar appendix that owns the rest of the surface syntax.

A reader of `grammar.md` alone тАФ for example an implementer building the type-grammar / arm-body parser in `V2a` тАФ cannot resolve `Pattern` without already knowing that the table lives in `error-model.md`. A reader of `error-model.md` alone has the table but no signal that the grammar appendix is the home of related surface productions. The grammar appendix's own stated scope ("the few surface-syntax forms that no single topic page owns end-to-end") leaves the split defensible тАФ `match`'s pattern grammar arguably belongs with `match`'s semantics тАФ but the current pages do not honour either resolution: the table is in `error-model.md` *and* the appendix declines to cross-reference it from the `MatchArm` production.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** multi-commit-interaction
**Introducing commits:** `53f5831` (2026-05-03, "spec: rewrite query primitive, system prompts, and schema syntax") landed the Pattern grammar table in the then-monolithic `spec.md`; `f841b12` (2026-05-04, "spec: resolve Cluster 2 (literal sublanguage + grammar appendix)") created the grammar appendix and decided to leave the pattern table in the errors-and-results material while introducing `MatchArm ::= Pattern "=>" ArmBody` in the new appendix without an inline back-pointer at the `MatchArm` production.
**History:** The pattern table predates the grammar appendix by one day. The placement defect did not exist when `53f5831` landed тАФ there was no second file to cross-reference. It crystallised in `f841b12`, which authored the appendix with the express scope of owning "surface-syntax forms no single topic page owns end-to-end," authored `MatchArm`/`ArmBody` there, attached a `Pattern` cross-reference only to the unrelated `LetStmt` production, and chose not to relocate or back-reference the pattern table from the appendix. The split-into-topics commit `fecb504` (2026-05-04) is mechanical (it moved `error-model.md` out of `spec.md` into `errors-and-results/`) and did not contribute to the defect. The 100 KB-cap rename pass `f5e89f4` is a verbatim search-and-replace and is also non-contributory.

## Solution Space

**Shape:** multiple

### Option A тАФ Move the pattern grammar table into the appendix

**Approach.** Lift the Pattern grammar table, the disambiguation paragraph, and the deferred-features paragraph out of `error-model.md` into `grammar.md`. Either expand `## match arm body` to cover both halves (renaming it to `## match expression` with sub-sections `Pattern` and `ArmBody`), or add a sibling `## match pattern` section directly above `## match arm body`. Replace the lifted prose in `error-model.md` with a one-line cross-reference ("See [Grammar Appendix тАФ `match` pattern](../grammar.md#match-pattern).") immediately above the `## match arm body` paragraph.

**Spec edits.**
- `grammar.md`: insert the lifted table and prose as a new `## match pattern` section above `## match arm body`; update the `MatchArm ::= Pattern "=>" ArmBody` block's surrounding prose to point at the new section instead of, or in addition to, the `LetStmt` aside.
- `error-model.md`: replace the lifted section with a cross-reference; verify the surrounding `Arm syntax.` paragraph and the `Exhaustiveness.` paragraph still flow.
- `queryerror-variants.md`: update the "Pattern grammar (loom 1.0) table at the top of this file" wording to point at the appendix.
- `diagnostics/code-registry-parse.md`: re-point the `match-guard-not-supported` and `rest-pattern-not-supported` "Spec home" cells at the appendix.

**Pros.** Both halves of `match`'s surface syntax live in one place; the grammar appendix becomes complete in fact, not just in name; implementers of `V2a` parse the whole grammar from one file.

**Cons.** `error-model.md`'s opening flow is disrupted тАФ the table sits early in that page and orients the reader before they hit `Arm syntax.` / `?` / `Terminal outcomes`; replacing it with a bare cross-reference makes the page open with semantics that reference an unintroduced concept.

**Risks.** Touches four files; two of them (`queryerror-variants.md`, the diagnostics registry) are downstream citers whose anchor wording must move in lock-step.

### Option B тАФ Leave the table in place; add reciprocal cross-references

**Approach.** Keep the table in `error-model.md` (consistent with the grammar appendix's stated scope of "forms no single topic page owns end-to-end" тАФ `match` semantics own this one). Add an inline cross-reference from the `MatchArm` production in `grammar.md` directly to the pattern table, and a reciprocal cross-reference from the pattern table in `error-model.md` to `## match arm body` in the appendix.

**Spec edits.**
- `grammar.md`: directly under the `MatchArm ::= Pattern "=>" ArmBody` code block, add a sentence: "`Pattern` is the left-hand side of a `match` arm; its six forms are defined at [Errors and Results тАФ Pattern grammar](./errors-and-results/error-model.md#pattern-grammar-loom-10)." (Add the matching anchor in `error-model.md` if one is not already present.)
- `error-model.md`: immediately under the Pattern grammar table, add a sentence pointing at `## match arm body` in the appendix as the home of the arm-body grammar.

**Pros.** Single-file diff per page; respects the appendix's own scope statement; preserves `error-model.md`'s opening flow; cheapest fix.

**Cons.** The grammar appendix remains a half-house for `match` тАФ a reader still has to context-switch between two files to see both halves of the production; the underlying critique ("grammar appendix incomplete") is mitigated, not closed.

**Risks.** None material. Adds anchors only.

### Recommendation

Option B. The grammar appendix's stated charter explicitly defers forms a topic page already owns end-to-end, and `errors-and-results/error-model.md` owns `match` semantics тАФ pattern grammar is part of those semantics, not a stray surface form. The defect is navigability, not ownership; reciprocal cross-references at the `MatchArm` production and under the pattern table close the navigability gap at one-tenth the surface area of a move, leave the diagnostics-registry "Spec home" cells unchanged, and avoid disrupting `error-model.md`'s opening orientation. The implementer in `V2a` who reaches `MatchArm` will see the pointer in the same paragraph as the production, not buried in a `LetStmt` aside; the implementer in `V4a` reading the pattern table will see the appendix pointer adjacent to the table.

Edge cases the implementer must watch:
- The new sentence under the `MatchArm` block must reference the pattern table by anchor, not by section title тАФ add an explicit `<a id="pattern-grammar-loom-10"></a>` (or equivalent kebab-cased anchor) in `error-model.md` immediately above the `**Pattern grammar (loom 1.0).**` heading so the link is stable across future re-titlings.
- The existing `LetStmt` aside in `grammar.md` already cross-references "Errors and Results тАФ Pattern grammar" at the topic level. Keep it; do not collapse it into the new `MatchArm`-adjacent pointer тАФ the two pointers serve different productions and a reader of either production benefits from the local cross-reference.
- Do not edit `queryerror-variants.md`'s "Pattern grammar (loom 1.0) table at the top of this file" wording тАФ under Option B the table stays at the top of `error-model.md`, so that pointer remains accurate.
