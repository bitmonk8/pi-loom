## Problem

`H2a` installs four mutually-independent cross-cutting gates in one horizontal leaf: the `no-broad-catch` ESLint rule, the `no-restricted-syntax` sequential-by-default Promise-combinator ban, the `no-restricted-syntax` blocking-runtime `Sync`-suffix ban, and the `src/**` module-level mutable-binding architectural test. Each arm has its own rule body, fixture, and violation pattern and shares no surface beyond the same `npm test` run, yet `Ships when.` ANDs all four assertions into a single completion condition. Because the leaf reports as one pickable unit, an implementer can land three of four arms and review/CI cannot attribute the missing arm — partial correctness is invisible at the leaf's granularity. The bundled shape is also inconsistent with the per-arm gate-leaf pattern the `H5` series (`H5a`/`H5c`/`H5d`/`H5e`/`H5f`) already establishes for the same class of work.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** present-since-inception
**Introducing commits:** `c6a664e` (2026-06-10, "build/update plan for spec.md + review" — created `H2a` already bundling three enforcement mechanisms); `c33d309` (2026-06-17, "resolve 'Blocking-runtime ban concedes enforcement to manual review'" — added the fourth mechanism, the `*Sync` blocking-runtime `no-restricted-syntax` check)
**History:** `H2a` bundled multiple cross-cutting enforcement mechanisms from its creation in `c6a664e` — the `no-broad-catch` ESLint rule, the `Promise.all` sequential-by-default `no-restricted-syntax` rule, and a `src/**` architectural test (then phrased as a global/static/singleton check). The fourth mechanism, the `*Sync` blocking-runtime `no-restricted-syntax` rule, was added later in `c33d309` while resolving an unrelated enforcement-strength finding, raising the bundled count to four. The multi-mechanism bundling is therefore an inception-era structural property of the leaf, not an artefact a single later edit introduced; `c33d309` only grew the count.

## Solution approach

Split `H2a` into separate horizontal gate leaves, one per independent enforcement mechanism, mirroring the `H5` per-arm precedent; keep the Promise-combinator ban and the blocking-`Sync` ban together in one leaf since they share the `no-restricted-syntax` selector and the comment-keyed exemption machinery. Add the new leaf file(s) under `docs/plan_topics/` and link them under §Horizontal phases in `docs/plan.md`. Retarget every `H2a` reference to the specific arm leaf: `H3a`'s `Deps.`, the `conventions.md` mechanical-gates list and residue-inspection items 1/3/6, and `H1a`'s `no-broad-catch` plugin-population prose.

## Solution constraints

- Allocate new leaf IDs per the plan ID scheme — next free letter in the target horizontal phase, never reusing a retired ID.
- When splitting the leaf, keep `docs/plan_topics/conventions.md` and `docs/plan_topics/coverage-matrix.md` consistent per the plan's leaf create/split/merge rule.
