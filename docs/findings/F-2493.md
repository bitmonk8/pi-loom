## Problem

`CancelledError`'s schema sits under the `### Query-time variants` subsection in `queryerror-variants.md`, but it fires when an `AbortSignal` aborts a query, tool call, or invoke ŌĆö a cross-cutting trigger. That subsection sits alongside `### Code-side tool-call variant` and `### Invoke variants`, so the "Query-time" grouping misclassifies the variant's scope and misleads readers about where it applies.

**Verdict:** multi-commit-interaction

**History:** Items (a), (b), (d), (e), and `version-bump-intro.md`'s "three requires-re-validating sentences" enumeration all entered in f5e89f4 (2026-06-04), the commit that split the monolithic spec into topic files ŌĆö the `# Queryerror` H1, the `### Query-time variants` grouping, the inline-only user-defined-error-types non-goal, and the `last_tool_name | null` comment were created in that form and never corrected. ## Solution approach

Move `CancelledError`'s schema out of `### Query-time variants` in `queryerror-variants.md` into a cross-cutting grouping (or relabel it) so the variant's query/tool/invoke scope is unambiguous.
