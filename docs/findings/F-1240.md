## Problem

`H4a`'s **Adds** scopes the leaf's own input-side deliverable to a **minimal response-emission capability** "sufficient only to drive self-check items (i) and (ii)" — streamed-token order and single-turn append. Its **Tests** self-check, however, asserts all four fidelity-contract axes, including (iii) the `pi.on` cancel-forward subscription and (iv) cancellation propagation (CNCL-4); both require firing a host cancel / `ctx.signal` abort into the session double, an affordance enumerated nowhere in `H4a`'s deliverables and deferred to `H4b`'s category (e). Since `H4a`'s only **Dep** is `H3a` and `H4b` sits downstream, the leaf cannot borrow the affordance, so its **Ships when** gate asserts a self-check it has no enumerated means to drive.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** single-commit introduction
**Introducing commits:** `32ea081` (2026-06-20, "fix F-0477 — define H4a minimal emission capability + H4a/H4b boundary")
**History:** The four-axis self-check (enumerating (i)–(iv), including the `pi.on` cancel-forward subscription and cancellation propagation) already existed in `H4a`'s **Tests** at the parent of `32ea081`. That commit added the "minimal response-emission capability … sufficient only to drive self-check items (i) and (ii)" framing and the "self-checkable from its own deliverables" claim, narrowing `H4a`'s owned input-side capability to (i)/(ii) while leaving the self-check asserting (iii)/(iv) and deferring the full scripted-injection surface (including cancellation injection) to `H4b`. The mismatch between the scoped deliverable and the four-item self-check entered at that commit.

## Solution approach

In `H4a`'s **Adds**, name a minimal host-cancel / `ctx.signal`-abort triggering affordance as `H4a`'s own deliverable alongside the minimal response-emission capability, and widen the self-check coverage statement from items (i)/(ii) to cover (i)–(iv). Reconcile `H4a`'s **Tests** and **Ships when** so the self-check drives axes (iii) and (iv) against this affordance.

## Solution constraints

- Out of scope: the scripted-injection category (e) injection-point matrix is `H4b`-owned — the `H4a` affordance must stay a single fire-a-host-cancel trigger, not the pre-call / in-flight / during-retry injection surface.
- The spec is read-only for this fix.
