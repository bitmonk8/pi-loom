## Problem

`PIC-2. Prompt-mode sequential execution` (`<a id="pic-2">`) is authored as the final paragraph of `subagent.md`, appended after `PIC-22` and the `concurrent-invocation-isolation` block. The rule is wholly about prompt-mode bodies and is derived from premises that live elsewhere — Pi's per-session slash-handler serialisation at `tool-registration-lifetime.md#snapshot-restore-pi-behavioural-preconditions`, the load-time rejection of prompt-mode `tools:` callees in `frontmatter-fields-a.md`, and the `invoke(...)` suspension and closed `subagent → prompt` fan-out in `invocation.md`. A reader navigating the prompt-mode surface (`conversation-drive.md`, `tool-registration-lifetime.md`, `invocation.md`) finds neither PIC-2 nor a forward pointer to it; the rule is reachable only by anchor from distant aggregator and plan-leaf citations.

**History:** The prompt-mode sequential-execution rule originated as inline prose in `spec.md`'s session-model paragraph (added by `40ffdc5`, 2026-05-27, *T19a — Replace session-model paragraph with eight SM-N sub-units*) and was then decomposed under `SM-7c` on `spec.md` by `9271e6a` (2026-05-30). `7d7bfe2` relocated the normative content from `spec.md` into a new `<a id="pic-2">` PIC paragraph and chose `subagent.md` as the destination — appended after `PIC-22` and the `concurrent-invocation-isolation` block whose closing sentence references it by contrast. The structural placement choice that this finding flags entered the corpus at `7d7bfe2`; `f5e89f4` (2026-06-04) later split `pi-integration-contract.md` into per-topic child pages and carried the paragraph through unchanged in its `subagent.md` slot.

## Solution approach

Move the `<a id="pic-2">` PIC-2 paragraph from `subagent.md` to `tool-registration-lifetime.md`, where the `PIC-17`/`PIC-19` snapshot/restore contract it derives from already lives. Update the moved paragraph's internal references and `subagent.md`'s `concurrent-invocation-isolation` back-reference to the cross-file / same-page forms the new path requires, and repoint the inbound links — `session-model-and-appendix.md`'s `SM-7c`, `governance/req-id-prefix-table-active-b.md`'s GOV-30 enumeration, and the `V9c` / `V9c-T` / `V15d` / `V15d-T` plan-leaf **Spec** lines — to the new path. Add a forward pointer near `PIC-18` on `conversation-drive.md` so a reader on the prompt-mode driver surface still reaches the rule.
