## Problem

`V7a` (`docs/plan_topics/V7a-diagnostics-primitive.md`) carries two independent surfaces in one leaf. The first is a host-independent data-layer primitive: the `Diagnostic` shape, the serialised content-line format, and the multi-error `Diagnostic[]` assembly and `(file, line, col)` ordering. The second is the Pi delivery channel: the `loom-system-note` `sendMessage` envelope, its renderer registration, the `PIC-21` renderer-throw trap, and the `sendSystemNote → ctx.ui.notify → console.error` fallback chain. These are distinct failure-mode surfaces with distinct test obligations (pure data assertions versus host-boundary failure injection); the primitive can be built and verified with no Pi host present while the channel cannot, yet the current packaging forces both surfaces — and their paired `V7a-T` tests — into one implementer task and diff.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** present-since-inception
**Introducing commits:** `c6a664e` ("pi-loom plan: build/update plan for spec.md + review")
**History:** `git log --follow` over `docs/plan_topics/V7a-diagnostics-primitive.md` shows the leaf was created in `c6a664e` already titled "Diagnostics primitive and `loom-system-note` channel", with its **Adds** bullet bundling the `Diagnostic` shape, content-line format, batching, the `sendMessage` envelope, renderer registration, and fallback chain in one paragraph. The three later commits (`af2ec83` PIC-21 renderer-exception test, `249cec5` IMPL coverage-matrix mapping, `23e327b` emission-fallback-chain test) only added or refined tests; none separated the two surfaces. The bundling is an inception-time packaging decision, not a regression introduced by a later edit.

## Solution approach

Split `V7a` along the data-primitive / delivery-channel seam: keep `V7a` for the `Diagnostic` primitive (shape, content-line format, multi-error assembly/ordering) and extract the `loom-system-note` channel (envelope, renderer registration, `PIC-21` trap, fallback chain) into a new V7-slice leaf with its `-T` pair. Apply the matching split to `V7a-T`, add the new channel leaf to the V7 slice listing in `docs/plan.md`, and update `V7a`'s title and **Spec** field to drop the channel-only references. Re-target the affected `docs/plan_topics/coverage-matrix.md` rows and the `docs/plan_topics/conventions.md` broad-catch `PIC-21` attribution to the new leaf, and re-point each dependent leaf's **Deps** (including the `H5b` coverage-producing range) at the half it consumes. The multi-error path spans the seam — keep the `(file, line, col)` ordering assertion with the primitive and the single-envelope batch-delivery assertion with the channel.

## Solution constraints

- Allocate the new channel leaf's ID per the `## Plan rules` `V<N><letter>` scheme — next free letter in the V7 phase, no reuse of a retired ID — and keep `docs/plan_topics/conventions.md` and `docs/plan_topics/coverage-matrix.md` consistent with the split.
