## Finding

Items (f)–(ak) are described as "SHOULD-level-audit-but-MUST-record" in Adds (auditing SHOULD, recording MUST even for `not-performed`) but as "SHOULD-level" in Ships when, which could be read as both audit and recording being SHOULD-level — the opposite of Adds. A developer reading only Ships when (the completion gate) misses the MUST-record obligation.

**Suggested fix:** Use the full compound term consistently, or add a parenthetical in Ships when restoring the MUST-record qualifier.
