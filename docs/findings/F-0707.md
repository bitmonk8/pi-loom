## Problem

The Block-expressions statement-form-block paragraph in `grammar.md` (`id="block-expressions"`) ends with a tail-`Expr`-discard rule whose `?` early-return carve-out names only the `@…?` form. Read literally, this restricts early-return to the query-with-postfix-`?` shape and leaves the spec silent on the other operands `?` admits. ERR-18 (`error-model.md#err-18`) enumerates the admissible `?` operands as a `@`-query, an `invoke(...)`, or an explicit `Ok(...)` / `Err(...)`, and `return.md` pins `?` as the universal `return Err(e)` desugaring. A reader treating the grammar paragraph as authoritative concludes that `invoke(child)?` or `f()?` in the tail position of a statement-form `if` / `for` / `while` body does not propagate `Err`, contradicting the universal desugaring.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** present-since-inception (of the *Block expressions* statement-form-block paragraph)
**Introducing commit:** `816852b` (2026-06-05) — *pi-loom spec: resolve "Block expressions — no grammar production for statement-form if/for/while bodies"*
**History:** Before `816852b`, `grammar.md` distinguished only *expression-position blocks* and *body blocks*; statement-form `if` / `while` arm bodies were lumped under "expression-position blocks" and no statement-form-block paragraph existed. `816852b` introduced the `StmtBlock`, `IfStmt`, `ElseClause`, `WhileStmt`, and `ForStmt` productions and the new paragraph carrying the tail-`Expr`-discarded rule together with the *"except that a tail `Expr` of the `@…?` form still triggers `?` early-return on failure"* clause. The clause has been verbatim since that commit; subsequent edits to `grammar.md` (`057159c`, `5c8041a`, `c3b7c87`, `e1f5c2a`) did not touch this paragraph. `git log --all -G '@…\?' -- docs/spec_topics/grammar.md` and `git log --all -S 'still triggers \`?\` early-return'` both surface `816852b` as the sole introducing commit. The defect is inception-state in this paragraph: it entered the corpus already restricting the carve-out to the `@…?` shape, rather than being a regression from a broader earlier wording.

## Solution approach

Rewrite the tail-`Expr` carve-out clause in the `id="block-expressions"` statement-form-block paragraph so the early-return trigger is the postfix `?` operator over any operand admitted by ERR-18, not the single `@…?` shape. Keep the forward-link to `return.md` for the `return Err(e)` desugaring and add a forward-link to `error-model.md#err-18` for the operand set.
