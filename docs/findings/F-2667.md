## Problem

PIC-52's grant-model clause states loom-side filesystem, network, and Pi-API access is "bounded only by Pi's extension-host grants" as established fact. But the orientation Trust-boundary bullet (`overview-and-orientation.md#non-functional-requirements`) frames Pi's absence of per-extension privilege scoping as an unaudited presupposition on the pinned Pi minor, routed to a per-bump editorial-review item. PIC-52, the normative owner, carries neither the presupposition caveat nor a cross-reference to that hedged framing, so a reader of PIC-52 alone over-trusts a grant-model claim the orientation page deliberately hedges.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** present-since-inception
**Introducing commits:** `52219c4` (2026-05-06, "Address all 41 spec-review findings")
**History:** All three defect tokens — `"bounded only by Pi's extension-host grants"`, `"MUST NOT introduce additional access channels … major-version concern"`, and `"silent success on denial is forbidden"` — trace via `git log -S` to a single commit, `52219c4` (2026-05-06), which authored the PIC-52 paragraph in `spec_topics/pi-integration-contract.md` as the resolution (Option A) of a prior spec-review on the Trust-boundary bullet. The text was carried verbatim through `e4017a8` (2026-06-25, relocation to `pi-integration-contract/trust-boundary.md`) and `1acdc55` (2026-06-25, coinage of the explicit `PIC-52` anchor); neither commit altered the wording or its under-specification. The defects therefore coincide with the introduction of the rule itself.

## Solution approach

Rewrite PIC-52's grant-model clause in `trust-boundary.md` to frame access as bounded by whatever the pinned Pi minor's host process grants — something the runtime presupposes rather than audits — instead of asserting an audited grant model. Add forward-links from the reworded clause to the orientation Trust-boundary bullet (`overview-and-orientation.md#non-functional-requirements`) and to the per-extension privilege-scoping facet item (`version-bump-step2.md#bump-checklist-privilege-scoping-facet`).
