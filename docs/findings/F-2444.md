## Problem

The "Signal source" and "Forwarding into `loomAbort`" paragraphs in `cancellation.md` specify runtime↔SDK signal-chain wiring — per-invocation `AbortController`, entry-point forwarding shapes, `ctx.signal`-undefined handling, prompt-mode bidirectional `ctx.abort()`, and the subagent abort listener — that duplicates the authoritative `host-interfaces-core.md#cancellation-source` section. Holding two copies of the wiring mechanics invites drift between the pages as either copy is edited.

## Solution approach

Move the SDK signal-chain wiring mechanics out of `cancellation.md`'s "Signal source" and "Forwarding into `loomAbort`" paragraphs into `host-interfaces-core.md#cancellation-source`, folding in any detail that section does not already carry (e.g. the prompt-mode `ctx.abort()` linkage and the spawn-then-immediate-cancel ordering). Rewrite the two `cancellation.md` paragraphs as a loom-observable summary with a forward-link to `host-interfaces-core.md#cancellation-source`.
