## Problem

The `V9q` and `V9q-T` Tests assert that the `LoomRegistry` stays live and
dispatchable through arm (a) of `readDrainState` and that no drain-state tag is
written. `readDrainState`, the drain-state tag, and the dispatchable
`LoomRegistry` are production symbols owned by `V9m` (which transitively carries
`V9b`), but `V9q` declares `Deps. V9q-T, V8e, V7b, V7d` and `V9q-T` declares
`Deps. V8e, V7b, V7d` — neither reaches `V9m` or `V9b`. Under the pickability
predicate, `V9q-T` becomes pickable before `V9m`/`V9b` exist, so its suite
references not-yet-declared symbols and is a compile-time red, which
`conventions.md` §"Fail red for the intended reason" disqualifies from satisfying
the tests-task gate. Sibling drain-state leaves `V9h`/`V9h-T` and `V9p`/`V9p-T`
already carry the `V9m` edge for the same contract.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** present-since-inception
**Introducing commits:** `1901253` (2026-06-27, "fix F-1892 — add V9q paired leaf closing PIC-55 + watcher-terminated test; coverage-matrix + H5b Deps")
**History:** `git log --follow` on both leaf files shows `1901253` as the creating commit for the `V9q`/`V9q-T` pair. At that commit the Tests already asserted "`LoomRegistry` stays live and dispatchable through arm (a) of `readDrainState` … no `LoomRegistry` drain-state tag is written," while `Deps` were `V9q-T, V8e, V7b, V7d` (V9q) and `V8e, V7b, V7d` (V9q-T) — neither reaching `V9m`/`V9b`. The only later touch, `4a52fe8` (2026-06-28, F-2356), enumerated the PIC-55 adapter→runtime terminal-signal channel and left the `Deps` lines unchanged. The missing edge has existed since the leaves were authored.

## Solution approach

Add a `V9m` edge to the **Deps.** field of both `V9q` and `V9q-T`. `V9m`
transitively carries `V9b` through its own `Deps`, so the dispatchable
`LoomRegistry`, `readDrainState`, and the drain-state-tag symbols all become
declared before either suite compiles, mirroring the edge `V9h`/`V9h-T` and
`V9p`/`V9p-T` already carry.
