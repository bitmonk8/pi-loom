## Finding

Code-side Pi-tool calls pass a runtime-synthesised `toolCallId` into `execute(toolCallId, …)`. The only constraint given is the `loom-direct:` prefix; the suffix, uniqueness scope (per-call vs per-invocation), and collision behaviour are unspecified. A Pi tool that keys side effects or idempotency on `toolCallId` would behave differently across reasonable suffix schemes. - **What an implementer would invent:** A suffix scheme (counter, UUID, or constant), risking collisions if a constant or per-process counter is chosen across concurrent invocations.
