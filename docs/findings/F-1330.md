## Problem

The complete `ValidationIssue`-to-system-note rendering contract for the `<ajv-summary>` placeholder has no single canonical home. The `ValidationIssue` schema, the ERR-14 ordering rule, and the ERR-17 synthesised shapes all live in `errors-and-results/queryerror-variants.md`, but the actual rendering rule lives in `binder/determinism-cancellation-failure.md` §Failure-mode templates (normative), and `query/query-failure-and-repair.md` defers into that binder anchor for rendering. A reader auditing `ValidationIssue` never sees how an issue becomes user-visible bytes, and the respond-repair surface sources its template from a binder anchor unrelated to respond-repair. The rule is a property of the `ValidationIssue` schema, not of the binder, and multiple surfaces consume it through an indirect citation chain.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** multi-commit-interaction
**Introducing commits:** `9d10d82` (2026-06-01) → `c3ea1d1` (2026-06-04) → `34517a0` (2026-06-05)
**History:** the rendering rule was authored validator-neutral in `9d10d82` inside `docs/spec_topics/binder.md` §Failure-mode templates — the binder file was the rule's natural host at that time because the `<ajv-summary>` placeholder appeared only on the binder surface. ERR-14 (`ValidationIssue` ordering) was subsequently relocated under the `ValidationError` schema in `errors-and-results/queryerror-variants.md` in `c3ea1d1`. The ERR-17 synthesised shapes were moved out of `conversation-drive.md` into queryerror-variants.md in `34517a0`, consolidating the `ValidationIssue`-cluster on the schema page. The shard split (`f5e89f4`) carried the rendering rule forward without revisiting its placement. The split surfaced now is the unfinished tail of those three consolidations: the schema, ordering, and synthesised-shapes all migrated to the protocol page, but the rendering rule — consumed by the new respond-repair surface (`V13h`) added after the original placement — was never moved.

## Solution approach

Move the validator-neutral `<ajv-summary>` rendering rule out of `binder/determinism-cancellation-failure.md` §Failure-mode templates (normative) and co-locate it with the `ValidationIssue` schema and ERR-14 (`#err-14`) in `errors-and-results/queryerror-variants.md` behind a stable anchor. Replace the binder's inline rendering clause with a forward-link to the new anchor, and update `query/query-failure-and-repair.md`'s `<ajv-summary>` bullet to cite the new anchor directly instead of routing through the binder's `#failure-mode-templates-normative` anchor.

## Solution constraints

- Preserve the rendered byte form exactly — the two-character separator `; ` (`;` + `U+0020`) and the depth-walk single-issue form — because `V11f`/`V13h` assert byte-equality.
- Out of scope: the binder-only depth-walk fast-fail clause stays in `binder/determinism-cancellation-failure.md` as the rule it overrides for that sub-case; do not relocate it.
