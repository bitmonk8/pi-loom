## Finding

`V4f` is the coverage-matrix closing leaf for `ERR-13` (no-rollback / completed-callee finality): `V4f-T` authors all six §No-rollback authoring-site cases as red-first tests through the `H4a` session double, and `V4f` `Ships when` gates them under `npm test`. `V4f` then explicitly *delegates* the live-surface re-assertion of the completed-callee-finality vector to three production surfaces: its Tests bullet states "the completed-callee-finality vector's live-surface re-assertion is carried by the `V14a`, `V13c`, and `V15a` production surfaces as delegated live-carrier witnesses." Each of `V15a`, `V13c`, and `V14a` accordingly carries an `ERR-13` delegated-live-carrier-witness bullet in its **Tests** section.

That delegation is never closed by a test gate. The `ERR-13` witness bullet is absent from `V15a-T`, `V13c-T`, and `V14a-T` (each of those tests-tasks authors its other bullets — `INV-1/2/3`, static-resolution, final-value, cross-mode matrix, the `cka-47` checkpoint, etc. — but no `ERR-13`), and it is absent from all three implementation leaves' **Ships when** clauses. Under the mandatory per-phase TDD ritual (`conventions.md` §"MVP and vertical features — tests-task → implementation-task pairing"), the `-T` leaf must author a red-first failing test for every REQ-ID the feature introduces, and the impl leaf turns it green; a Tests bullet that lives only on the implementation leaf, with no paired `-T` failing test and no `Ships when` criterion, is verified by nothing.

The plan already contains the correct, established pattern for exactly this construct: `V9i`/`V9i-T` carry the analogous `ERR-8` delegated-live-carrier witness (`V4c`'s deferral). There the witness bullet appears in **both** `V9i` and `V9i-T`, `V9i` lists `V4c` in its `-T` and impl Deps, and `V9i` `Ships when` states how the live surface is witnessed — explicitly flagging `ERR-8` as a **real-host-only** behaviour confirmed at the manual smoke gate rather than under `npm test`. The `ERR-13` carriers were introduced into the impl leaves at the same time as `ERR-8` but were never brought into that paired shape, so they sit in the original, incomplete state.

## Solution Space

**Shape:** single

### Recommendation

Restore the tests-task ↔ implementation-task pairing for the three `ERR-13` delegated-live-carrier witnesses, mirroring the established `V9i`/`V9i-T` (`ERR-8`) treatment, with one content difference noted below.

In `V15a-T`, `V13c-T`, and `V14a-T`, add an `ERR-13` delegated-live-carrier-witness **Tests** bullet matching the bullet already present on the paired implementation leaf:

- `V15a-T`: `` - `ERR-13` (delegated live-carrier witness for `V4f`'s completed-callee-finality deferral): an `invoke` child driven to completion on the live `V15a` surface, then a downstream `?`/panic/cancel fired, leaves the completed callee's side effect in place with no compensating turn injected. ``
- `V13c-T`: same bullet with "a query-tool-loop callee driven to completion on the live `V13c` surface" (matching `V13c`'s wording).
- `V14a-T`: same bullet with "a code-side tool call driven to completion on the live `V14a` surface" (matching `V14a`'s wording).

In each of the three `-T` leaves, add `V4f` to the `Deps.` list (mirroring `V9i-T`, which lists the deferral owner `V4c` in its Deps).

In `V15a`, `V13c`, and `V14a` `Ships when`, add a clause asserting the `ERR-13` completed-callee-finality live-carrier holds on that leaf's live surface — a callee driven to completion, then a downstream `?`/panic/cancel fired, leaves the completed callee's side effect in place with no compensating turn injected.

Content difference from the `ERR-8` template — **the `ERR-13` carriers are `npm test`-assertable, not real-host-only.** `V9i`'s `ERR-8` witness is real-host-only because no offline source can feed a real `createAgentSession` a scripted cancellable mid-stream cancellation, so it is witnessed only at the manual smoke gate. The `ERR-13` live-carrier surfaces here (`invoke`, the query tool loop, code-side tool calls) are loom-runtime-internal and are already driven under `npm test` by each leaf's own `Ships when` (and `V4f-T` already exercises completed-callee finality through the `H4a` double). The `ERR-13` witness should therefore be a red-first `npm test` assertion in the `-T` leaf and an `npm test` clause in `Ships when` — do **not** carry over the `ERR-8` real-host-only / manual-smoke-gate caveat. If, on writing the test, a specific site's live-surface re-assertion turns out to require a real host, give only that site the real-host-only treatment (witnessed at `real-host-smoke-gate.md`, per the `V9i` shape); otherwise it lands under `npm test`.

Removing the bullet from the impl leaves is not the right resolution: `V4f` explicitly names `V14a`/`V13c`/`V15a` as the delegated live-carriers, so striking the bullets would orphan `V4f`'s delegation.

## Consequence

**Severity:** correctness

`V4f` delegates the live-surface re-assertion of completed-callee finality to `V15a`/`V13c`/`V14a`, but no `-T` leaf authors the failing test and no `Ships when` gate asserts it, so the delegated obligation silently ships unverified — `V4f`'s claim that the three production surfaces "carry" the witness is unenforced. Two reasonable implementers diverge: one writes the live-surface `ERR-13` test, another reads the impl-only bullet as documentation and writes nothing, with no gate distinguishing the outcomes.
