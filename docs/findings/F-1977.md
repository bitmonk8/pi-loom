## Finding

The trigger fires when the walk "hit its file-count cap (`looms.scanPackagesMaxFiles`) or wall-clock cap (`looms.scanPackagesTimeoutMs`)", and `<cap>` is a single-valued closed enum of the two cap keys. The Message template (`package-discovery walk aborted at <root>: <cap> cap reached`) carries exactly one `<cap>`. No rule says which key renders when both caps are satisfied at the same abort point (e.g. the file that crosses the file-count threshold is also the read that crosses the wall-clock deadline).
