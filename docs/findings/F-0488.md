## Finding

The `**Deps.**` field of `H6a-live-corpus-activation.md` declares its normative dependency list as `H5b, H7a`, then appends a multi-sentence italicised parenthetical block. That block explains why `H5b` and `H7a` appear, why the `H7a` edge is pinned directly rather than threaded through `H5b` (because `H7a` closes no new spec REQ-ID), that `H5b` owns the complete coverage-producing set kept transitively complete under *REQ-ID discipline*, and that the warn-only and hard-fail footings "cannot drift" because both run `H5a`'s gate machinery over the live corpus.

None of this is dependency data. The completeness-of-set and transitive-maintenance rationale is owned by `conventions.md` §REQ-ID discipline; the sequencing rationale (why H6a follows H5b, what the two footings reconcile) is already stated in H6a's own `Adds.` prose. The parenthetical restates both. A `Deps` field's normative content is the dependency list itself; the surrounding decision-log is derivable from the Adds and the convention and duplicates obligations maintained elsewhere.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** present-since-inception
**Introducing commit:** `5353dd7` ("pi-loom plan: resolve \"Release-gate activation has no owning leaf\"", 2026-06-10) — the commit that created `H6a-live-corpus-activation.md`.
**History:** The leaf was created in `5353dd7` already carrying a `Deps` parenthetical that restated the "complete coverage-producing set" and transitive-completeness rationale; the defect class predates every later edit. The current wording accreted across subsequent fix commits — `ea6b1da` (added the transitive-completeness / "two footings cannot drift" sentences), `1cab2ae` (added the H7a direct-pin rationale when the H7a edge was introduced), and `37733fd` / `1d22b5c` (reshaped the block) — but each of those resolved a different finding and inherited the pre-existing parked-rationale pattern rather than introducing it. The durable fix targets the parenthetical itself, not any one accreting commit.

## Solution Space

**Shape:** single

### Recommendation

In `docs/plan_topics/H6a-live-corpus-activation.md`, delete the italicised parenthetical block that follows `**Deps.** `H5b, H7a``. The dependency list `H5b, H7a` is the field's normative content and stands on its own.

Edge case for the implementer: one fact in the block is not derivable from the Adds prose — why the `H7a` edge is pinned directly on H6a rather than inherited transitively through `H5b` (H7a closes no new spec REQ-ID, so it is not a member of H5b's coverage-producing set). If that distinction is judged worth preserving for a future maintainer, relocate that one clause into H6a's `Adds.` prose; do not retain it in `Deps`. The transitive-completeness-of-set rationale must not be restated anywhere in this leaf — it is owned by `conventions.md` §REQ-ID discipline.

## Consequence

**Severity:** cosmetic

If shipped unfixed nothing breaks: H6a's executable dependency list (`H5b, H7a`) is correct and the prose only restates rationale. The cost is maintenance — the parenthetical duplicates the transitive-completeness obligation owned by `conventions.md`, so the two can drift, and the redundant decision-log inflates the leaf reviewers must read.
