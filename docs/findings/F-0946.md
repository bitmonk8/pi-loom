## Problem

The `V13d` leaf (`docs/plan_topics/V13d-query-failure-repair.md`) closes two concern clusters that fail for different reasons and share no logic: stateful respond-repair loop control (attempt accounting, the `none`/`0` short-circuit, proximate-variant propagation, the `ContextOverflowError` short-circuit, per-attempt budget) and pure byte-exact follow-up template rendering (the `validator_error`/`schema_repeat` methodology bodies, the lowered-schema `<schema-json>`/`<slug>` substitution, and the most-recent-attempt-only `<ajv-summary>` ordering). A rendering bug and a control-flow bug are diagnosed from different fixtures and fail independently. Bundling them puts both behind one `Ships when` gate, so the leaf cannot go green until both halves are built and a red gate does not localise to one cluster.

## Solution approach

Split the byte-exact rendering assertions out of `V13d` into a new dedicated leaf with a paired `<new>-T` test leaf, retaining `V13d` as the loop-control leaf so existing references stay valid. Move the three rendering `Tests.` bullets (byte-for-byte methodology bodies, lowered-schema `<schema-json>`/`<slug>` substitution, most-recent-attempt-only `<ajv-summary>` ordering) to the new leaf, keeping the loop-control bullets and the `ERR-17` forced-respond injection bullet on `V13d`, and trim `V13d`'s `Adds.`/`Ships when` to drop the verbatim-template language. Propagate references: add the new leaf to the `### V13 — Query` index in `docs/plan.md` and to the `cka-12` query-area row in `coverage-matrix.md`, and to `H5b`'s `Deps.` per the transitive-completeness rule for coverage-mapped obligations.
