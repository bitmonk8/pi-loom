## Finding

The sentence "The synonyms `schema hash`, `schema-hash`, `sha12`, `lowered-schema hash`, and `lowered-schema content hash` are drift to be avoided" includes `sha12`. The recipe produces a **16**-hex-character slug (step 4: "First 16 hex characters of the digest"). `sha12` has no obvious referent — "SHA-12" is not a standard algorithm, and "12 hex chars" does not match the 16-hex output. This appears to be a stale internal shorthand from an earlier version of the algorithm (possibly when the slug was 12 hex chars) that was not cleaned up when the output length changed.
