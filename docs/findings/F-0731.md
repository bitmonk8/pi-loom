## Problem

The `terminal-outcomes-aggregator` paragraph in `overview-and-orientation.md` § Overview opens as the success / fail / cancelled trichotomy summary a reader expects, but its single sentence then expands into roughly 1,500 characters of normative routing detail: the per-arm classification for ceiling #4, the (a) binder argument-binding / slash-load `params` cross-routing carve-out with its `invoke(...)` arm split and `Err(InvokeInfraError { cause: "validation", ... })` envelope, the (b) in-loop model-driven tool-call args row exclusion, the `loomAbort.signal` plumbing, the per-cause caller-surface map, and the partial-append-contract pointer. This material is already authoritatively owned at `errors-and-results/error-model.md#terminal-outcomes`, `hard-ceilings/ceilings-3-and-4.md#ceiling-4-table`, and `errors-and-results/error-model.md#partial-append-contract`, so the Overview restatement is a second authoritative-seeming surface that has drifted independently across successive edits. The sibling aggregators on the page carry the `*Orientation aggregator (per GOV-30)*` tag; this paragraph does not, so its forward-links read as clarifications inside a normative statement rather than as the entry point to the owner.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** multi-commit-interaction

**Introducing commits:**
- `a5ad529` (2026-05-06) — *pi-loom spec: resolve "Orientation conflates cancellation with a separate failure mode"*. Rewrote the paragraph from the prior two-outcome "succeeds or fails" sentence into the closed three-outcome trichotomy with cancellation as a peer arm. As written at this commit the paragraph carried no carve-out detail.
- `8168ea8` (2026-05-10) — *pi-loom spec: resolve "T30 ceiling #4 model-driven row trichotomy carve-out"*. Added the ceiling-#4 in-loop model-driven tool-call args row as an excluded case.
- `a90a58a` (2026-05-10) — *pi-loom spec: resolve "T29 pre-evaluation exclusion clause names ceiling #3 only"*. Expanded the exclusion clause to cover ceiling #3 alongside ceiling #4 and added the per-arm classification forward-link.
- `cdfdc0f` (2026-06-05) — *pi-loom spec: resolve "ERR-5 conflates two pre-evaluation failure surfaces"*. Split the binder argument-binding carve-out to distinguish ceiling #4's slash-load `params` arm (cross-routed through ceiling #3) from the `invoke(...)` `params` arm (which IS an evaluation Failure), spelling out the `Err(InvokeInfraError { cause: "validation", ... })` envelope inline.
- `81bf52a` (2026-06-11) — *docs: collapse spec/ section pages into spec\_topics/*. Pure relocation (`docs/spec/` → `docs/spec\_topics/`); content unchanged.

**History:** the defect did not arrive in any single commit. `a5ad529` produced a clean Overview-shaped paragraph; each subsequent resolution (`8168ea8`, `a90a58a`, `cdfdc0f`) correctly addressed its own narrower defect by qualifying the trichotomy with one more carve-out, layered onto the Overview text rather than onto the owner page. The accumulated weight — three carve-out layers plus the per-arm classification forward-link plus the partial-append-contract pointer — is what now exceeds what an Overview paragraph can carry. The durable fix is to relocate the accreted material to its already-tagged Orientation › Scope aggregator, where the GOV-30 lock-step convention governs how future qualifications should land.

## Solution approach

Rewrite the `terminal-outcomes-aggregator` paragraph back to an Overview-shaped trichotomy summary: name the three outcomes (success / fail / cancelled), forward-link `errors-and-results/error-model.md#terminal-outcomes` as the closed-enumeration owner and `cancellation.md`, and tag it `*Orientation aggregator (per GOV-30)*` so future qualifications land on the owner page under the lock-step convention. Move the per-ceiling routing and carve-out detail next to the ceiling #4 entry under the `hard-runtime-ceilings` aggregator bullet, which already enumerates ceiling #4 with a forward-link to `ceilings-3-and-4.md#ceiling-4-table`. Relocate the `loomAbort.signal` plumbing sentence and the partial-append / caller-surface pointers into the Orientation vicinity or drop them from Overview, since the owner forward-link already reaches them.

## Solution constraints

- Preserve the `terminal-outcomes-aggregator` HTML anchor (or move it with whichever block downstream cross-references resolve into).
- Out of scope to restate: the (a)/(b) Failure-cause carve-out envelope detail owned by `errors-and-results/error-model.md#terminal-outcomes` — the relocated text forward-links it rather than re-stating the envelope.
