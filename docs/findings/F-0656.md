## Problem

The presuppositions `#complete-forced-tool-presupposition` and
`#complete-retry-and-cancellation-presupposition` live on
`conversation-drive.md` under the *Provider compatibility for typed
queries* section, yet both explicitly govern the binder's `complete()`
call at `binder-inference.md#binder-inference-call` (forced-tool selection
via `options.toolChoice`, `options.signal`-honouring cancellation, and
internal retry/`Retry-After` timing). `binder-inference.md` does not
anchor either property locally: its `options.signal` bullet and trailing
retry-delegation sentence stand with no inbound link, and the single
`context.tools` cross-reference to the forced-tool presupposition points
across files into a typed-query-titled section. An auditor or implementer
walking the binder call's contract surface from `binder-inference.md`
misses the two behavioural properties it depends on, and the
corresponding version-bump checklist items (u), (aa), (ab) read as
typed-query-only obligations.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** multi-commit-interaction
**Introducing commits:** `e63ba6c` ("pi-loom spec: resolve \"Per-provider complete() forced-tool re-validation gate\"", 2026-06-05); `3683345` ("pi-loom spec: resolve \"Binder relies on three unpinned complete() behaviours\"", 2026-06-06)
**History:** `git log -S 'complete-forced-tool-presupposition' --follow docs/spec_topics/pi-integration-contract/conversation-drive.md` shows the `#complete-forced-tool-presupposition` paragraph first appeared in `e63ba6c`, authored under the *Provider compatibility for typed queries* section of `conversation-drive.md` (the section that owns the typed-query forced-respond turn), with a single inbound link added in the same commit from `binder-inference.md`'s `context.tools` bullet. `git log -S 'complete-retry-and-cancellation-presupposition' --follow docs/spec_topics/pi-integration-contract/conversation-drive.md` shows `#complete-retry-and-cancellation-presupposition` was added one day later in `3683345` as a second paragraph adjacent to the first, in the same section; that commit edits `version-bump-step2.md` to add checklist items (aa) and (ab) but does not add any inbound link from `binder-inference.md` to the new anchor, and leaves the binder's `options.signal` and retry-timing bullets standing without local cross-references. The placement is the joint outcome of two separate fixes choosing to extend the same typed-query-titled section rather than landing the binder-governing portion of each presupposition on the binder page; neither commit on its own is the defect.

## Solution approach

Move both presuppositions from `conversation-drive.md` into
`binder-inference.md`'s `#binder-inference-call` section, and leave a
forward-link under `conversation-drive.md`'s *Provider compatibility for
typed queries* section pointing the typed-query forced-respond turn at the
relocated anchors. Rewrite `binder-inference.md`'s `context.tools`
cross-reference to `#complete-forced-tool-presupposition` as a same-file
link, and add forward-links from the binder's `options.signal` bullet and
its retry-delegation sentence to `#complete-retry-and-cancellation-presupposition`.
Rephrase the breadcrumb link text on `version-bump-step2.md` items (u),
(aa), (ab) to name `binder-inference.md` as the host page.

## Solution constraints

- Preserve the `#complete-forced-tool-presupposition` and
  `#complete-retry-and-cancellation-presupposition` anchor ids unchanged —
  inbound links from `version-bump-step2.md` items (u)/(aa)/(ab) and from
  `docs/findings/F-0537.md` depend on them resolving.
- Out of scope: keep the `#pic-typed-query-noncompliance` diagnostic-limitation
  block on `conversation-drive.md`; reference it cross-file after relocation
  rather than moving it.
