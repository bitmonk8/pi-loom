## Problem

Several non-narrative `spec_topics/**` pages carry normative MUST/MUST-NOT obligations that own no numbered `PREFIX-N` REQ-ID and no `loom/...` registry code, yet the `coverage-matrix.md` *Code-keyed obligation areas (no numbered REQ-IDs)* table binds none of them to a closing leaf: `pi-integration-contract/capability-inventory-items.md` (the seven "Pi MUST expose …" items and the peer-dep re-validation MUST), `diagnostics/placeholder-rendering-a.md`/`-b.md` (the category-4/6, §8 discriminator-verbatim, and eight-category Closure MUSTs), and `pi-integration-contract/version-bump-step2.md`/`-step2b.md` (the editorial-review, inventory-closure-audit, `engines.node` re-confirmation, and `peerDependencies` joint-move MUSTs). All sit under prefix-owning subtrees, so `H5e`'s un-anchored-MUST text-scan walks them. Implementation coverage already exists (`V7c`/`V4b`/`V18a`/`V9a`/`V18c`), but because no `cka` row enumerates these pages, the obligations are not formally traced to a closing leaf and `H5e`'s verdict on them is indeterminate on the live-corpus footing that binds at the `H6a` release gate.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** single-commit (matrix-inception + the un-anchored-MUST surface; per-page omissions present since each surface's inception)
**Introducing commits:** `c6a664e` ("pi-loom plan: build/update plan for spec.md + review", 2026-06-10); `0603eb4` ("resolve 'un-anchored normative MUSTs invisible to closing gate'", 2026-06-10)
**History:** The `coverage-matrix.md` cka table, the `PIC-15` → `V18a` mapping, and `V18c` were authored together in `c6a664e`. The *Code-keyed obligation areas* un-anchored-MUST surface was introduced in `0603eb4`, whose enumeration sweep did not list `diagnostics/placeholder-rendering-a.md`/`-b.md`, `pi-integration-contract/version-bump-step2.md`/`-step2b.md`, or `capability-inventory-items.md`. Pickaxe walks (`git log -S` over `coverage-matrix.md` for each page basename) return nothing — none of the three page-sets has ever been added to or removed from the matrix. Later commits added adjacent version-bump coverage piecemeal (`version-bump-triggers.md` in `81ab342`, the `audit-*` pages in `7ad9df1`) but never closed these gaps. Each omission has persisted for the full life of its surface; none was introduced by a later edit.

## Solution approach

In the *Code-keyed obligation areas* table, add one append-only `cka` row keyed on `placeholder-rendering-a.md`/`-b.md` closing on `V7c`, with a `V4b` co-witness annotation for the `Infinity`/`NaN` → `loom/runtime/internal-error` arm. Add a second append-only `cka` row keyed on `version-bump-step2.md`/`-step2b.md` closing on `V18c`. For `capability-inventory-items.md`, record a back-reference exclusion in the table preamble rather than a row — its seven "Pi MUST expose" items are owned by `PIC-15`→`V18a` and its peer-dep re-validation MUST by `cka-19`→`V18d` plus the `V18c` static gates. Add the citing test(s) the matrix conventions require for each new `cka-<n>` token.

## Solution constraints

- Out of scope: `H5b` Deps ranges — `V7c`, `V4b`, `V18a`, `V9a`, and `V18c` are already covered there, so no Deps edit is needed.
- The spec is read-only for this fix; do not anchor these MUSTs in `spec_topics/**`.
- Follow the coverage-matrix *Closing-leaf column authoring* and *Multi-leaf-row per-facet citing tests* conventions: backtick-delimit each leaf-ID token, mark the `V4b` co-witness with the literal `co-witness` token, and take the next free `cka-<n>` from the append-only `Token` column.
