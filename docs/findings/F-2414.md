## Problem

The inventory-audit pages interleave loom-1.0 MUSTs with post-1.0 hardening mechanics under a post-1.0 framing, with no phase boundary legible at a glance. `inventory-audit-intro.md` carries the loom-1.0 PIC-15 SDK capability inventory and the loom-1.0 strict-capability absence-under-the-probed-name arm immediately around the post-1.0 inventory-closure-audit paragraph with no separator. `audit-target-categories.md` stamps its whole page as post-1.0 hardening via a top-of-page banner, yet states two loom-1.0 step-2(a) obligations — the minimum-entry-shape enforcement and the branch-table-arm precondition — as normative MUSTs buried inside multi-hundred-word paragraphs. Correct phase ownership is recoverable only by careful reading; no per-obligation phase label, separator, or Non-goals index exists.

## Solution approach

Restructure `inventory-audit-intro.md` (`#sdk-capability-inventory`) so the loom-1.0 obligations read as loom-1.0 gates distinct from the post-1.0 `#sdk-cap-inventory-closure-audit` paragraph, separating the loom-1.0 `#strict-capability-absence-under-probed-name` arm from the post-1.0 audit mechanics. Add a Non-goals index on that page, modelled on `version-bump-intro.md`'s Non-goals section, forward-linking `future-considerations/model-changes-and-non-goals.md#loom-1-0-non-goals` and naming the deferred automated inventory-closure audit. On `audit-target-categories.md`, restructure the `#audit-target-surface-categories` paragraph so the minimum-entry-shape enforcement and branch-table-arm precondition loom-1.0 MUSTs read as loom-1.0 gates against the page's post-1.0 banner.

## Solution constraints

- Keep the Non-goals index a cross-link list, not a restatement: `future-considerations/model-changes-and-non-goals.md#loom-1-0-non-goals` owns the canonical loom 1.0 non-goals.
- Do not re-scope either obligation while relabeling: the surface-set-closure obligation remains a loom-1.0 MUST and the automated inventory-closure audit remains post-1.0.
