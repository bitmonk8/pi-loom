## Problem

`tool-registration-lifetime.md` already carries the `PIC-8`, `PIC-17`, and `PIC-19` REQ-ID anchors, but two normative MUST areas on the page remain un-anchored: the cache-hit byte-equality verification the runtime must perform before reusing a cached registration (the `loom/runtime/registration-cache-collision` defining MUST), and the registered-before-body-runs / not-unregistered-on-teardown pair — two temporally-disjoint obligations expressed under a single prose claim. With no `PIC-N` anchor at these sites, GOV-9's `#prefix-n` cross-link contract is unsatisfiable for them.

## Solution approach

Coin the next free `PIC-N` REQ-ID anchor in GOV-1 dual-form at each un-anchored defining site under the page's registered `PIC` prefix, per GOV-22, querying the current `PIC` high-water mark at edit time. Split the temporally-disjoint registered-before-body / not-unregistered-on-teardown pair into two separate `PIC-N` anchors per the REQ-ID atomicity discipline. Close the corresponding `coverage-matrix.md` row for the newly anchored MUSTs.
