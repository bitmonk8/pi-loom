## Problem

`docs/spec_topics/query/query-escapes-stringification.md` is titled — by both its H1 and filename — for two concerns only: template escapes (QRY-17) and interpolation stringification (QRY-18). It additionally carries QRY-19 ("Discarded query results are a parse error", query-`Result`-handling discipline) and QRY-20 ("Observability of discarded results", a runtime-event-channel delivery contract), neither of which is about escapes or stringification. The title therefore under-describes the page's scope: a reader navigating by topic for discard discipline or discard observability has no reason to open it, and the plan corpus already separates these concerns across leaves `V13a` (render/escapes) and `V13g` (discard discipline and observability). QRY-21 ("Panics during interpolation are not caught by `let _ =`") straddles both topics and is a placement edge case, not part of the core defect.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** single-commit
**Introducing commits:** f5e89f4 ("docs: enforce 100KB hard / 30KB recommended size cap on spec set")
**History:** The discard sections originated inside the monolithic `docs/spec_topics/query.md`, where they sat between "Stringification of interpolated values" and the tool-loop sections under the undifferentiated "Query" page — no file-title mismatch was possible there. Commit f5e89f4 split that page into topic-scoped files under `query/` to satisfy the spec-set size cap and, in doing so, bundled "Escapes", "Stringification of interpolated values", "Discarded query results are a parse error", "Observability of discarded results", and "Panics during interpolation" into a single file titled `# Query escapes stringification`. The title-vs-contents mismatch was introduced by that bundling choice; the later QRY-1..21 code coinage (716a13b) only labelled the already-misfiled sections and did not introduce or relocate them.

## Solution approach

Move QRY-19 and QRY-20 out of `query-escapes-stringification.md` into a new dedicated discard-discipline topic file under `docs/spec_topics/query/`, and add that file to the `query.md` child-page index. Keep QRY-21 with the stringification content in `query-escapes-stringification.md` and add a forward-link from it to the new discard file. Rewrite the existing cross-references into the relocated sections — the `code-registry-parse.md` discarded-query-result row, the `query.md` index, and the `V13g` / `V13g-T` Spec pointers — to target the new file.

## Solution constraints

- Relocate QRY-19/QRY-20 verbatim; do not edit their normative content (content edits to those sections are owned by sibling findings touching the QRY-20 discard-observability surface).
- Preserve the QRY-19/QRY-20 REQ-ID labels and anchors on the move; do not renumber or re-prefix.
