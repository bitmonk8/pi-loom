## Problem

Output (c)'s green predicate pins *green* to "each of the six surfaces above exercised", but names no mechanism that reddens `npm test` when the contributor-selected `.loom` omits one of the six surfaces. Green is therefore necessary-but-not-sufficient for the stated predicate: a bump whose `.loom` skips a surface can land green, so a Pi minor that regresses behaviour behind an unexercised surface goes undetected, defeating output (c)'s purpose.

## Solution approach

Rewrite output (c)'s green predicate in `version-bump-triggers.md` so six-surface coverage is mechanically enforced rather than implied by `.loom` selection — either by requiring the gate fixture to carry a per-surface coverage assertion that reddens when any of the six surfaces is unexercised, or by cross-referencing an `H4a` guarantee that the harness fails when a named surface is absent from the driven `.loom`. State that contributor selection of the `.loom` does not substitute for the mechanical check.
