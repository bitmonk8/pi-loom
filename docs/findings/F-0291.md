## Problem

`V15a` (`docs/plan_topics/V15a-invocation-core.md`) and its paired `V15a-T` carry a single `Tests.` list of roughly fourteen bullets drawn from six spec topics, covering largely independent concerns: discovery-root `realpath` containment (`INV-1`/`INV-2`/`INV-3`), the fresh-vs-attach cross-mode matrix, the static-resolution transitive parse-cache walk, the `invoke` final-value propagation contract, six `loom/parse/*` + `loom/load/*` diagnostic codes, the swallowing-handler cancellation-suppression assertion delegated from `V17a`, two ceiling-#4 depth-surface rows delegated from `V5e`, and the `SLSH-5` per-frame invocation-record provenance seam consumed by `V12b`. The single `Ships when.` gate fires only when all fourteen obligations are green at once, so no single review pass can validate the leaf and a regression in any one facet reddens the whole leaf without localising the failure. Several parked facets already have distinct downstream consumers (`V12b` consumes the provenance record; the six diagnostic codes close an `invocation.md` code-keyed coverage-matrix row; the ceiling-#4 rows are a delegated witness for `V5e`), making them natural candidates for their own paired leaves.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** multi-commit-interaction (accretion)
**Introducing commits:** `7a8565a` (added the `invoke` final-value-propagation bullet), `249cec5` (added the static-resolution transitive parse-cache walk bullet), `cddd2b4` (added the six `loom/parse/*` + `loom/load/*` diagnostic-code bullets), `75a9bcd` (added the swallowing-handler cancellation-suppression bullet), `65363c9` (added the `SLSH-5` provenance bullet), `3d1ffd7` (added the ceiling-#4 depth-surface bullet)
**History:** At inception (`c6a664e`) `V15a` carried four test bullets — `INV-1`/`INV-2`/`INV-3` and the cross-mode matrix. The defect emerged from the cumulative interaction of the six later gap-closing "resolve …" commits above, each of which closed an unrelated coverage gap by parking its obligation onto `V15a` without re-checking the leaf's growing total. A "Systemic leaf over-bundling" pass (`cb8737a`) ran in the same window and peeled the prompt→prompt parent-suspend facet off into `V15d`, but did not arrest the continuing accretion that brought the bullet count to ~14. No single commit is responsible; the over-bundling is the product of their interaction.

## Solution approach

Decompose `V15a` / `V15a-T` by concern cluster: peel the independent parked obligations into their own paired `<new>`/`<new>-T` leaves and leave `V15a` as the invoke-execution core (`INV-1`/`INV-2`/`INV-3` containment, the cross-mode matrix, the static-resolution cache + walk, and final-value propagation). Peel the six `loom/parse/*` + `loom/load/*` diagnostic codes, the `SLSH-5` provenance producer seam, and the ceiling-#4 depth-surface rows + swallowing-handler suppression assertion into separate paired leaves; register the new leaves in the V15 listing in `docs/plan.md`, repoint the `invocation.md` six-code coverage-matrix row to the new parse-codes leaf, and repoint `V12b` / `V12b-T` `Deps.` to the new provenance leaf. Set each new leaf's `Deps.` and `Ships when.` to cover only its own obligation, and verify every repointed dependency edge names the leaf that owns the facet it depends on.

## Solution constraints

- The prompt→prompt parent-suspend facet is owned by `V15d` — out of scope; do not fold it back into `V15a`.
- New paired leaves follow the plan's `V<N><letter>` + `-T` leaf-ID ritual (use `<new>` placeholders for the new IDs, never reuse a retired ID); keep `docs/plan_topics/conventions.md` and `docs/plan_topics/coverage-matrix.md` consistent when adding leaves.
