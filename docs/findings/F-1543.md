## Problem

The operator-facing observability property that loom diagnostics durably enter the user-session model context and contribute to `ctx.getContextUsage()` and compaction decisions lives only inside `runtime-event-channel.md`'s `#custom-message-context-entry-presupposition` note, in the middle of a mechanism-level SDK presupposition. This is a cross-cutting non-functional disposition governing how operators reason about context-window growth across every loom, independent of any single channel variant. The dedicated home for this class of statement ŌĆö `overview-and-orientation.md` ┬¦"Non-functional requirements", "Runtime observability" bullet ŌĆö covers the channel, the always-log set, the success-side null-policy, and per-mode surfaces, but says nothing about diagnostics counting toward context growth and does not forward-link the presupposition note. An operator consulting the NFR aggregator cannot discover the context-growth property; it is reachable only by reading deep into the channel-implementation section.
