## Finding

"genuine" is an undefined qualifier. The sentence is trying to distinguish `await` calls that yield only to the microtask queue (e.g., `await Promise.resolve()`) from those that yield a macrotask turn (e.g., `await fs.readFile(...)`). An implementer might read "no genuine `await`" as "no `await` at all" and incorrectly infer that any `await` expression — including a microtask-only one — satisfies the event-loop-release requirement.
