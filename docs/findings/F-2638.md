## Problem

The loom 1.0 renderer requirement in `extension-bootstrap-and-per-loom.md` mixes a future-widening non-goal into must-build-now text. The sentence beginning "Widening the renderer to use `expanded` to disclose the structured `details` payload … is reserved for a future revision and is not part of loom 1.0 conformance" sits inside the normative v1 renderer paragraph that pins the `MessageRenderOptions` obligation. Deferred capability described inside the normative body risks being built for loom 1.0 and is hard to discover as a deferral.

## Solution approach

Move the `expanded`-widening deferral sentence out of the renderer paragraph in `extension-bootstrap-and-per-loom.md` and into `future-considerations/surface-extensions.md` under `#surface-extensions-v1-leaves-a-seam` as a deferred-surface entry. In the renderer spec, retain the loom 1.0 obligation (`MUST be defined for both `expanded === false` and `expanded === true`; MAY render identically`) and add a forward-link to the relocated deferral.
