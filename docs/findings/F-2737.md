## Problem

`V14b`'s lowering rules presuppose a real-Pi parallel-tool-mode delivery shape (concurrent execution, settle-all-before-next-turn, independent-per-sibling lowering), but its Tests and Ships-when drive the batch only through the in-process `H4a` session double, whose fidelity contract does not enumerate parallel-batch delivery. The shape is therefore measured by nothing observable for loom 1.0: not by the double, not by the real-host smoke gate (it is not a scored criterion or fidelity axis), and not by version-bump item **(an)** (SHOULD-level, and it audits only a future host-side *change* rather than confirming the current pin delivers the shape). The `V14b` Adds frames its routing as "mirroring the way SLSH-2's real-Pi streaming-ordering behaviour routes to item (ak)," but that parity is false — SLSH-2's streamed-token ordering *is* a scored fidelity axis of the real-host smoke gate, whereas parallel-batch delivery carries no such axis. The Adds prose thus overstates `V14b`'s real-host coverage relative to its cited sibling.

## Solution approach

Rewrite the `V14b` Adds prose to remove the false SLSH-2 real-host parity framing and state the measurement status accurately: that the parallel-batch delivery shape is dispositioned but unmeasured against a real host for loom 1.0, covered by no real-host smoke criterion or fidelity axis, and routed only to SHOULD-level version-bump item (an) — which audits a future host-side change rather than confirming the current pinned Pi delivers the assumed shape. Leave the leaf's unmeasured-presupposition posture intact; correct only its disclosure so it no longer overstates real-host coverage.
