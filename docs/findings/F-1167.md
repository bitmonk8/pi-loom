## Problem

On `version-bump-step2.md`, item (a) of the *Editorial-review checklist for unpinned host presuppositions* is a one-shot, dispositive open question that MUST be resolved before SM-4/5/6 are implemented and records one of two verdicts (`governed-by-rebind` / `provably-differs — <cited evidence>`) — semantics unlike every peer row, which records a recurring per-bump `pass` / `fail` / `N/A` / `not-performed` audit outcome. Yet item (a) is rendered as a peer row at `#bump-checklist-instance-survival`, sandwiched between (b) and (c), so its blocking nature is discoverable only by reading that row and the *Item (a) authoritative-determination recording shape* paragraph (at `#bump-step-2-outcome-recording-conventions`, ~100 lines further down) back-to-back. The `SM-4/5/6` reference in the item-(a) row body is plain text; its cross-link to `session-model-and-appendix.md#sm-4-session-only-degraded-state` exists only in that distant recording-conventions paragraph.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** single-commit
**Introducing commits:** 7447d1e — pi-loom plan: resolve "Session-only degraded-state presupposition (a) contradicts Pi's documented lifecycle" (2026-06-10, Thomas Andersen)
**History:** The row was first introduced as a bare reference to the host-prerequisites clause at f5e89f4 (2026-06-04, the spec-split commit that created `version-bump-step2.md`); at that point clause (a) on `host-prerequisites.md` itself was framed as just another presupposition (per the diff in 7447d1e, which rewrites clause (a) from "When `event.reason ∈ {…}`, Pi keeps the same extension instance loaded …" to "open contradiction against Pi's documented lifecycle … MUST be resolved before SM-4/5/6 …"). 7447d1e is the commit that introduced the blocking semantics into both `host-prerequisites.md` clause (a) and the `version-bump-step2.md` item-(a) row, in the same edit, but kept the item-(a) row formatted identically to its peers (b)–(ao) and left the ordering MUST unenforced — that is the defect this finding identifies. Subsequent commits (80fcd63, 2026-06-20) bolted on the item-(a)-specific authoritative-determination recording shape as a paragraph at the bottom of the page rather than rethinking the row's placement, hardening the very split-across-the-page structure this finding objects to.

## Solution approach

Restructure the checklist preamble so item (a) is named as a separate blocking category rather than the lead element of the `(a)`–`(e)` MUST-audited group, rewording the affected count and range references. Mark the item-(a) row at `#bump-checklist-instance-survival` as a one-shot pre-implementation blocker distinct from the recurring per-bump audit rows. Move the *Item (a) authoritative-determination recording shape* sub-paragraph out of `#bump-step-2-outcome-recording-conventions` to co-locate it with the item-(a) row body. Add a forward-link to `session-model-and-appendix.md#sm-4-session-only-degraded-state` at the first `SM-4/5/6` occurrence in the row body.
