## Problem

`V5e` / `V5e-T` exercise the loom-owned JSON-depth walk **in isolation** against the harness: AJV and the five live enforcement sites are not present at V5e, and Ships-when scopes the proof to V5e's position "without the downstream surface-owning leaves." Against that isolated mechanism the third Tests bullet — "The walk runs before AJV; respond-repair applies only at site #1." — asserts a property the harness cannot observe: there is no AJV in the isolated walk to order against, and respond-repair is owned at the typed-query/repair site, not at V5e. An author following the leaf either drops the clause or writes a vacuous test that reports green while witnessing nothing. The before-AJV ordering is witnessable only where the depth walk and AJV are co-present — at the live-carrier site owners.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** single-commit
**Introducing commit:** `3fa39a9` — "pi-loom plan: resolve \"V5e per-boundary routing test asserts destination error surfaces its Deps cannot reach\""
**History:** The "The walk runs before AJV" bullet was authored at the plan's initial build (`c6a664e`), when V5e's first two Tests bullets and Ships-when exercised the depth walk "at each of the five sites" — sites where AJV is co-present, so the before-AJV ordering was witnessable. Commit `3fa39a9` refactored bullets 1–2 and Ships-when to drive the walk **in isolation against the harness** and delegated the live carriers to `V13c`/`V14a`/`V15a`/`V4e`, but left the third bullet unchanged. From that commit the before-AJV assertion no longer matches the now-isolated mechanism. (Verified via `git log -S`/`git show` over `docs/plan_topics/V5e-depth-enforcement.md`; the corpus is git-tracked.)

## Solution approach

Rewrite the third Tests bullet in `V5e` and `V5e-T` to assert only what the isolated depth-walk harness observes — the `maxDepth` fire and the per-boundary routing decision already carried by bullets 1–2 — and remove the before-AJV ordering and respond-repair claims. The before-AJV ordering already lives at the live-carrier owners `V14a` and `V15a`. Confirm `V13c` carries the equivalent before-AJV observable for its depth-6 co-fire vector, adding it there if absent.

## Solution constraints

- The spec is read-only for this finding; the fix is internal to the plan leaves.
- Out of scope: respond-repair scoping is owned at the typed-query/repair site (`V13c`/`V13d`), not `V5e`.
