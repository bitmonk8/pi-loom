## Finding

Three of the five *Tests* bullets in `V9b-T-registration-reload-wiring.md` — the `getCommands() snapshot read-only`, `structural-change watcher note, empty-window suppression`, and `structural-change watcher note, same-window rename emission` bullets — open their spec-link annotation with `(PIC area; un-anchored GOV-22 residue; facet \`cka-23.<sub>\`)`.

The `PIC area; un-anchored GOV-22 residue` portion is governance provenance: it records *why* the underlying obligation carries no numbered `PREFIX-N` REQ-ID (it is un-anchored GOV-22 residue on a PIC-area page). That provenance does not constrain the test and the test author cannot act on it. The only actionable token in each parenthetical is the `cka-23.<sub>` facet sub-label, which is what the per-facet citing-test gate (`H5f`) reconciles against the test corpus. The GOV-22 classification for `cka-23` is already carried by the `coverage-matrix.md` `cka-23` row, which enumerates the three obligation areas and their facet sub-labels — so the inline `un-anchored GOV-22 residue` text duplicates classification metadata that lives at its authoritative single source.

These annotations mirror, verbatim, the same redundant text on the paired implementation leaf `V9b-registration-reload-wiring.md`; the two leaves are a test/impl pair and must stay in sync.

## Solution Space

**Shape:** single

### Recommendation

In `docs/plan_topics/V9b-T-registration-reload-wiring.md`, strike the literal string `PIC area; un-anchored GOV-22 residue; ` from the parenthetical of each of the three *Tests* bullets that carry it (`getCommands() snapshot read-only`, `structural-change watcher note, empty-window suppression`, `structural-change watcher note, same-window rename emission`), leaving the spec-link and the `facet \`cka-23.<sub>\`` sub-label. Each parenthetical becomes `(facet \`cka-23.getcommands\`)`, `(facet \`cka-23.note-empty\`)`, and `(facet \`cka-23.note-rename\`)` respectively. Do not alter the first *Tests* bullet (`registry swap`, `(PIC area; facet \`cka-23.swap\`)`) — it carries no GOV-22-residue text and is outside this finding. Leave `coverage-matrix.md` and the spec untouched; the GOV-22 classification stays at its single source in the `cka-23` row.

## Consequence

**Severity:** cosmetic

The redundant provenance text changes no test behaviour and triggers no gate: `H5f` scans the test corpus for the retained `cka-23.<sub>` facet sub-label, not the discarded provenance prose. The cost is reader noise and a duplicated classification that can drift from the `cka-23` coverage-matrix row.
