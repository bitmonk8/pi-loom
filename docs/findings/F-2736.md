## Problem

`V14a`/`V14a-T` bundle two distinct verification surfaces in one paired leaf: the parse/type-contract surface (the three parse-time argument codes, the closed `CodeToolError` enum, the two return-type rows, and the `.loom`-callable failure) and the runtime-execution surface (the five accepted-path `execute()` envelope-lowering mechanics, the `cka-47` tool-call cancellation-checkpoint facet, and the `ERR-13` completed-callee-finality witness). These are written and verified differently and cannot be turned red→green in one coherent increment, exceeding the smallest-shippable-leaf convention in `conventions.md`. Sibling live-surface leaves `V13c` and `V15a` each carry only their core feature plus one `cka-47` facet and one `ERR-13` witness; `V14a` additionally carries the five-mechanic runtime block on top of that baseline.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** multi-commit-interaction
**Introducing commits:** `77f2e88`, `849d926`, `a63786f`
**History:** Commit `5a1915d` ("resolve V14a bundles eight independently-testable concerns", 2026-06-17) had already split `V14a`, peeling its off-surface routing, host-denial, depth-ceiling, and swallowing-handler concerns into `V14c`–`V14f` and leaving `V14a` lean (parse codes + enum + return rows + `.loom`-callable failure). Three later, independently-motivated edits re-inflated it: `77f2e88` ("code-side tool-call envelope-lowering MUSTs unanchored", 2026-06-18) re-added the five `execute()` envelope-lowering mechanics; `849d926` (F-1404, 2026-06-25) added the `cka-47` tool-call-checkpoint facet distributed off `V17c`; and `a63786f` (F-1937, 2026-06-27) added the `ERR-13` completed-callee-finality co-witness. Each edit was locally reasonable and small; their accumulation re-crossed the leaf-size threshold the earlier split had restored.

## Solution approach

Split a new paired leaf out of `V14a`/`V14a-T` to own the runtime `execute()` envelope-lowering surface, carrying the `cka-47` checkpoint facet and `ERR-13` witness with it (they ride on the live tool-call execution surface, the way `V13c` and `V15a` co-locate theirs), and leave the parse/type-contract surface — the argument checks, the closed `CodeToolError` enum, the return-type rows, and the `.loom`-callable failure — in `V14a`. Retarget the `V14a` `cka-47` facet, the `cka-46` `execute()`-lowering reference, and the `ERR-13` `V14a` co-witness entries in `coverage-matrix.md` to the new leaf, and add the new leaf's link to the `### V14 — Tool calls` list in `plan.md`. Set the new leaf's `Deps` to its live-execution-surface inputs and re-audit the `V14b`–`V14f` `Deps` edges to point at whichever leaf owns each consumed surface.
