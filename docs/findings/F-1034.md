## Problem

Rule 2 of *Conversation drive ‚ÄĒ subagent mode* in `subagent.md` justifies passing `tools: []` with the parenthetical "matching `tools: []` ‚Č° absent `tools:` from [Parameters and Frontmatter]". The bare `‚Č°` operator carries no scope and sits two sentences upstream of the directly opposing claim in the same rule ‚ÄĒ "omitting `tools` would re-enable Pi's default built-in `read` / `bash` / `edit` / `write` tools regardless of what the loom declared". The equivalence holds only at the loom-frontmatter layer (`frontmatter-fields-a.md`); at the `createAgentSession` layer the surrounding prose is normating, the two forms are *not* equivalent ‚ÄĒ an empty allowlist suppresses built-ins, an omitted field re-enables them. A reader anchoring on the `‚Č°` symbol before reaching the next sentence sees a flat contradiction against the tool-isolation invariant the rule rides on.
