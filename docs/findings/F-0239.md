## Finding

`V3d` owns the final-value contract and the function-result seam. Its final-value Tests bullet — `the function body's produced value is its final value at the function-result seam on success and is absent on fail/cancel` — and the matching Ships-when clause assert a property over the full success / fail / cancel trichotomy. `V3d`'s Deps are `V3d-T`, `V3a`, `V4a`.

The success and fail halves are driveable at `V3d`'s implementation point: `V4a` supplies the `?` operator's Err early-return desugaring (`return Err(e)`), and through `V4a → V4d` the `QueryError` shapes are reachable, so the fail branch can be exercised. The cancel half has no producer in scope. The cancellation runtime — the `loomAbort` controller, the checkpoint set, and the late-settlement discard rules — is owned by `V17a`, and abort injection into a test is owned by the `H4b` response-programming surface. Neither `V17a` nor the `H4a`/`H4b` harness is among `V3d`'s declared Deps, and both sit in much later phases than `V3` (`V17` and the horizontal phase respectively). `V3d` therefore cannot drive a live cancellation, so the cancel clause of its acceptance criteria cannot be honoured as written.

The plan already demonstrates the correct pattern elsewhere: `V4c` (terminal outcomes), which owns the closed success/failure/cancelled trichotomy and turn-grain finality symmetric across cancellation and `?`-propagation, declares `V17a`, `H4a`, and `H4b` in its Deps. `V15a` and `V9i` likewise assert "absent on fail/cancel" against the seam `V3d` defines, as downstream consumers. `V3d` asserts the cancel facet without acquiring any of the machinery those leaves declare.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** present-since-inception

**Introducing commit:** `c6a664e1194227df0702609667f3ee8be2e8c6b2` — "pi-loom plan: build/update plan for spec.md + review" (2026-06-10)

**History:** `V3d` was created in the initial plan build (`c6a664e`); at that commit its final-value Tests bullet already read "the final value propagates to an `invoke`/subagent caller on success and is absent on fail/cancel" with Deps `V3d-T, V3a, V4a` — i.e. the "absent on fail/cancel" cancel clause was present from inception with no `V17a`/harness edge in Deps. The bullet was later reworded by `7a8565a8a3429e83d9f328d13b9011dc798372c2` ("resolve \"V3d-T over-asserts final-value propagation\"", 2026-06-11), which narrowed the claim from cross-boundary propagation to "the function body's produced value is its final value at the function-result seam" in both `V3d` and `V3d-T`; that edit touched the same bullet but addressed the over-asserted propagation facet only and left the cancel-producer gap intact. The Deps line was never amended to add a cancel source. The defect has therefore been continuously present since the plan's first build.

## Solution Space

**Shape:** multiple

### Option A — Narrow `V3d` to success + fail; defer the cancel facet to its owners

**Approach.** Restrict `V3d`'s final-value obligation to the halves driveable with its existing Deps and let the cancel branch be carried by the leaves that own the cancellation machinery.

**Plan edits.** In `docs/plan_topics/V3d-functions-and-return.md`, change the final-value Tests bullet and the Ships-when clause so they assert that the function-result seam yields the produced value on success and yields no final value on the fail path (`?`/Err propagation), dropping the live-cancel obligation. Mirror the same change in `docs/plan_topics/V3d-T-functions-and-return.md`. The cancel facet of the final-value contract is then carried by `V4c` (turn-grain finality symmetric across cancellation and `?`-propagation; it already declares `V17a`, `H4a`, `H4b`) and by the seam consumers `V15a` / `V9i` ("absent on fail/cancel" against the seam `V3d` defines).

**Spec edits.** None.

**Pros.** Respects the V3-before-V17 phase ordering; no forward dependency; the cancel branch lands where the cancellation machinery actually exists.

**Cons.** `V3d` no longer asserts any cancel facet of the seam it defines; coverage of the cancel branch rests entirely on downstream leaves.

**Risks.** If `coverage-matrix.md` maps the RET final-value cancel facet to `V3d`, the row must be updated to credit `V4c`/`V15a`/`V9i` for the cancel branch, or the matrix will over-credit `V3d`.

### Option B — Keep the cancel facet at `V3d` as a function-result-seam property

**Approach.** Retain a cancel assertion at the leaf that owns the final-value contract, but express it as a seam property exercised against a supplied terminal outcome rather than a live cancellation.

**Plan edits.** In `docs/plan_topics/V3d-functions-and-return.md` (and the `V3d-T` mirror), reword the cancel clause so the seam is handed a cancelled terminal outcome — the cancelled `QueryError` shape is reachable through the existing `V4a → V4d` dep chain — and the test asserts no final value flows, without driving the live `V17a` cancellation runtime (`loomAbort` / checkpoint / late-settlement discard) or the `H4b` abort-injection harness. Reflect the seam-property phrasing in the Ships-when clause.

**Spec edits.** None.

**Pros.** The leaf that owns the final-value contract keeps asserting the full success/fail/cancel trichotomy of its own seam; uses only Deps `V3d` already declares; no forward dependency; no shift of coverage onto downstream leaves.

**Cons.** The cancel assertion is a synthetic injected outcome, not a live cancellation; the live cancel-through-checkpoint path remains proven only at `V17a`/`V4c`.

**Risks.** The injected cancelled-outcome representation must match the `V4d` `QueryError` cancelled `kind`/`cause` form that `V17a`/`V4c` later drive live, or the seam property will test a representation that does not match what ships.

### Recommendation

Take **Option B**: keep the cancel facet at `V3d` as a seam property. It is the minimal, ordering-safe edit — it requires only Deps `V3d` already declares, introduces no forward dependency on `V17a`, and keeps the leaf that owns the final-value contract testing its own seam rather than offloading the cancel branch to downstream leaves. Edge case for the implementer: the injected cancelled terminal outcome must use the `V4d` `QueryError` cancelled `kind`/`cause` form so the seam property does not drift from the representation `V17a`/`V4c` later drive live.

## Consequence

**Severity:** correctness

An implementer landing `V3d` in the `V3` phase has no cancellation machinery available: `V17a`'s `loomAbort`/checkpoint/late-settlement discard and the `H4b` abort-injection harness are neither in scope nor in Deps. The fail half of "absent on fail/cancel" is driveable via `V4a`, but the cancel half is not, so the Ships-when gate either passes vacuously (the cancel branch goes untested) or the implementer fabricates an ad-hoc cancellation that diverges from the `V17a`-owned semantics. Two reasonable implementers would resolve this differently.
