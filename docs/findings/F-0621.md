## Problem

`docs/spec_topics/binder/binder-bypass-and-envelope.md` ┬Д *System-prompt structure (normative)* item 8 ("No-invent-defaults instruction") requires the prompt to contain "**a single line**" carrying both `defaulted` and a directive substring. The phrase admits two incompatible readings Рђћ exactly-one (additional matching lines make the prompt non-conforming) and at-least-one (additional matching lines are permitted) Рђћ which yield different conformance verdicts and different conformance tests. The neighbouring items pin their cardinality explicitly (item 1 "Exactly one such line per prompt", item 3 "MUST appear exactly once", item 4 "one per-field line per declared field"), leaving item 8 the lone unpinned cardinality in the section. A renderer that emits the directive on two lines (e.g. one for parameter defaulting, one for array-element defaulting) sits squarely in the gap between the two readings.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** single-commit
**Introducing commits:** `93439f0` (2026-05-07) "pi-loom spec: resolve \"T05 Рђћ Item 8 of binder system-prompt structure has no testable surface\""
**History:** Item 8 originally read "The prompt MUST contain an instruction directing the model not to invent values for defaulted parameters the user did not specify. Wording is non-normative; the instruction's presence is." Commit `93439f0` rewrote it to the current pinned-token form ("a single line that includes both the literal substring `defaulted` Рђд and at least one of the directive substrings `Do not`, `omit`, or `skip`") to close T05's testability gap. The rewrite introduced the "a single line" cardinality phrasing Рђћ the testability fix landed but the cardinality boundary was not disambiguated alongside the substring pins. The later spec-set split `f5e89f4` (2026-06-04) moved the prose from `spec_topics/binder.md` to `docs/spec_topics/binder/binder-bypass-and-envelope.md` without altering item 8's wording.

## Solution approach

Rewrite item 8's cardinality phrase from "a single line" to "**at least one line**", matching the presence-not-exclusivity intent of the testability rewrite and the implicit at-least-one cardinality of sibling items 5 and 7. The directive predicates (`defaulted` plus one of `Do not` / `omit` / `skip`) must still co-occur on the same line; only the line-count cardinality changes.
