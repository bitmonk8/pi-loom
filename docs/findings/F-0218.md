## Problem

`H5d`'s `Adds.` reconciles each `coverage-matrix.md` closing-leaf cell against `H5b`'s `Deps.` by reading "at least one of the leaf IDs that closing-leaf cell lists," but it never states how a cell's free text is tokenised into that leaf-ID list. Comma separation appears only in the illustrative `CIO-5 | V16a, H7a` example, not as a declared parse contract. Live cells interleave backtick-wrapped IDs with parenthetical prose (the `DISC-4`, `CIO-1`, `PIC-17`/`PIC-18`, and multi-leaf Code-keyed rows), so a naive "split on comma, trim" reader and a backtick-token reader derive different leaf-ID sets from the same cell — the simpler reading mis-parses the parenthetical-bearing rows that already populate the matrix, reddening or vacuously greening the gate against correct data.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** present-since-inception (of the transitive-completeness arm)
**Introducing commits:** `08bd641` (2026-06-11, "resolve 'H5b coverage Deps completeness has no mechanical backstop'") introduced the transitive-completeness arm; `617a3ad` (2026-06-16, "resolve 'H5a bundles gate arms spanning four data sources'") split it into the standalone `H5d` leaf, carrying the gap forward.
**History:** `git log -S 'transitive-completeness'` shows the arm first appeared in `08bd641` and was never authored with a cell-tokenisation rule; the subsequent `37733fd`/`ea6b1da` edits and the `617a3ad` split into `H5d-transitive-completeness-gate.md` preserved the same omission. `git show 08bd641` confirms no `split on` / `comma-separat` / delimiter language was present at inception.

## Solution approach

In `H5d`'s `Adds.`, declare the cell-tokenisation step that runs before the existing contiguous-range and named-singleton expansion: state how the arm extracts the leaf-ID set from a closing-leaf cell's free text. Ground the rule against the live parenthetical-bearing rows (`DISC-4`, `CIO-1`, `PIC-17`/`PIC-18`, and the multi-leaf Code-keyed rows) so the contract resolves their backtick IDs and ignores surrounding annotation prose, and keep the already-excluded `<new>` placeholder rows excluded. Extend the `Tests.` and `Ships when.` seeded fixtures to include a closing-leaf cell carrying parenthetical prose alongside its backtick ID(s).

## Solution constraints

- Out of scope: `coverage-matrix.md` is the read-only data surface for this fix — the parse contract is owned by `H5d`, not added to `coverage-matrix.md`.
