## Problem

The per-query bracket in which a prompt-mode loom snapshots the active tool set, calls `pi.setActiveTools`, drives the query, and restores in a `finally` is named three non-equivalent ways: `V9c` Adds calls it the "active-set gating window", the `V9c` / `V9c-T` Tests (PIC-2, PIC-17) call it the "snapshot/restore window", and `V9f` Adds calls it "the snapshot/restore on the prompt path". No leaf declares these as the same concept and there is no glossary bridge. A reader of `V9f` cannot locate the canonical definition (it lives under a different name in `V9c`), and the V9c Adds/Tests split names one window two ways within a single file. The behaviour is fully pinned, so the variance costs cross-leaf navigability rather than changing what is implemented.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** present-since-inception

**Introducing commits:** c6a664e (initial plan build — "active-set gating
window" in V9c Adds and "snapshot/restore on the prompt path" in V9f Adds);
2ce483b (added the PIC-2 bullet carrying the "snapshot/restore window"
phrasing to V9c / V9c-T).

**History:** `git log -S` shows the two inception-era variants ("active-set
gating window" in `V9c-conversation-drive.md` and "snapshot/restore on the
prompt path" in `V9f-tool-registration-lifetime.md`) both entered at the
first plan build, c6a664e (2026-06-10). The third variant ("snapshot/restore
window") entered when the PIC-2 cross-body non-overlap bullet was added to the
V9c/V9c-T pair in 2ce483b (2026-06-10). The underlying divergence — two leaves
naming the same window differently — was therefore present from inception; the
later PIC-2 resolution introduced an additional surface form rather than
creating the inconsistency.

## Solution approach

Canonicalise the per-query window on the noun "active-set gating window" across `V9c` Adds, the `V9c` / `V9c-T` Tests (PIC-2, PIC-17), and `V9f` Adds, retaining "snapshot/restore" only as the descriptor of the window's mechanism. The `V9c` Adds inline definition is the existing definition site the `V9f` and Tests references should converge on.

## Solution constraints

- Out of scope: `V15d`'s prompt→prompt parent-suspend `setActiveTools` window — it is a distinct cross-mode parent-suspend window and must not be folded into this term.
- Preserve the PIC-2 (cross-body non-overlap) vs PIC-17 (single-query snapshot/restore) distinction; the rename is of the shared window noun only, not a merge of the two assertions.
