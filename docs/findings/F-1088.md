## Problem

`conventions.md` §Leaf format defines the **Deps.** field as an enumeration of leaf IDs (plus ranges) governed by the pickability predicate. Both `V9l` and `V9l-T` overload that field with a prose rationale clause naming `V18c` as the clause-(a) resolution owner and restating the blocking condition. That rationale is already authoritative in `plan.md` §Blocked obligations, so the Deps fields duplicate a subset of it into two leaf files, where the copies can drift independently and the field no longer reads as the IDs-only enumeration the convention requires.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** present-since-inception
**Introducing commits:** `55ed974` — "pi-loom plan: resolve \"degraded-state obligations green over open spec contradiction\"" (2026-06-12)
**History:** `git log --follow` shows `V9l-session-only-degraded-branch.md` and `V9l-T-session-only-degraded-branch.md` were both added (diff-filter=A) in `55ed974`, and `git log -S "clause-(a) resolution owner"` / `git log -G "is \*\*blocked\*\* until"` confirm the Deps-field rationale prose was present in that creating commit — the defect has existed since the leaves were authored. A later commit `9c42f2d` (2026-06-16, "V9l fallback path belongs in §Blocked obligations") moved the branch-unreachable fallback into `plan.md` §Blocked obligations but left the Deps-field rationale prose in both leaf files.

## Solution approach

Strike the blocking-rationale clause from the Deps field of both `V9l` and `V9l-T`, leaving the leaf-ID enumeration plus a terse pointer to `plan.md` §Blocked obligations as the authoritative blocking site. Do not restate the unblock condition in the leaf.

## Solution constraints

- The `V18c` dependency edge must remain in both Deps lists — it enforces blocking through the §Leaf format pickability predicate and is not removable prose.
- Out of scope: the stable-ID citation surface of these Deps fields is owned by F-1085; do not retarget how the unblock condition is cited.
