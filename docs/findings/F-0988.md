## Problem

`grammar.md` and `expressions.md` are non-narrative pages with registered REQ-ID prefixes (`GRAM`, `EXPR`), yet three defining obligation sites carry normative MUSTs with no co-located `**PREFIX-N.**` anchor: the field-completeness MUST under *Field rules*, the `///`-placement MUST NOT in the *`///` placement* section, and the `replace` conformance-vectors MUST in `expressions.md`. GOV-22 requires a fresh REQ-ID anchor at each defining obligation site under the page's registered prefix. Without one, the GOV-9 `#prefix-n` cross-link contract is unsatisfiable for these MUSTs and the `H5e` un-anchored-MUST gate reddens against each site on activation.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** present-since-inception (per file)
**Introducing commits:**
- `grammar.md:42` (the field-completeness MUST) and `grammar.md:157` (the `///` placement MUST NOT) — `f841b12` *spec: resolve Cluster 2 (literal sublanguage + grammar appendix)* (2026-05-04): the file's add-commit. The defect predicates were present in the file's first version; no anchor was ever coined.
- `expressions.md:87` (the `replace` conformance-vectors MUST) — `d292b46` *pi-loom spec: resolve "string.replace row missing conformance vectors"* (2026-06-04): the commit that added the conformance-vectors paragraph and its accompanying normative MUST.

**History:** GOV-22's progressive-coinage rule (the trigger that would have required a `GRAM-N` / `EXPR-N` anchor at each defining obligation site at edit time) was itself authored on `2026-06-03` (`4f588e0` *resolve "Non-narrative pages with zero coined REQ-IDs non-referenceable"*) and last edited on `2026-06-04` (`f5e89f4` *docs: enforce 100KB hard / 30KB recommended size cap on spec set*). The two `grammar.md` MUSTs therefore predate GOV-22 by roughly a month and fall under the "standing spec defects, drained progressively" carve-out GOV-22 itself names. The `expressions.md` MUST landed in `d292b46` on the same day GOV-22's last edit landed and is the cleanest case of progressive-coinage drift: a defining obligation site was added without the required REQ-ID anchor while the obligating governance rule was already in force on `main`.

## Solution approach

Coin a REQ-ID anchor at each of the three defining obligation sites under the page's already-registered prefix, allocating the numeric tail per GOV-3 (next free under the prefix; with no live `GRAM-N` / `EXPR-N` IDs these are `GRAM-1`, `GRAM-2`, and `EXPR-1`). Add `GRAM-1` to the field-completeness MUST under `grammar.md`'s *Field rules*, `GRAM-2` to the MUST NOT sentence in the *`///` placement* section, and `EXPR-1` to the `replace` reference-vectors MUST in `expressions.md`, following GOV-1's dual-form layout.

## Solution constraints

- Out of scope: the `cka-1` / `cka-3` rows in `coverage-matrix.md` — coining the anchors removes these MUSTs from the un-anchored residue without changing those code-keyed-obligation rows.
