## Finding

"Additional reader-side defence" is undefined. An implementer does not know which defences are already considered covered ("the reader behaviour pinned in this arm") and what would count as an "additional" one that the MAY permits omitting. For example: is a re-entrant guard around the slash-handler short-circuit path considered a "reader-side defence"? Is a read barrier on `drainStateTag` a "reader-side defence"?
