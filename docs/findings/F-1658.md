## Problem

`docs/spec_topics/future-considerations/model-changes-and-non-goals.md` ends with a third H2, `## Open contradictions (blocking, not deferrals)`, hosting a single bullet for the extension-instance-survival open contradiction. `future-considerations.md` declares exactly four buckets — tooling deferrals, surface extensions, model-level changes, loom 1.0 non-goals — all answering "what loom 1.0 ships without". An open contradiction is a blocking obligation whose resolution gates implementation, the opposite of a deferred feature, so the H2 is an undeclared fifth bucket on a page whose name (`model-changes-and-non-goals.md`) does not name it. The bullet is only a re-pointer; its normative content already lives at `host-prerequisites.md` clause (a) (`#degraded-state-host-prerequisites`) and on the version-bump checklist (`#bump-checklist-instance-survival`), so the misplacement both misclassifies blocking content and duplicates an existing anchor.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** single-commit
**Introducing commits:** `b8f004f` — "pi-loom spec-plan: fix F-0719 — SM-4/5/6/3b degraded-state implementation-gate contingency markers" (2026-06-19)
**History:** `git log -S "Open contradictions (blocking, not deferrals)" -- docs/spec_topics/future-considerations/` returns a single hit, `b8f004f`. `git show b8f004f -- docs/spec_topics/future-considerations/model-changes-and-non-goals.md` shows the H2, its lead paragraph, and the single Extension-instance-survival bullet appended in one diff hunk to a previously-non-existent location. The same commit edited `session-only-degraded-state.md`, `session-model-and-appendix.md`, and removed the F-0719 entry from `spec-plan-review.md` — i.e. the placement was authored as part of recording the implementation-gate contingency for F-0719 and has not been touched since. The original F-0719 fix put the index entry where Future Considerations content was being authored at the time, rather than at a dedicated open-contradictions home; the misclassification entered with that single commit.

## Solution approach

Delete the `## Open contradictions (blocking, not deferrals)` H2 (its lead paragraph and the single Extension-instance-survival bullet) from `model-changes-and-non-goals.md`, restoring the declared four-bucket structure. At the top of `host-prerequisites.md`'s `#degraded-state-host-prerequisites` block, before clause (a)'s prose, add a call-out flagging clause (a) as the spec's open contradiction at the loom 1.0 Pi-SDK pin, forward-linking the version-bump checklist item `#bump-checklist-instance-survival` and `plan.md` §Blocked obligations (`#blocked-obligations`) rather than restating their content.
