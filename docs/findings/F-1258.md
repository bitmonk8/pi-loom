## Problem

The impl leaf `V14a` carries the ERR-13 delegated live-carrier witness bullet (a code-side tool call driven to completion on the live `V14a` surface, then a downstream `?`/panic/cancel fired, leaving the completed callee's side effect in place with no compensating turn injected) and lists `V4f` in its `Deps.`. Its paired test leaf `V14a-T` has neither: the `Tests.` block stops at the five `execute()` envelope-lowering mechanics and the `Deps.` line reads ``V15a`, `V9f`, `V8a`, `V5e`, `V4d`` with no `V4f`. `V4f` defers the completed-callee-finality vector's live-surface re-assertion to `V14a` as a delegated witness, and `coverage-matrix.md` records `V14a` as an ERR-13 co-witness on that basis. Under the per-phase TDD ritual the implementer authors `V14a-T` red first, so the omitted witness test is never written and the delegated coverage ships unverified.
