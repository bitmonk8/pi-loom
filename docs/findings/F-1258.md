## Finding

The impl leaf `V14a` carries the ERR-13 delegated live-carrier witness bullet — "a code-side tool call driven to completion on the live `V14a` surface, then a downstream `?`/panic/cancel fired, leaves the completed callee's side effect in place with no compensating turn injected" — and lists `V4f` in its `Deps.`. Its paired test leaf `V14a-T` has neither: its `Tests.` block stops at the five `execute()` envelope-lowering mechanics and its `Deps.` line reads ``V15a`, `V9f`, `V8a`, `V5e`, `V4d`` with no `V4f`.

`V4f` (no-rollback guarantee) explicitly defers the completed-callee-finality vector's live-surface re-assertion to `V14a`, `V13c`, and `V15a` as delegated live-carrier witnesses, and `coverage-matrix.md` records `V14a` as an ERR-13 co-witness on exactly that basis. The plan's per-phase TDD ritual has the implementer land the `V14a-T` tests red first, then implement `V14a`. An implementer reading `V14a-T` to author the failing tests never encounters the ERR-13 witness requirement, so the failing test is never written and the delegated witness ships unverified even though the impl leaf names it.

In `docs/plan_topics/V14a-T-tool-calls.md`:

- Add an ERR-13 witness bullet to its `Tests.` block mirroring the impl leaf's requirement — the failing test for: a code-side tool call driven to completion on the live `V14a` surface, then a downstream `?`/panic/cancel fired, leaving the completed callee's side effect in place with no compensating turn injected (delegated live-carrier witness for `V4f`'s completed-callee-finality deferral).

`V4f` lives in shard-03; the addition is a cross-shard dependency. The impl leaf `V14a`, `V4f`, and `coverage-matrix.md` are the source of truth here and need no change — only the test leaf is brought back into parity with its pair.
