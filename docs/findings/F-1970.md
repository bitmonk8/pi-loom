## Finding

The pronoun "its" is ambiguous. It could refer to "the extension's ES-module declaration obligation" (yielding: the obligation and the obligation's build-time verification) or to "the extension" (yielding: the extension and the extension's build-time verification). The first reading is the intended one, but applying "its" to an abstract obligation is syntactically unusual and the second reading is grammatically equally valid.
