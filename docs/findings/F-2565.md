## Problem

`host-prerequisites.md` is the canonical inventory of unpinned Pi host-behaviour presuppositions; each family member uses the shared "treats it as a **presupposition** … in the same sense as … the *No concurrent user sessions* framing" phrasing, carries an *Authoritative source* pointer, and is gated by a lettered version-bump checklist item. The *Factory-time `cwd == project root`* presupposition (`#cwd-project-root-presupposition`) is the sole family member not homed there — it is authored inline inside PIC-13's `cwd()` bullet in `host-interfaces-services.md`, structurally a family member but shelved away from its siblings. The established precedent is the `TokenEstimator` per-message-determinism presupposition (`#token-estimator-determinism-presupposition`), whose prose lives with the family in `host-prerequisites.md` while its seam mechanics stay in PIC-16, joined by a cross-reference.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** single-commit introduction (present since the presupposition's inception)
**Introducing commit:** `4ea0544` — "pi-loom spec: resolve 'Factory-time cwd == project root premise is unpinned'" (2026-06-05)
**History:** The corpus is git-tracked. `git log -S 'cwd-project-root-presupposition'` and `git log -S 'cwd == project root'` show the presupposition block — and its version-bump checklist item (w) — were first authored in commit `4ea0544`, added (2 lines) directly into `host-interfaces-services.md` (PIC-13); `git log -S 'cwd == project root' -- host-prerequisites.md` returns nothing, confirming the block was never in `host-prerequisites.md`. The presupposition family it belongs with already existed in `host-prerequisites.md` at that point: `git log -S 'as a **presupposition**' -- host-prerequisites.md` shows family members landing earlier (e.g. `79fa3b9`, 2026-06-04, the binder-model registry-population presupposition). The mis-homing was therefore a placement choice made at authoring time against an already-established convention. Subsequent commits widened the divergence without correcting it: `803106d` (2026-06-07) added *Authoritative source* pointers to both the in-place `cwd` block and the `host-prerequisites.md` family, and later commits (e.g. `341bcd9`, 2026-06-27, the `TokenEstimator` per-message-determinism presupposition) kept adding new members to the family in `host-prerequisites.md`, none relocating the `cwd` one.

## Solution approach

Move the `#cwd-project-root-presupposition` presupposition prose (including its *Authoritative source* pointer) from PIC-13's `cwd()` bullet in `host-interfaces-services.md` into `host-prerequisites.md` alongside the host-behaviour presupposition family. Mirror the `#token-estimator-determinism-presupposition` precedent: the presupposition prose moves to `host-prerequisites.md` while the `cwd()` member contract, the production capture-once behaviour, and the no-direct-`process.cwd()` ban stay in PIC-13, joined by a cross-reference to the relocated block. Retarget version-bump checklist item (w) in `version-bump-step2.md` to the relocated block's new home.

## Solution constraints

- Preserve the `cwd-project-root-presupposition` anchor id verbatim on the relocated block so version-bump checklist item (w) and the PIC-13 `cwd()` bullet's own cross-reference continue to resolve.
