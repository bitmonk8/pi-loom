## Problem

`V18c`'s **Adds** field bundles two fundamentally different *natures* of verification under one **Ships when**: roughly nine mechanical build-time gates (SDK surface-inventory tests, the `engines.node` three-way equality gate, the `peerDependencies` pin, two pinned-constant literal-read assertions, the `session-shutdown-reason-snapshot` brand-string gate, the provider seed-field coverage gate, and the strict-capability probe), plus the **manual** per-bump editorial-review checklist items `(a)`–`(ak)` that the leaf's own prose flags as "Separately — and *not* a build-time gate". The mechanical half reddens `npm test`; the manual half is a human editorial audit and additionally carries the `V9l` clause-(a) resolution ownership. As a single landing/review/revert unit the mechanical and manual halves cannot be reverted independently, and the leaf is coarser than its seams warrant.

**Verdict:** present-since-inception
**Introducing commits:** c6a664e — pi-loom plan: build/update plan for spec.md + review (2026-06-10, Thomas Andersen); 90e95f9 — resolve "Provider seed-field Api-coverage assertion in V18c" (2026-06-11, Thomas Andersen); afaaf56 — resolve "V18c strict-capability probe gate has no asserting test" (2026-06-11, Thomas Andersen)
**History:** The leaf's first commit (c6a664e) already bundled the surface-inventory, `engines.node`, peer-dep, constants + reason-snapshot, provider seed-field, and strict-capability gates together with the editorial-review checklist items in one **Adds** field, so the bundling is present at inception. Later commits widened the bundle (90e95f9 added the provider seed-field `Api`-coverage gate, afaaf56 the strict-capability asserting arms); 8af3204 (2026-06-11) peeled the runtime-evidence acceptance gate out into `V18d` but left the static-gate + editorial-checklist bundle intact.

## Solution approach

Split `V18c` along the verification-nature boundary the leaf already names: keep the mechanical build-time gates in `V18c`, and move the manual editorial-review checklist `(a)`–`(ak)` plus its clause-(a) resolution ownership into a new paired leaf. Retarget the clause-(a) `Deps` edges on `V9l` and `V9l-T` from `V18c` to the new leaf, and update the `docs/plan.md` §Blocked obligations clause-(a) owner pointer and the §V9 interleave note's clause-(a) reference correspondingly. Move both the `Deps` retarget and the `docs/plan.md` pointers in the same change so `V9l` is never left naming a stale owner.

## Solution constraints

- Out of scope: the spec is read-only for this fix.
- `V9h`/`V9h-T` and `V18d`/`V18d-T` keep their `V18c` edges — they consume mechanical-gate outputs, not the editorial checklist; do not retarget them to the new leaf.
- Allocate the new leaf and its `-T` tests pair per the `V<N><letter>` scheme in `## Plan rules` / `conventions.md` (next free letter, no reused IDs); do not invent a non-conforming ID.
