## Problem

In `docs/plan_topics/V18c-version-bump-checklist.md`, the Adds field previews the contributor editorial-review checklist as "MUST-level for `(a)`‚Äì`(e)` and SHOULD-level-audit-but-MUST-record for `(f)`‚Äì`(al)`." The compound coinage "SHOULD-level-audit-but-MUST-record" reads naturally as "if the audit is performed, record the outcome" ‚Äî admitting a conformant path where a contributor skips an item and records nothing. The authoritative `version-bump-step2.md` and `V18c`'s own Ships-when field both require the opposite: a per-item outcome (including `not-performed` when the audit is skipped) for every item, with omitting a row being non-conformant. The defect is confined to the non-normative Adds preview; the binding statements elsewhere are unambiguous.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** multi-commit-interaction
**Introducing commits:** `f1cda48` (Thu Jun 11 2026, "resolve 'V18c mislabels editorial-review checklist as a static build-time gate'") and `828b7d3` (Tue Jun 16 2026, "resolve 'V18c editorial-checklist MUST/SHOULD blocking distinction missing from exit gate'")
**History:** `git log -S "SHOULD-level-audit-but-MUST-record"` shows the coinage first entered the Adds field in `f1cda48`, which split the editorial-review checklist out of the static-build-time-gates list. At that point the coinage was immediately followed by a disambiguating sentence ("Its done condition is not `npm test` but the per-item outcome recording the spec mandates ‚Ä¶ `pass` / `fail` / `N/A` / `not-performed` + one-line rationale ‚Ä¶"), which pinned reading (B). `git log -S "Its done condition is not"` shows that disambiguating sentence was removed in `828b7d3`, which relocated the MUST/SHOULD blocking distinction into Ships-when and trimmed the Adds preview ‚Äî leaving the compound coinage standing alone in Adds with no in-field clarification. The current ambiguity is the product of the second commit stripping the disambiguator the first commit had paired with the coinage.

## Solution approach

Rewrite the trailing editorial-review-checklist clause in `V18c`'s Adds field so it separates the audit level from the recording obligation, mirroring `version-bump-step2.md`'s recording rule. Keep the MUST-level statement for `(a)`‚Äì`(e)`, and for `(f)`‚Äì`(al)` make explicit that the audit is SHOULD-level but the per-item outcome recording is mandatory ‚Äî a recorded `not-performed` (with a one-line rationale) is required when an audit is skipped, and omitting a row is non-conformant.

## Solution constraints

- Out of scope: `version-bump-step2.md` and `V18c`'s Ships-when field, which already state the obligation unambiguously ‚Äî confine the fix to the Adds field.
- Keep the leaf's existing `(f)`‚Äì`(al)` item range; do not import the spec's wider item range.
