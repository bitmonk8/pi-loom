"Link the new leaf into the **appropriate section** below" gives no decision rule; an author of a cross-slice leaf or one straddling slice groups cannot determine which section to link under.

**Suggested fix:** Replace with a concrete rule keyed on leaf-ID prefix (H→Horizontal, M→MVP, V→matching vertical slice group).
