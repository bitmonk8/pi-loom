## Problem

`V9i` bundles the session-local `subscribe`-based completion detection (`PIC-42`) and the `agent_end` query-result extraction (`PIC-43`) with the spawn/lifecycle/guard/abort core. These completion-detection obligations (terminal-event selection, cancellation/transport short-circuits, trailing-text concatenation) form a separable sub-surface. Carrying all of `V9i`'s obligations in one leaf produces an oversized commit that exceeds the atomicity threshold and inflates the diff a reviewer must hold in scope.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** multi-commit-interaction
**Introducing commits:** `7a3b907` (2026-06-10, "resolve \"subagent parallel-initiation MUST has no closing leaf\""); `e690bb0` (2026-06-23, "fix F-1226 … mint PIC-23 + coverage-matrix/V9i cascade"); `3253d16` (2026-06-25, "fix F-1346 — coin PIC-40..43 on subagent.md + map to V9i coverage")
**History:** At creation (`c6a664e`, 2026-06-10) `V9i` carried a coherent single-session lifecycle (`PIC-9`, the isolation contract, model-guard / dispose-failure diagnostics, ≈3 obligations). The breadth accreted across later fixes: `7a3b907` (same day) added the `PIC-22` parallel-spawn path; `e690bb0` added `PIC-23`; `3253d16` coined `PIC-40`–`PIC-43` and mapped them onto the `V9i` coverage row. An earlier split (`6d7661d`) removed the swallowing-handler concern to `V9o` but did not re-evaluate the remaining surface, so the cumulative additions pushed the leaf past the atomicity threshold.

## Solution approach

Extract the `PIC-42` and `PIC-43` completion-detection obligations from `V9i` into a new paired implementation/tests leaf that lists `V9i` in its `Deps`. Move the `PIC-42`/`PIC-43` Tests bullets out of `V9i` and `V9i-T`, strike `PIC-42`/`PIC-43` from the `V9i.Adds` anchoring sentence and `PIC` enumeration, and retarget the `PIC-42`/`PIC-43` mappings in `coverage-matrix.md` to the new leaf. Link the new implementation leaf into the V9 leaf listing in `docs/plan.md`.
