## Problem

`docs/spec_topics/pi-integration-contract/host-interfaces-services.md` is the host-facing DI-seam inventory (the PIC-10 → PIC-20 sequence, each entry pinning one constructor-injected interface). Three blocks appended after PIC-20 are not DI seams: the `**Discovery API.**` registration-flow summary, the `loom 1.0 seam — Pi-owned subagents collision source set` blockquote (the normative home of the `SlashCommandSource` collision-candidate set), and the `**No Pi-owned discovery path enumerates `.loom` or `.warp`.**` namespace-exclusivity guarantee. Readers scanning the natural subject anchors (discovery, registration, namespace exclusivity) do not find these where they belong, and the collision-source-set blockquote is cross-referenced from three sibling files by a `host-interfaces-services.md#…` URL even though its subject — the `session_start` collision-check membership test — lives in `registration-steps.md`.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** multi-commit-interaction
**Introducing commits:** `b987cb4` (2026-05-04 — *resources_discover is a
contribution event, not an inbound path feed*) introduced the **Discovery

introduced the **V1 seam — Pi-owned subagents collision source set**
blockquote. All three landed in the then-monolithic
`spec_topics/pi-integration-contract.md`, where they sat in the trailing
*Discovery API* / *Pi-version-bump* region of a 576 KB file. The file split
commit `f5e89f4` (2026-06-04 — *enforce 100KB hard / 30KB recommended size
cap on spec set*) routed all three paragraphs verbatim into the new
`host-interfaces-services.md` child page on the mechanical "trailing block of
the parent file goes to the file that absorbed the last preceding section"
heuristic the splitter used, even though none of them is about a DI seam.
**History:** The misplacement is the interaction between three
independently-motivated additions and the size-cap split: each paragraph was
appended without a placement audit (each commit message confirms it
addresses a different review finding entirely), and the splitter then
cemented the trailing position without re-classifying by subject.

## Solution approach

Move each of the three trailing blocks out of `host-interfaces-services.md` to the file whose subject it matches. Move the `**Discovery API.**` paragraph into `registration-steps.md` as the framing paragraph for the registration flow. Move the `loom 1.0 seam — Pi-owned subagents collision source set` blockquote into `registration-steps.md` adjacent to step 3 (the step that consults `pi.getCommands()` and applies the collision-source-set membership test). Move the `**No Pi-owned discovery path enumerates `.loom` or `.warp`.**` paragraph into `discovery/discovery-sources.md` near the `#file-extension-namespace` section. Repoint the three external cross-references currently targeting `host-interfaces-services.md#v1-seam-pi-owned-subagents-collision-source-set` (`registration-steps.md` step 3, `host-prerequisites.md` degraded-state branch, `future-considerations/surface-extensions.md`) to the blockquote's new site.
