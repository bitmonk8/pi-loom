## Problem

`capability-probe.md` §"On failure: refusal and diagnostic" bullet (ii) routes the `loom/load/host-incompatible` diagnostic through the **System notes** fallback chain `sendSystemNote → ctx.ui.notify → console.error`, but the middle rung names a `ctx` that does not exist at factory-probe time. The factory entry point is `default function (pi: ExtensionAPI)`, and `ExtensionAPI` has no `ui` member; `ctx.ui` exists only on the per-handler `ExtensionContext` Pi supplies to slash-command and `pi.on(...)` callbacks, the first of which is the step-3 `session_start` handler. The spec does not say what the factory does instead of the unreachable rung. The same hazard recurs in `extension-bootstrap-and-per-loom.md`: its `pi.registerMessageRenderer` failure bullet says System notes "permanently degrade to the `ctx.ui.notify` arm", and its trailing `loom/load/extension-bootstrap-failed` routing paragraph reuses the same three-rung chain for factory-time emissions, both presupposing a `ctx` the factory does not have.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** single-commit
**Introducing commits:** 126ff8a — pi-loom spec: Step 0 capability probe rewrite + V1.0 deferrals + Choice B (2026-05-06, Thomas Andersen)
**History:** The `loom-system-note` runtime fallback chain (`pi.sendMessage` → transient toast → `console.error`) was introduced in `fae85d3` (2026-05-04), where it lived in the per-handler runtime "System notes" paragraph and the `ctx` it cited was the slash-command handler's `ExtensionCommandContext`. Commit `126ff8a` re-used the same chain verbatim for the new factory-emitted `loom/load/host-incompatible` diagnostic — adding the sentence "Routed through the **System notes** fallback chain below (`sendSystemNote` → `ctx.ui.notify` → `console.error`), since the renderer itself may be the missing capability" to both the capability-probe section and the `loom/load/host-incompatible` registry row — without re-examining that the new caller (the factory) has no `ctx`. The split into `capability-probe.md` (commit `81bf52a`, 2026-06-21) carried the defect verbatim. The renderer-failure-degrade callsite in `extension-bootstrap-and-per-loom.md` inherits the same caller-context mismatch.

## Solution approach

Clarify in `capability-probe.md` §"On failure: refusal and diagnostic" bullet (ii) that no `ctx` exists at factory-probe time, so the factory-time fallback chain skips the `ctx.ui.notify` rung and degrades to `sendSystemNote → console.error`. Mirror the same factory-time-chain caveat at `extension-bootstrap-and-per-loom.md`'s trailing `loom/load/extension-bootstrap-failed` routing paragraph, whose factory-time emissions share the hazard. In that file's `pi.registerMessageRenderer` failure bullet, clarify that the `ctx` whose `ctx.ui.notify` arm the degradation names is the per-handler `ExtensionContext` supplied at later runtime emissions, so the degradation governs post-factory emissions only.
