## Finding

"Clause" is used without defining the unit of granularity. It could mean a single sentence, a named anchor-tagged block, a numbered sub-section, or any arbitrarily sized block of prose. Two maintainers adding content to this file could reach incompatible conclusions about whether a new paragraph with no named anchor is one clause or several, affecting whether it inherits the gated default.
