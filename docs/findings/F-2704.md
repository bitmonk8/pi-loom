## Problem

The real-host smoke gate's pass/fail criterion (d) in `real-host-smoke-gate.md` requires that the four session-double fidelity-contract axes "hold," but only axes (i) (streamed-token order relative to `ctx.waitForIdle()` resolution) and (ii) (one prompt-mode turn per streamed response) carry an inline observable a live-host scorer can check. Axes (iii) "the `pi.on` cancel-forward subscription" and (iv) "cancellation propagation" are bare noun phrases naming a behaviour but stating no observable that must be true for the axis to hold. A contributor scoring (d) against a live Pi host cannot determine what to observe: (iii) has no observable text anywhere, and (iv) restates a concept criterion (c) already scores without saying whether it adds anything or merely points back at (c). Two scorers could diverge — one passing (iii) vacuously — letting a genuine cancel-forward divergence pass the gate unflagged.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** present-since-inception
**Introducing commits:** `eeb0014` (2026-06-10) authored the `H4a` session-double fidelity contract enumerating the four axes; the criterion was relocated into `real-host-smoke-gate.md` criterion (d) by `fd22a3d` (2026-06-17).
**History:** At `eeb0014` the four-axis enumeration was first authored with axes (iii) "the `pi.on` cancel-forward subscription" and (iv) "cancellation propagation" already as bare noun phrases, while axes (i)/(ii) carried observables. The axis-(ii) ambiguity was separately repaired at `dd875e1` (2026-06-12) by adding the single-turn invariant, but (iii)/(iv) were left bare. The criterion was relocated into `real-host-smoke-gate.md` criterion (d) at `fd22a3d`, and `c768005` (2026-06-25) added criterion (c) without touching the bare axes. No commit regressed a previously-correct state — axes (iii)/(iv) have lacked an inline observable since first authored.

## Solution approach

In `real-host-smoke-gate.md` criterion (d), give axes (iii) and (iv) inline observables parallel to the ones on (i) and (ii), so each axis names what must be true on a live host. For axis (iii), state the observable that a host-side cancel signal delivered through the `pi.on` subscription forwards into loom-side cancellation, drawing on `conversation-drive.md` PIC-18 and `cancellation.md` "Forwarding into `loomAbort`". For axis (iv), make its relationship to criterion (c)'s cancellation observable explicit — either point (iv) at criterion (c) or restate the live observable per `cancellation.md` CNCL-4 — so it is no longer a bare phrase.

## Solution constraints

- The spec is read-only: PIC-18, "Forwarding into `loomAbort`", and CNCL-4 are source references for the observable text, not edit targets.
- Out of scope: `H4a`'s fidelity-contract authoring and its self-check items — confine the edit to `real-host-smoke-gate.md` criterion (d).
