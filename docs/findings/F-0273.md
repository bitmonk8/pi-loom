## Problem

`V9c` and its paired test leaf `V9c-T` cover three independently-testable concern clusters in a single TDD pair: query-drive mechanics, active-set gating (`PIC-2` / `PIC-17` / `PIC-18`), and transport-error mapping (four error-detection sub-cases, including the three-way cancellation-precedence ordering). The leaf carries seven `Tests` bullets across these clusters, yet its `Ships when` gates only two observables — the active-set snapshot/restore and one `stopReason:"error"` turn — so the absent-`errorMessage` fallback, the synchronous-throw secondary mapping, and the cancellation-precedence sub-case can ship under-observed. The error-mapping cluster consumes the drive cluster's probe result and `Ok(string)` extraction points but is otherwise an independent surface that can be authored and gated on its own, downstream of the drive surface.

**History:** `V9c` was created at c6a664e already bundling two clusters — query-drive mechanics and active-set gating — with three `Tests` bullets (`PIC-17`, `PIC-18`, trailing-turn extraction). Commit 10cbf96 then appended the entire transport-error-mapping cluster (four error-detection bullets, including the three-way cancellation-precedence ordering) while resolving a separate "asserted by no test" finding, tipping the leaf past the atomicity threshold. The over-size therefore arises from the interaction of the inception bundle and the later error-mapping addition, neither of which was sized against the combined whole.

## Solution approach

Split the `V9c`/`V9c-T` pair along the error-mapping seam into two paired leaves (drive+gating, error-mapping) following the TDD-pairing convention, with the error-mapping impl leaf naming the drive impl leaf in its `Deps`. Move the `PIC-2` / `PIC-17` / `PIC-18` and trailing-turn `Ok(string)` extraction tests to the drive pair and the four error-detection tests to the error-mapping pair, keeping the cancellation short-circuit precedence assertion in the error-mapping pair and exposing the probe-result and `Ok(string)` extraction points as the drive leaf's seam. Retarget the `coverage-matrix.md` `PIC-2` / `PIC-17` / `PIC-18` and `conversation-drive.md` §`untyped-query-ok-extraction` rows to the drive impl leaf and the §`prompt-mode-error-detection` row to the error-mapping impl leaf, retarget the `V9c` `Deps` in `V12a` / `V13c` / `V14b` (and their `-T` pairs) to the drive impl leaf, retarget `V13f` / `V13f-T` per the dependent's actual reliance on the synthesised `cancelled`/`transport` `QueryError`, and replace the `V9c` bullet in `docs/plan.md`'s V9 listing with bullets for the two new pairs.
