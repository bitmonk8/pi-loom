## Problem

`H6a` is the leaf that activates `H5c`'s live-corpus footing — `H5c` declares its `no-broad-catch` allow-list arm flips to the live `src/**` allow-list "by the single `H6a` gate-machinery flip." But `H6a`'s `Deps` are `H5b, H7a`, and neither path reaches `H5c`: `H5c` closes no new spec REQ-ID, so it is not a member of `H5b`'s coverage-producing set; `H7a` does not name it; and `H5c`'s own `Deps` are just `H5a`. Nothing in `H6a`'s transitive closure forces `H5c` to land before the flip, so a scheduler may land `H5c` after `H6a`. In that ordering the single flip fires with no `H5c` arm present, and the broad-catch live allow-list gate ships permanently stuck in its seeded-fixture footing.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** single-commit-regression
**Introducing commit:** `cb8737a` (2026-06-12) — "pi-loom plan: resolve \"Systemic leaf over-bundling across the plan corpus\""
**History:** Before `cb8737a` the `no-broad-catch` allow-list reconciliation arm lived inside `H5a`, which is reachable from `H6a` through `H5b`'s `Deps`. `cb8737a` split that arm out into the new `H5c` leaf and authored its "single `H6a` gate-machinery flip" live-corpus-binding prose, but touched only `H5a` and the new `H5c` file — it did not add `H5c` to `H6a`'s (or `H5b`'s) `Deps`. `H6a`'s `Deps` were `H5b, H7a` both immediately before and immediately after the commit (verified via `git show cb8737a~1:` and `git show cb8737a:`). The split therefore severed the dependency edge that previously reached the arm and created the undeclared-dependency gap.

## Solution approach

Add `H5c` to `H6a`'s `Deps` field in `docs/plan_topics/H6a-live-corpus-activation.md`. Pin `H5c` directly on `H6a`, mirroring the existing `H7a` treatment, since `H5c` closes no new spec REQ-ID and so cannot be inherited transitively through `H5b`'s coverage-producing set. With the edge in place the pickability predicate forces `H5c` to land before the flip that reconfigures it.

## Solution constraints

- Out of scope: the broader `H6a` `Deps` decision-log parenthetical rewrite or deletion is owned by F-0488; this fix adds only the `H5c` edge.
- If the `Deps` parenthetical is retained, its "identical set of leaves by construction" claim must be reconciled with `H5c` not being a member of `H5b`'s coverage-producing set.
