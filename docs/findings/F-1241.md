## Finding

`H5a`'s `Adds.` establishes the closing gate's **warn-only live-corpus mode** and defines its coverage as "the same three live-corpus reconciliation surfaces — the live spec REQ-ID set and the live numbered-REQ-ID citing-test surface this leaf owns, and the live `spec_topics/**` normative-MUST surface the `H5e` arm contributes". That enumeration omits the per-facet citing-test surface entirely, even though the same `Adds.` paragraph credits `H5f` with owning "the per-facet citing-test arm — which derives each multi-leaf `coverage-matrix.md` row's facet partition and reconciles each facet leaf against its facet-naming citing test".

`H5b` (the warn-only canary that consumes this mode) and `H5f` both require the per-facet surface to be part of the warn-only mode. `H5b`'s `Tests.` and `Ships when.` assert the returned warn-only findings collection carries an entry for **four** gap kinds — an unmapped executable REQ-ID, a coverage-matrix-mapped numbered REQ-ID with no citing test, **a multi-leaf coverage-matrix-row facet whose closing leaf carries no facet-naming citing test**, and an un-enumerated un-anchored MUST. `H5f`'s `Adds.` states the per-facet surface "is the surface `H5b`'s warn-only canary pre-flights". So the leaf that *defines* the warn-only mode lists three surfaces while the two leaves that *consume and contribute to* it expect four.

An implementer building `H5a`'s warn-only mode from `H5a`'s text alone wires only the three enumerated surfaces and omits the `H5f` per-facet arm. `H5b`'s seeded per-facet-gap test then asserts a per-facet-tagged entry the collection never produces, so `H5b` cannot ship green.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** single-commit
**Introducing commits:** 26a8794 — pi-loom plan: resolve "split coverage-matrix rows can ship a facet untested" (2026-06-18, Thomas Andersen)
**History:** Commit 26a8794 introduced `H5f` (the per-facet citing-test arm) and propagated the new fourth gap kind into `H5b`'s Tests, Ships-when, and Deps, but amended `H5a`'s `Adds.` only to credit `H5f`'s ownership of the arm — it left `H5a`'s warn-only-mode enumeration at "three live-corpus reconciliation surfaces" without adding the per-facet surface. Commit 7b88876 later rewrote that same warn-only sentence for an unrelated reporting-contract fix (F-0922) and carried the omission forward unchanged.

## Solution Space

**Shape:** single

### Recommendation

In `docs/plan_topics/H5a-closing-gate-automation.md`, the `Adds.` warn-only-live-corpus-mode sentence must name the `H5f` per-facet citing-test surface alongside the spec-REQ-ID, numbered-REQ-ID-citing-test, and `H5e` normative-MUST surfaces it already enumerates, and correct the surface count so the enumeration matches the four gap kinds `H5b`'s `Tests.`/`Ships when.` require (unmapped executable REQ-ID; coverage-matrix-mapped numbered REQ-ID with no citing test; multi-leaf coverage-matrix-row facet whose closing leaf carries no facet-naming citing test; un-enumerated un-anchored MUST). Credit the per-facet arm in the same form already used for the `H5e` arm ("the live `spec_topics/**` normative-MUST surface the `H5e` arm contributes") — i.e. an equivalent clause naming the per-facet citing-test surface the `H5f` arm contributes. The spec is read-only for this fix; no edit outside `H5a` is required — `H5b`, `H5f`, and `H6a` already enumerate the per-facet surface and its gap kind correctly.

## Consequence

**Severity:** correctness

Two reasonable implementers diverge on whether the warn-only findings collection includes per-facet gaps: one reads `H5a`'s "three surfaces" and wires only the spec-REQ-ID, numbered-citing-test, and normative-MUST arms; the other reads `H5b`/`H5f` and adds the per-facet arm. The first build cannot satisfy `H5b`'s seeded per-facet-gap test, so `H5b` cannot ship green against its own `Ships when.` condition.
