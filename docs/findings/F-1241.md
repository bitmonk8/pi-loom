## Problem

`H5a`'s `Adds.` defines the closing gate's **warn-only live-corpus mode** as covering "the same three live-corpus reconciliation surfaces" — the live spec REQ-ID set, the live numbered-REQ-ID citing-test surface, and the live `spec_topics/**` normative-MUST surface the `H5e` arm contributes. That enumeration omits the per-facet citing-test surface that `H5f` owns, even though the same paragraph credits `H5f` with that arm. `H5b`'s `Tests.`/`Ships when.` and `H5f`'s `Adds.` both require the warn-only findings collection to carry a fourth gap kind — a multi-leaf coverage-matrix-row facet whose closing leaf carries no facet-naming citing test. An implementer wiring `H5a`'s warn-only mode from its text alone omits the `H5f` per-facet arm, so `H5b`'s seeded per-facet-gap test asserts an entry the collection never produces and `H5b` cannot ship green.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** single-commit
**Introducing commits:** 26a8794 — pi-loom plan: resolve "split coverage-matrix rows can ship a facet untested" (2026-06-18, Thomas Andersen)
**History:** Commit 26a8794 introduced `H5f` (the per-facet citing-test arm) and propagated the new fourth gap kind into `H5b`'s Tests, Ships-when, and Deps, but amended `H5a`'s `Adds.` only to credit `H5f`'s ownership of the arm — it left `H5a`'s warn-only-mode enumeration at "three live-corpus reconciliation surfaces" without adding the per-facet surface. Commit 7b88876 later rewrote that same warn-only sentence for an unrelated reporting-contract fix (F-0922) and carried the omission forward unchanged.

## Solution approach

In `docs/plan_topics/H5a-closing-gate-automation.md`, rewrite the `Adds.` warn-only-live-corpus-mode enumeration to name the `H5f` per-facet citing-test surface alongside the three surfaces it already lists, and correct the surface count so the enumeration matches the four gap kinds `H5b`'s `Tests.`/`Ships when.` require. Credit the per-facet arm in the same form `H5a` already uses for the `H5e` arm — a clause naming the per-facet citing-test surface the `H5f` arm contributes.

## Solution constraints

- Out of scope: `H5b`, `H5f`, and `H6a` already enumerate the per-facet surface and its gap kind correctly — confine the edit to `H5a`.
- The spec is read-only for this fix.
