## Problem

The (P-a)/(P-b-1)/(P-c-1) "full five-sub-step teardown still runs" fixture obligations in `patch-skew-degradation.md` are stale relative to the closed `details.call` label set owned by the **Per-step isolation** paragraph of `session-shutdown-semantics.md`. They assert that "each of the **eight** call sites" was invoked and that "**Sub-steps 2 and 3** carry no `details.call` label", but the authoritative set now has **eleven** members — including sub-step 3's three bounded-await `Clock` calls (`Clock.now()`, `Clock.setTimeout(awaitCap)`, `Clock.clearTimeout(awaitCap)`), which carry `details.call` labels. `session-only-degraded-state.md` already enumerates `3 × 11 = 33` fixtures over the eleven sites, so the two pages cannot both be satisfied: an implementer following `patch-skew-degradation.md` spies eight sites and never witnesses sub-step-3 teardown emission.

**History:** Commit a7d2a80 introduced the "eight call sites" count and the "Sub-steps 2 and 3 carry no `details.call` label" phrasing into `patch-skew-degradation.md`; at that time the closed set genuinely had eight labels (sub-step 1: 2, sub-step 4: 3, sub-step 5: 3) and sub-step 3 carried only the unlabelled `Promise.allSettled` await, so both claims were correct. Commit 4e531dc (F-2231) then brought sub-step 3's three `Clock` calls under teardown throw-isolation — adding three labels to the closed set in `session-shutdown-semantics.md` (now eleven) and updating `session-only-degraded-state.md`'s (b) count to `3 × 11 = 33` — but its inbound-reference sweep did not touch `patch-skew-degradation.md`, leaving the stale eight-count and the sub-step-3 mis-classification behind.

## Solution approach

In `patch-skew-degradation.md`, rewrite the (P-a)/(P-b-1)/(P-c-1) fixture obligations to align with the eleven-member closed `details.call` set: correct the stale "eight call sites" count and correct the follow-on sentence so that only sub-step 2 and sub-step 3's per-entry `Promise.allSettled` await over `disposeBarrier`s are label-free and witnessed separately. Make clear that sub-step 3's three bounded-await `Clock` calls carry `details.call` labels and belong to the spied closed set, not the separately-witnessed bucket.

## Solution constraints

- Out of scope: `session-only-degraded-state.md`, whose `3 × 11 = 33` count and eleven-site co-emission list already match the authoritative set.
- The closed `details.call` label set is owned by the **Per-step isolation** paragraph of `session-shutdown-semantics.md`; do not redefine the set here.
