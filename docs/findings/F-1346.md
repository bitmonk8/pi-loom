## Problem

`subagent.md` owns `PIC-2`, `PIC-9`, `PIC-22`, and `PIC-23`, but several normative MUST/MUST-NOT obligations on the page carry no co-located `PIC-N` anchor: the `Pre-spawn model guard`'s `MUST NOT` call `createAgentSession` when the resolved `model` is `undefined`; the `MUST NOT` pass a `signal` field to `createAgentSession`; the `MUST` use `session.subscribe` rather than the global `pi.on("agent_end", …)`; the subagent-mode `agent_end` extraction MUSTs (cancellation short-circuit, transport-failure short-circuit, trailing-assistant-text concatenation); and the `dispose()`-finally-block obligation. Without a `PIC-N` anchor these sites are uncitable: GOV-9's `#prefix-n` cross-link contract is unsatisfiable, per-facet citing tests cannot bind a stable handle, and the `coverage-matrix.md` closure gate reddens against every un-anchored PIC MUST.
