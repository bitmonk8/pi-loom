## Problem

The *Seam minimality (normative)* clause in `session-only-degraded-state.md` (under *Predicate split*) and PIC-30 in `drain-state-contract.md` fence behaviour with prohibitions phrased against internal state representation rather than observable behaviour. Several enumerated prohibitions have no observable correlate: a `branchTakenWasClosedSet` local computed from `capturedEventReason`, a per-sub-step success roll-up (vacuous because **Per-step isolation** already guarantees sub-step 4 returns), or a third internal drain-state field that does not change `readDrainState`'s three-arm return each produce byte-identical observable behaviour yet are forbidden by the literal text, so a conformant runtime carrying a redundant intermediate variable is mislabelled non-conformant. The observable contract these clauses actually defend is narrower and correct ŌĆö the two predicate forms, the captured-reason discriminator, and the closed three-arm `readDrainState` dispatch ŌĆö and must survive any reframing. The defect is purely the framing.

## Solution approach

Rewrite both clauses as invariants on observable values and the closed dispatch surface, and demote the representation-only prohibitions to non-normative implementation notes. In `session-only-degraded-state.md`'s *Seam minimality (normative)* clause, keep normative the diagnostic-emission predicate `pinnedConstantReadOk && capturedEventReason Ōłł {"new","resume","fork"}`, the value-independent tag-transition predicate `capturedEventReason Ōłē {"quit","reload"}`, and the captured-reason discriminator, and demote the "no `branchTakenWasClosedSet` / no per-sub-step roll-up / no extra seam" enumeration to a note. In PIC-30 (`#pic-30`), keep normative the closed three-arm `readDrainState` enumeration (PIC-29, `#pic-29`) and the no-dispatch-suppression requirement on the post-handler `(drained: false, tag: undefined)` tuple, demoting the "no third boolean field" enumeration to a note. Realign the on-page acceptance-criteria prose for the "no seam introduced" / "no third boolean field" cases so they assert the observable predicate values and three-arm dispatch behaviour.

## Solution constraints

- Preserve as normative the captured-reason-vs-fresh-`event.reason`-reread requirement and the closed-three-arm / no-dispatch-suppression requirement on the `(drained: false, tag: undefined)` tuple ŌĆö these are the observable core, not representation prohibitions, and dropping them re-opens the divergences this branch closes.
