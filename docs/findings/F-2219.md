## Problem

`runtime-event-channel.md` pins a dense set of best-effort delivery obligations for the `loom-system-note` fallback chain — the three-step ordering, the `display: false` / `content: ""` step-1 skip, the payload-sourcing rule, the `Clock.wallNow()`-throw no-re-invocation rule, and PIC-54's terminal-`console.error` swallow that must hold across all three reach-paths — but states them only as inline prose. Unlike its sibling pages `diagnostic-emission-isolation.md` and `unknown-reason-rule.md`, it carries no `## Acceptance criteria` sub-section. The two hardest-to-verify obligations — the `Clock.wallNow()`-throw branch and PIC-54's reach-path independence — are exactly the ones a conformance author is most likely to leave untested, and the paired plan leaves (`V7d` / `V7d-T`) carry neither as an independent fixture.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** present-since-inception
**Introducing commits:** 11b9199 — pi-loom spec: resolve "pi.sendMessage failure leaves system notes silently dropped" (2026-05-04, Thomas Andersen); 032dda7 — pi-loom spec: resolve "always-log construction + ctx.ui.notify fallback unpinned" (2026-06-07, Thomas Andersen); cfb3943 — pi-loom spec-plan: fix F-1395 — coin PIC-54 wrapping terminal console.error at fallback-chain definition site (2026-06-25, Thomas Andersen)
**History:** The `sendSystemNote` fallback chain and its `loom/runtime/system-note-delivery-failed` step were first authored at 11b9199 with no `## Acceptance criteria` sub-section (`git log -S "Acceptance"` over the page returns no commit — the section has never existed). 032dda7 then strengthened the `Clock.wallNow()` re-invocation prohibition and cfb3943 coined PIC-54's reach-path-independent swallow, each adding a hard, easy-to-leave-untested obligation to the same chain without adding acceptance criteria; the testability gap was present from the chain's first commit and widened by the later two. (The page itself was carved out of the monolithic `pi-integration-contract.md` at f5e89f4 by the size-cap split, which moved the already-acceptance-criteria-less content verbatim.)

## Solution approach

Add a `## Acceptance criteria` sub-section to `runtime-event-channel.md`, matching the form used by the sibling `diagnostic-emission-isolation.md` and `unknown-reason-rule.md` pages, enumerating the fallback-chain obligations as conformance fixtures. Cover at minimum the `Clock.wallNow()`-throw branch (no re-invocation, occurrence accounted for by the step-2 diagnostic) and PIC-54's terminal-`console.error` swallow asserted independently for each of the three reach-paths: the per-invocation `finally` clean-cancel path, the extension-bootstrap SDK-failure path, and the capability-probe `host-incompatible` path.
