## Problem

The Self-failure paragraph in `capability-probe.md` specifies `details.cause = <error.message>`, a naked `.message` read. That paragraph exists to trap *unforeseen* throws inside the probe ÔÇö and it enumerates awkward throw shapes by name (hostile getters, `Proxy`, `null` prototype, malformed `package.json`) ÔÇö but `throw <any value>` is legal JavaScript and need not be `Error`-shaped. A non-`Error` throw yields `undefined`, defeating the "always a string" diagnostics contract; a hostile-getter `Proxy` whose `.message` access throws yields a synchronous `TypeError` inside the catch that escapes the probe and violates PIC-6 ("the factory MUST NOT throw"). Sibling caught-throw sites already route through the `#underlying-error-coercion` recipe in `placeholder-rendering-b.md` ┬º6; `details.cause` does not.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** multi-commit-interaction

**Introducing commits:**
- `126ff8a` (2026-05-06) ÔÇö Step 0 capability probe rewrite. Introduced the `"probe-failed"` `kind` and authored the Self-failure paragraph with `details.cause = <error.message>` as a naked `Error.message` read; the surrounding parenthetical already enumerated the awkward throw shapes (hostile getter, `Proxy`, `null` prototype, malformed `package.json`) the read does not cover.
- `f5e89f4` (2026-06-04) ÔÇö corpus split. Moved the Self-failure paragraph from the monolithic `pi-integration-contract.md` into the new `pi-integration-contract/capability-probe.md` topic file; the `details.cause = <error.message>` text was carried verbatim.
- `bf85092` (2026-06-06) ÔÇö non-Error-throw coercion. Authored the [`#underlying-error-coercion`](../docs/spec_topics/diagnostics/placeholder-rendering-b.md#underlying-error-coercion) recipe in `placeholder-rendering-b.md` ┬º6, listed the producer sites it covers (a diagnostic `details.error`, a `hint`/`message` carrying a caught throw's message, `CodeToolError.message`, plus the ┬º8 `<error>` placeholder on `loom/load/extension-bootstrap-failed` and `loom/runtime/active-set-restore-failed`), but did **not** extend coverage to `details.cause` on `loom/load/host-incompatible` and did not touch the Self-failure paragraph.

**History:** The defect was authored at `126ff8a` as an isolated paragraph and would have been a clean single-commit miss. The `bf85092` rewrite made the corpus's overall coercion story sound for every sibling caught-throw site and named those sites explicitly, but stopped short of `details.cause` ÔÇö turning what was a uniform gap into an asymmetric one where the coercion contract exists, names its peers, and skips this one. The durable fix is the missing producer-site link between `bf85092`'s recipe and `126ff8a`'s payload rule, not a revert of either.

## Solution approach

Rewrite the `details.cause = <error.message>` clause in the Self-failure paragraph of `capability-probe.md` to obtain the underlying string via the existing [`#underlying-error-coercion`](../diagnostics/placeholder-rendering-b.md#underlying-error-coercion) recipe in `placeholder-rendering-b.md` ┬º6, retaining the existing "string, not the `Error` object ÔÇö diagnostics are serialisable" framing. Add `details.cause` on `loom/load/host-incompatible` to that recipe's enumerated producer sites. Rewrite the `"probe-failed"` clause in `code-registry-load.md`'s `loom/load/host-incompatible` row so it cites the coercion recipe rather than a naked `Error.message` read.
