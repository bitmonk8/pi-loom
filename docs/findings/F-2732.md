## Problem

`V11b` couples two independently shippable and testable concerns: a parse-time frontmatter field contract (the `bind_context` (`none`|`session`) field and its `loom/parse/bind-context-session-on-subagent` rejection on subagent-mode looms) and a runtime transcript-rendering surface (the `BNDR-7a–7i` byte-exact renderer, `BNDR-8` ordering, and the `BNDR-9` `custom-type-unsafe` render-time abort). The two fail red against unrelated fixtures — frontmatter-parse fixtures versus render vectors — so holding them in one TDD pass implicates unrelated mechanisms in a single leaf, inflating its verification surface and per-leaf review diff.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** present-since-inception
**Introducing commits:** c6a664e (`pi-loom plan: build/update plan for spec.md + review`)
**History:** `V11b`'s first committed form (c6a664e) bundled the `bind_context` field, the truncation walk, the transcript renderer, and the `BNDR-9` custom-type-unsafe path in one leaf. A later refactor (d0c127f, "resolve 'V11b bundles transcript renderer with truncation walk'") extracted the runtime truncation walk into `V11i` but left the parse-time `bind_context` field contract bundled with the runtime transcript-rendering surface. Subsequent commits (ef76896, fe694dd, 3763100, 31c8cf2, f41af5d) refined the `BNDR-9` wording, the diagnostic-code citation, and the truncation-boundary test, but none touched this coupling. The defect has been present since the plan corpus was first built, narrowed once but never fully separated.

## Solution approach

Split the parse-time `bind_context` field contract out of `V11b` into a new paired V11-phase leaf (bare + `-T`): the `bind_context` (`none`|`session`) field, the `loom/parse/bind-context-session-on-subagent` subagent-rejection rule, and its matching parse test. Retain the runtime transcript-rendering surface — the `BNDR-7a–7i` renderer, `BNDR-8` ordering, and the `BNDR-9` `custom-type-unsafe` render-time abort — on `V11b`, keeping the unsafe-`customType` guard with the renderer it protects. Retitle `V11b` to drop the bind-context concern, link the new leaf into the §Vertical slices V11 listing in `docs/plan.md`, and give it the Deps its parse-time rule needs. If the `bind-context-session-on-subagent` parse rule is coverage-matrix-mapped, repoint that row in `docs/plan_topics/coverage-matrix.md` to the new leaf.

## Solution constraints

- Out of scope: the runtime session-context truncation walk owned by `V11i`, and the `BNDR-7, BNDR-8, BNDR-9` renderer coverage-matrix row, which stays on `V11b`.
- The new leaf must follow the `V<N><letter>` paired-leaf scheme (next free letter in the V11 phase, no retired-ID reuse) per `## Plan rules`, and keep `docs/plan_topics/conventions.md` and `docs/plan_topics/coverage-matrix.md` consistent with the split.
