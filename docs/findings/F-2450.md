## Problem

The *Post-probe SDK-shape drift* section (`#post-probe-sdk-shape-drift` in `capability-probe.md`) embeds a loom-1.0 deferral inside normative current-build prose: it states that the `loom/runtime/internal-error` host-drift-vs-loom-defect `details.kind` discriminator is an additive registry-minor change deferred past loom 1.0. There is no corresponding entry in Future Considerations, so the deferral is invisible at the page that owns deferred surfaces, degrading discoverability.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** multi-commit-interaction
**Introducing commits:** 126ff8a — pi-loom spec: Step 0 capability probe rewrite + V1.0 deferrals + Choice B (2026-05-06, Thomas Andersen); eb33bf9 — pi-loom spec: resolve "Post-probe SDK-shape drift has no stated routing or operator attribution" (2026-05-07, Thomas Andersen)
**History:** The bundled defects have distinct (non-causal) origins, so the union spans two commits. The Step 0 capability-probe rewrite 126ff8a introduced obligations 1, 2, and 3 simultaneously: it added the `PiExtensionAPI` adapter-layer wording in the *On failure* bullet, the invalid-SemVer-`semver.valid`-routes-to-`probe-failed` clause alongside a throw-only *Self-failure* `details.cause` contract, and the "MUST stay disjoint" sentence in Step 0(c). Obligation 4's inline deferral entered one day later in eb33bf9, which added the *Post-probe SDK-shape drift* section (including the `details.kind`-discriminator-deferred-past-loom-1.0 statement). The 2026-06-04 commit f5e89f4 only relocated the content from `docs/spec/` into `docs/spec_topics/` (the size-cap collapse) and did not introduce any of the four defects.

## Solution approach

Move the discriminator-deferral statement out of the *Post-probe SDK-shape drift* section into `future-considerations/surface-extensions.md` as a deferred-surface entry, leaving at most a brief forward-link in the drift section. Keep the loom-1.0 behavioural statement inline (silent drift is undefined behaviour; `loom/runtime/internal-error` does not discriminate host-drift from loom-defect).

## Solution constraints

- The relocated entry MUST NOT be added to the inventoried seam set counted in the GOV-31 "Forward-compatibility seams" 12-seam tally.
