## Problem

The `lexical.md` **Number literals** bullet states the type-compatibility relation `integer ⊑ number` ("`integer` widens implicitly to `number` in arithmetic and assignment positions; the reverse is `loom/parse/integer-narrowing`"), which is a subtyping fact rather than a lexical one. `type-system.md` already owns that relation canonically as TYPE-2 (`id="type-2"`) inside its closed `⊑` list, a section that declares itself normative. The relation is therefore stated twice with inverted ownership: TYPE-2 defers outward to Lexical, while the lexical copy under-scopes widening to "arithmetic and assignment positions" whereas TYPE-2 governs every compatibility site. Compounding this, `diagnostics/code-registry-parse.md` names Lexical as the owning page for `loom/parse/integer-narrowing` while `frontmatter/frontmatter-fields-a.md` cites `type-system.md#type-compatibility` for the same rule.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** multi-commit-interaction
**Introducing commits:** `8eea523` ("spec: fill implementation-blocking gaps"), `0226b80` ("pi-loom plan: resolve \"V15c references an undefined compatibility relation\"")
**History:** `8eea523` (2026-05-04) added the new Lexical Structure section to the then-monolithic `spec.md`; its Number-literals bullet introduced "`integer` widens implicitly to `number` in arithmetic and assignment positions; the reverse is a parse error" at a time when no type-compatibility section existed, so Lexical was the only home. `fecb504` (same day) split the corpus per-topic, carrying the bullet verbatim into `lexical.md` (pure relocation). `0226b80` (2026-05-05) then introduced the formal `⊑` relation in `type-system.md`, with TYPE-2 stating `integer ⊑ number`, but did not consolidate the pre-existing lexical copy — instead wiring TYPE-2 to cite Lexical. The duplication and inverted ownership are the interaction of the original placement (`8eea523`) and the later canonical-relation addition (`0226b80`) that left the earlier copy in place.

## Solution approach

Move the `integer ⊑ number` compatibility relation to be owned solely by TYPE-2 (`id="type-2"`), stating the relation and its reverse diagnostic directly there and dropping the outward deferral to Lexical. Rewrite the `lexical.md` **Number literals** widening sentence as a cross-reference to `type-system.md#type-2`, retaining the literal-token type assignment and per-token magnitude diagnostics. Update the `loom/parse/integer-narrowing` owning-page column in `diagnostics/code-registry-parse.md` to point at `type-system.md#type-2`.

## Solution constraints

- `lexical.md` MUST retain a by-name reference to the `loom/parse/integer-narrowing` diagnostic that backs its per-token magnitude rules; only the `integer ⊑ number` compatibility relation moves out.
