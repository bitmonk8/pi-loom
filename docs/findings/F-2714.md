## Problem

`H6a` (`docs/plan_topics/H6a-live-corpus-activation.md`) gates its completion on three release-time activities that fail independently, need different execution environments, and produce separate evidence records, yet must all be discharged in one task and attested in one gate-activation commit message: the nine-item manual release-time residue inspection (`conventions.md#release-time-residue-inspection`), the manual real-host smoke run against a live Pi host (`real-host-smoke-gate.md`), and the mechanical flip of the `H5a`/`H5c` closing gate to its live-corpus footing. Each fails on its own and recovers differently, and a partial outcome (e.g. smoke green but a residue item dirty) has no clean representation at the leaf's completion tag. The three independently-failable verifications are entangled where remediation is most expensive.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** multi-commit-interaction
**Introducing commits:** 5353dd7 — resolve "Release-gate activation has no owning leaf" (2026-06-10, Thomas Andersen); 0bd1978 — resolve "Manual real-host smoke acceptance belongs in H6a Ships when, not Tests" (2026-06-17, Thomas Andersen); ee9acd0 — pin H6a as residue-inspection owner with evidence record (2026-06-20, Thomas Andersen)
**History:** `H6a` was created in `5353dd7` as the single owner of the mechanical gate-activation flip. The manual real-host smoke acceptance was folded into its `Ships when` in `0bd1978`, and the nine-item residue-inspection ownership was pinned onto the same leaf in `ee9acd0`. The bundle is the cumulative result of attaching two additional, separately-failable release-time obligations onto the original activation leaf; no single commit introduced all three.

## Solution approach

Split the three release-time activities into separate leaves so each independently-failable obligation lands and records its own evidence. Add a new H6-phase leaf for the residue inspection, citing `conventions.md#release-time-residue-inspection` and sequenced after `H5b`. Add a new H6-phase leaf for the real-host smoke run, citing `real-host-smoke-gate.md` and depending on `H7a` (the fixture producer). Narrow `H6a` to retain only the mechanical closing-gate flip plus its removed-mapping / removed-citation reddening assertions, with its **Ships when** confirming the two new leaves' evidence records are present and clean.

## Solution constraints

- `H6a` keeps its ID and remains the terminal release-gate flip, so `plan.md` item 5, the `coverage-matrix.md` intro, `H5b`, and `real-host-smoke-gate.md` references to it as gate-activation owner stay correct.
- Keep `conventions.md` and `coverage-matrix.md` consistent when adding the new leaves, and allocate new leaf IDs as the next free letters in the H6 phase per `## Plan rules` (never reuse a retired ID).
