## Problem

`schemas.md` §Object schema's trailing sentence — "The non-existence and the explicit-`null` cases are conflated, matching strict-mode provider behaviour." — admits two incompatible readings. One reading takes "conflated" at the validation level (an absent property is accepted and treated as `null`), which contradicts the same sentence's `required`/`additionalProperties: false` invariant and the `PIC-11` no-conversion / no-defaults contract. The other reading takes it at the loom-side language level (no `field?: T` shorthand exists, so absence is simply not expressible). Only the language-level reading is consistent with the rest of the corpus, but the prose gives an implementer reading this section alone no way to discriminate.

## Solution approach

Rewrite the cited trailing sentence to scope "conflated" explicitly to the loom-side language surface and reaffirm the validation-level contract. Add a forward-link to `PIC-11` (`./pi-integration-contract/host-interfaces-services.md#pic-11`) as the normative owner of the no-coercion / no-defaults rule.
