## Problem

`V1a` lists `V7a` in its `Deps.`, but neither leaf names the contract that edge stands for. `V1a`'s `Adds.` names only the `V8b` `FileSystem` byte-read seam and never states what the lexer consumes from `V7a` to emit its `loom/load/invalid-encoding` and `loom/parse/*` codes; `V7a`'s `Adds.` describes the delivery surface (the `Diagnostic` shape, content-line format, `loom-system-note` envelope, fallback chain, multi-error batching) but declares no producer-facing emission entry point a parse-pipeline producer calls to hand a constructed `Diagnostic` into that machinery. Under the `conventions.md` `Adds.`-binding rule, a cross-leaf seam binds only when the producer declares it and the consumer names it; here neither end names it, so the seam is illustrative on both sides and does not bind. `V1a` is the first vertical producer to hit this; every other `V7a` consumer (`V7b`, `V9a`, `V9d`, `V9k`) faces the same undefined emission surface.

The defect entered when the plan was first authored; no subsequent leaf-edit introduced or removed it.

## Solution approach

In `docs/plan_topics/V7a-diagnostics-primitive.md` `Adds.`, name the producer-facing emission entry point `V7a` owns — the surface a parse/load/runtime producer calls to submit a constructed `Diagnostic` (and the scan-time batch) into `V7a`'s batching and `loom-system-note` delivery machinery, distinct from the delivery-side envelope already described. In `docs/plan_topics/V1a-lexer-core.md` `Adds.`, name that same `V7a` emission entry point as the surface through which the lexer emits `loom/load/invalid-encoding` and its `loom/parse/*` codes, alongside the existing `V8b` byte-read seam, so the `Deps.` `V7a` edge has a stated contract. Mirror the seam reference in `docs/plan_topics/V1a-T-lexer-core.md` so the paired tests assert the lexer's codes fire through the named `V7a` surface.

## Solution constraints

- Keep the spec read-only: the `Diagnostic` wire shape and delivery channel are already pinned in `docs/spec_topics/diagnostics/diagnostic-shape.md` and `docs/spec_topics/diagnostics.md`; this fix adds only the plan-internal producer→`V7a` call surface, not a new spec obligation.
- Per-`.loom` single-`sendMessage` batching stays owned by `V7a`; producers must not each call `pi.sendMessage` directly.
