## Problem

`drain-state-contract.md` carries three normative MUSTs that lack a co-located `PIC-N` REQ-ID anchor: the `LoomRegistry.readDrainState` closed three-arm dispatch contract (under `id="loom-registry-drain-state-contract"`), the no-fourth-arm / no-alternative-gating-field prohibition (in the `id="idempotent-handler-clause"` clause), and the `readDrainState` read-failure fail-safe routing arm (under `id="read-failure-fallback"`). The `drain()`-called-first and no-throw obligations in the same cluster are also un-anchored. Without a co-located REQ-ID anchor at each defining obligation site, GOV-9's `#prefix-n` cross-link contract is unsatisfiable and GOV-22's progressive-coinage obligation is unmet.

Anchor the `drain()`-called-first and no-throw obligations in the same cluster on the same footing.
