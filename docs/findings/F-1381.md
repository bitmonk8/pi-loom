## Finding

`PIC-23` — "the spawn passes the loom-constructed `ResourceLoader` adapter and does not route the loom's `system:` through the `DefaultResourceLoader.systemPromptOverride` construction channel" — appears as a Tests bullet in both `V9i-subagent-isolation.md` and its paired `V9i-T-subagent-isolation.md`, but `V9i`'s **Ships when** clause does not name it. That clause selectively enumerates observable outcomes — `npm test` spawns an isolated subagent session, asserts `dispose()`-in-`finally` idempotency, and asserts the `ERR-8` live-surface outcome — and stops short of the PIC-23 ResourceLoader-routing assertion.

Per `conventions.md` §Leaf format, the binding surfaces of a leaf are its Tests REQ-ID bullets together with its **Ships when** gate. The PIC-23 Tests bullet binds, and the paired-leaf TDD ritual (`V9i-T`'s "the tests above" gate) makes `V9i` ship only after every test including PIC-23 passes — so no implementer ships `V9i` without the PIC-23 test green. The gap is confined to a reader treating `V9i`'s Ships-when in isolation as the authoritative done-condition: PIC-23 is the one new assertion of this delta and, unlike the comparably-significant `ERR-8` witness (whose Ships-when gate was added when ERR-8 landed), it was not reflected in Ships-when.

The immediately prior Ships-when edit was `f892019` (F-1243, "add ERR-8 red-first witness to V9i-T + Ships-when gate"), which did add its new assertion (`ERR-8`) to Ships-when; the PIC-23 cascade did not follow that pattern. The omission is original to `e690bb0`, not a later regression.

## Solution Space

**Shape:** single

### Recommendation

In `docs/plan_topics/V9i-subagent-isolation.md`, append the PIC-23 outcome to the **Ships when** clause so it lists the spawn-routing assertion alongside the existing dispose-idempotency and `ERR-8` outcomes — i.e. that the spawn passes the loom-constructed `ResourceLoader` adapter and does not route the loom's `system:` through `DefaultResourceLoader.systemPromptOverride`. Phrase it as an externally observable outcome consistent with the existing clause's voice; the literal cited mechanism is the PIC-23 Tests-bullet text. Leave `V9i-T`'s generic "the tests above" Ships-when unchanged (it already subsumes PIC-23), and leave the PIC-23 Tests bullets and the `coverage-matrix.md` row as-is.

## Consequence

**Severity:** cosmetic

A reader consulting `V9i`'s Ships-when in isolation as the leaf's done-condition will not see PIC-23 among the gating outcomes, even though it is the assertion this delta added. Because the PIC-23 Tests bullet binds and the `V9i-T` "the tests above" ritual gates `V9i` on all its tests, no implementer ships `V9i` without the PIC-23 test passing; the impact is limited to Ships-when readability and intra-leaf consistency with the `ERR-8` precedent.
