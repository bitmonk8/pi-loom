## Problem

Step 0 (c) of `capability-probe.md` opens by enumerating the factory-probable subset as "items 1, 2, 3, 4, and 6 (five capabilities, nine function members …)", excluding capability 5 (cancellation propagation) from both the item list and the per-capability member tally. The same step contradicts that exclusion: its dual-coverage sentence states capability 5 is covered jointly by step (b) and step (c)'s `AgentSession.prototype.abort` member, and that bullet itself is annotated "(capability 3 / capability 5)". An implementer wiring the `FACTORY_PROBABLE_CAPABILITIES` partition cannot tell whether capability 5 belongs in the set — the item list and tally give `{1, 2, 3, 4, 6}` while the dual-coverage sentence and bullet annotation give `{1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6}`. Because the consuming partition assertion is a self-consistency check, either reading passes and the divergence propagates silently into the `sdk-capability-missing` diagnostic interpretation.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** single-commit-regression
**Introducing commit:** `5c8e630` (2026-05-07) — *pi-loom spec: resolve "Subagent cancellation wiring depends on a non-existent createAgentSession({ signal }) option"*
**History:** Prior to `5c8e630` step (c) read "...items 1, 2, 3, 4, and 6 (five capabilities, seven function members — capabilities 4 and 6 each contribute two function members). Item 5 (cancellation propagation) is covered by step (b) above." — internally consistent: capability 5 was excluded from the factory-probable subset and step (b) alone was credited with covering it. `5c8e630` added a new factory-time probe for `AgentSession.prototype.abort` (to surface a missing handle-side cancellation primitive as `loom/load/host-incompatible` rather than a spawn-time `TypeError`), annotated the new bullet "(capability 3 / capability 5)", and rewrote the orientation sentence to "Item 5 (cancellation propagation) is covered jointly by step (b) above ... and step (c) below". The same commit bumped the function-member count from seven to eight but did **not** update the item list `{1, 2, 3, 4, 6}` or the per-capability contribution tally to reflect that capability 5 now contributes a probed member at step (c); the contradiction between the parenthetical's "five capabilities" / `{1, 2, 3, 4, 6}` framing and the new dual-coverage prose dates from that commit. Subsequent edits to the paragraph (`3e6ce2e` 2026-06-05 removed `ExtensionCommandContext.waitForIdle` and bumped the count to nine; `aa334c2` and `d8d5ad0` expanded surrounding text) preserved the contradiction unchanged.

## Solution approach

Rewrite Step 0 (c)'s opening parenthetical in `capability-probe.md` to include capability 5 in the factory-probable subset, matching the step's dual-coverage sentence and the `AgentSession.prototype.abort` bullet's `(capability 3 / capability 5)` annotation. Capability 5 is discharged jointly by step (b)'s `AbortSignal` / `AbortController` shape probe and step (c)'s `AgentSession.prototype.abort` member, both of which belong to the factory-probable set. Update item 5's framing in the SDK capability inventory (`#sdk-capability-inventory`) to state its factory-probable status consistently with the reconciled parenthetical.

## Solution constraints

- The nine function-member total is fixed: the `AgentSession.prototype.abort` member is shared between capabilities 3 and 5 and must be counted once, so reconciling capability 5 into the subset must not add or remove a probed member.
