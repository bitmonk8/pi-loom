## Problem

The *Host prerequisites for the degraded-state branch* section (`#degraded-state-host-prerequisites`) presents clauses (a)–(d) as a single flat list spanning two distinct priority classes. Clauses (b)–(d) are best-effort presuppositions re-audited on each Pi minor bump; clause (a) is a blocking open contradiction that gates implementation of the SM-4 / SM-5 / SM-6 (and SM-3b) degraded-state obligations. The distinction is carried only in prose (the intro's "Clause (a) is the exception" sentence, the "Open contradiction (blocking, not a deferral)" callout, and a mid-clause-(a) MUST-resolve sentence), with no structural element making the gate visible on a skim. An implementer skimming the four-clause list can misclassify clause (a) as a deferred presupposition like (b)–(d) and begin implementing SM-4/5/6 against the unresolved contradiction.

## Issue introduction
**Verdict:** introduced-by-commit
**Introducing commit:** `85d2f2d` — "pi-loom spec-plan: fix F-1658 — remove misfiled Open-contradictions bucket; flag clause (a) as open contradiction in host-prerequisites" (2026-06-27)
**History:**
- The four-clause degraded-state presupposition list (clauses (a)–(d)) on `host-prerequisites.md` predates the defect; clause (a)'s contradicts-Pi's-lifecycle framing was sharpened in `7447d1e` (2026-06-10) and the list carried through the spec split in `f5e89f4`.
- During that earlier arrangement the blocking framing had its **own dedicated home**: an "Open contradictions (blocking, not deferrals)" bucket in `future-considerations/model-changes-and-non-goals.md`, structurally separated from the (b)–(d) presuppositions and pointing at clause (a) by reference.
- `85d2f2d` (F-1658) correctly removed that bucket as misfiled — but inlined a new "Open contradiction (blocking, not a deferral)" callout box directly into the `host-prerequisites.md` clause list, above clause (a). This collapsed the previously-separated blocking framing into the same single list as the (b)–(d) presuppositions, producing the interleaving (one list, two priority classes, distinction in prose only) this finding flags. `git log -S "Open contradiction (blocking, not a deferral)"` confirms the phrase first appears in `85d2f2d`; `git log -S` for the relocated bucket confirms it was deleted from `model-changes-and-non-goals.md` in the same commit.

## Solution approach

Restructure the *Host prerequisites for the degraded-state branch* section so the blocking-gate clause and the best-effort presuppositions are separated into distinct, labelled priority groups visible on a skim, without relocating any normative obligation. Group clause (a) under a blocking-gate label and clauses (b)–(d) under a best-effort-presupposition label, and reposition the existing "Open contradiction" callout so it heads the blocking-gate group rather than sitting inline among the clauses.

## Solution constraints

- Out of scope: moving or duplicating the SM-4/5/6 (or SM-3b) obligations, which are owned by `session-model-and-appendix.md`.
- Out of scope: relocating the blocking framing into a non-goals bucket; the priority separation must remain inside `host-prerequisites.md` next to the clauses it classifies.
