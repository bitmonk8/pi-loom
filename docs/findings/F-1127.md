## Problem

The `#### System-prompt structure (normative)` sub-section of `docs/spec_topics/binder/binder-bypass-and-envelope.md` enumerates eleven distinct normative obligations carrying RFC-2119 MUST/MUST NOT modal tokens — the eight numbered structural items plus the *Type display*, *Default-literal rendering*, and *Parameter-line reference renderings* renderings — and none carries a `BNDR-N` REQ-ID anchor in the GOV-1 dual form. The section is standing GOV-22 progressive-coinage residue: GOV-9's `#prefix-n` cross-link contract is unsatisfiable for any page or finding that needs to depend on a single structural item. Downstream consumers already advertise the gap — coverage-matrix row `cka-45` tags the whole cluster `(un-anchored; GOV-22 residue)`, and the `V11d` / `V11d-T` Tests bullets must enumerate items by number rather than cite REQ-IDs.
