## Problem

The terminal-outcomes-aggregator paragraph in `overview-and-orientation.md` and the Terminal-outcomes section in `error-model.md` (`#terminal-outcomes`) both exclude certain hard-ceiling cases from "the Failure-cause enumeration", a capitalised phrase that reads as a defined term-of-art but is never introduced, anchored, or glossed anywhere in the corpus. A reader cannot tell whether it names the inline *fail* arm of the success/fail/cancelled trichotomy or the separately-owned closed list in `#terminal-outcomes`. The two referents are not interchangeable, and the second reading is self-contradictory: binder argument-binding failure is item ERR-5 in the pre-evaluation list, so it cannot be both listed there and "excluded from the Failure-cause enumeration". The intended referent is the *fail* arm of the trichotomy.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** single-commit
**Introducing commits:** 8168ea8 — pi-loom spec: resolve "T30 ceiling #4 model-driven row trichotomy carve-out" (2026-05-10, Thomas Andersen)
**History:** The term "Failure-cause enumeration" did not exist in the corpus before 8168ea8 (its parent has zero occurrences). That commit added the ceiling-#4 trichotomy carve-out to the terminal-outcomes-aggregator paragraph in `spec.md` and coined the capitalised phrase as an exclusion target without introducing or anchoring it. Later commits f5e89f4 (size-cap split) and 81bf52a (collapse `spec/` into `spec_topics/`) only moved the content mechanically — relocating the paragraph into `overview-and-orientation.md` with no content edits — so the undefined-term defect has been carried verbatim since 8168ea8.

## Solution approach

Rewrite each occurrence of "the Failure-cause enumeration" to name its referent explicitly — the *fail* arm of the success/fail/cancelled trichotomy — so the exclusion reads as "these cases are not evaluation Failures" rather than referencing an undefined alias. Apply this at all three occurrences: one in `overview-and-orientation.md`'s `#terminal-outcomes-aggregator` paragraph, and two in `error-model.md`'s `#terminal-outcomes` section (the Failure bullet's exclusion sentence and the trailing "no final value flows" sentence).
