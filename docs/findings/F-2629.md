## Problem

`docs/spec_topics/pi-integration-contract/session-only-degraded-state.md` opens with an inline *Implementation gate* blockquote that scopes by clause: two clauses are buildable now — the `## Drain-state tag write (state-independent)` setter contract and the **Isolation** clause under `## Diagnostic emission` — while every other clause (the branch-behaviour prose and acceptance criteria (a)–(m)) is gated pending clause (a). Those two non-gated clauses are physically scattered among the gated material, so the only thing separating buildable-now contracts from blocked content is the preamble enumeration, and the consuming `V9m`/`V9g` slices must reconstruct the partition by hand. Separately, the gate is release-status/governance metadata threaded through a normative contract page, forcing every reader to parse and continuously hold blocked-clause status before knowing which requirements apply to loom 1.0.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** multi-commit-interaction
**Introducing commits:** `b8f004f` (2026-06-19, "fix F-0719 — SM-4/5/6/3b degraded-state implementation-gate contingency markers"); `8bcd52a` (2026-06-23, "fix F-1163 — session-only-degraded-state gate scopes by clause not page")
**History:** The corpus is git-tracked. `git log --follow` shows the page was first carved into its own topic file at `f5e89f4` (the spec-set size-cap split), with no gate preamble. `git log -S "Implementation gate"` localises the gate's arrival to `b8f004f`, whose diff adds the inline *Implementation gate* blockquote — but at that point the gate was **file-level** ("The obligations on this page are blocked"), so the "governance metadata inline" half of the defect dates here while no gated/non-gated interleaving yet existed. `git log -S "scopes by clause, not by file"` localises the second half to `8bcd52a`, whose diff (confirmed via `git show`) rewrites the blockquote to scope **by clause**, enumerating the two non-gated contracts in prose while leaving them physically interleaved among the gated clauses. The interleaving defect the finding describes is thus the product of the two commits interacting: `b8f004f` introduced the inline gate; `8bcd52a` split it by clause without a matching physical reorganization of the page.

## Solution approach

Restructure the page so its physical layout reflects the by-clause gate partition: move the non-gated `## Drain-state tag write (state-independent)` section and the **Isolation** clause from `## Diagnostic emission` to the top of the page, and separate them from the gated branch-behaviour clauses and acceptance criteria (a)–(m) with a divider heading. Move the release/governance status (which clauses are blocked, the gating open question, the clearing verdict) out to the governance/phase-status home, and replace the inline *Implementation gate* blockquote with a forward-reference to that home.

## Solution constraints

- Preserve every existing in-page anchor when relocating clauses, including the **Isolation** clause's `try`/`catch` cross-reference target anchor that sibling pages point at.
- Out of scope: reverting the clause-level scoping — the two non-gated contracts are genuinely buildable now and the by-clause gate is correct.
- Keep the content as a single page; do not split into multiple files.
