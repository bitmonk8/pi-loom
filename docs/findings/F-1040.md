## Problem

Three cross-references in the **Failures** region of `docs/spec_topics/tool-calls.md` no longer resolve to the section that normatively owns the cited concept — residue of the spec's split into sub-pages. The *Non-settling Promise* link (line 29) to `./overview-and-orientation.md#hard-runtime-ceilings` lands on the Hard-ceilings orientation aggregator, not NOCEIL-1's defining claim, which lives in `hard-ceilings/ceiling-invariants-and-audit.md` under `#no-additional-ceilings`. The two `[Query — Tool calls during a query](./query.md)` links (lines 34 and 38) target the `query.md` hub page, whose body is only a `## Contents` TOC with no matching section — the owning section is `query/query-tool-loop.md#tool-calls-during-a-query`.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** multi-commit-interaction
**Introducing commits:** `11ef3b9` (2026-04-02) introduced the NOCEIL-1 link with target `../spec.md#hard-runtime-ceilings`; `7159420` (2026-06-03) introduced the two `[Query — Tool calls during a query](./query.md)` links on lines 34 and 38; `f5e89f4` (2026-06-04) split `spec.md` into `spec_topics/` and repathed the NOCEIL link to `../spec/overview-and-orientation.md#hard-runtime-ceilings`; `81bf52a` (later) flattened that to `./overview-and-orientation.md#hard-runtime-ceilings`.
**History:** The defect is the residue of two independent splits. (a) `git log -L '/Non-settling Promise/,+1:docs/spec_topics/tool-calls.md'` shows the NOCEIL link was originally correct against a monolithic `spec.md` (`11ef3b9`); the bulk-split commits `f5e89f4` and `81bf52a` mechanically rewrote the *path* but did not re-target the *fragment* to NOCEIL-1's new home in `hard-ceilings/ceiling-invariants-and-audit.md`. (b) `git show 7159420 -- docs/spec_topics/tool-calls.md` shows both `./query.md` references were authored already pointing at a `query.md` that, by then, was already a hub-only page (commit `f5e89f4` had already split out `query/query-tool-loop.md` a day earlier); the authoring of `7159420` therefore introduced the stale form directly. The cross-page-split repath sweep in `f5e89f4` / `81bf52a` either ran before `7159420` for the affected paragraphs or missed them, so neither the original split nor the later authoring round caught the targets.

## Solution approach

Repoint the line-29 NOCEIL-1 link to `./hard-ceilings/ceiling-invariants-and-audit.md#no-additional-ceilings` (the block anchor holding the four NOCEIL claims) and align its display text with the cited owner page. Repoint both `[Query — Tool calls during a query](./query.md)` references (lines 34 and 38) to `./query/query-tool-loop.md#tool-calls-during-a-query`, matching the sub-page cross-reference form already used for the `#tool-call-loop-bound` sibling in the same line-38 sentence.
