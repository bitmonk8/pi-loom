## Problem

`V13c` (Query tool loop and typed two-phase) carries Tests/Adds spanning four distinct spec obligations across four spec topics: free-phase round counting and the `max_rounds:0` / forced-respond boundary (`query-tool-loop.md`, CIO-4 / QRY); ceiling-#2 exhaustion and the ceiling-#4 co-fire `masked:["ceiling#2"]` surface (`ceilings-3-and-4.md`); the model-driven parallel tool-call batch `Promise.allSettled` settle-all-then-construct-next-turn rule with independent per-sibling lowering (`tool-calls.md` §Concurrency, TOOL); and the cancellation swallowing-handler three-channel suppression at the `@`-query provider abandonable-Promise site (`cancellation.md`, the per-site routing witness named by `V17a`). The latter two obligations are independently assertable at the session-double level, have no dependency on round counting, cite separate spec topics, and pull `V8a` / `V8c` Deps edges onto the leaf. A reviewer must verify free-phase loop semantics, two-axis ceiling arbitration, batch concurrency, and cross-channel cancellation suppression in a single commit — four orthogonal recognition paths.

## Issue introduction

**Verdict:** multi-commit-interaction
**Introducing commits:** `db4ef95` (2026-06-10, "resolve 'Parallel-batch settle-and-independent-lowering rule has no asserting leaf'"); `75a9bcd` (2026-06-11, "resolve 'V17a substrate-level suppression test claims four-site coverage'")
**History:** At creation (`c6a664e`, 2026-06-10) V13c was scoped to obligations 1 and 2 only — the free-phase loop, typed two-phase `complete()`, `max_rounds:0`, and ceiling-#2 evaluation (Deps `V13c-T, V13b, V9c, V16a, V5e`). Obligation 3 (the `tool-calls.md` §Concurrency parallel-batch assertion) was grafted on in `db4ef95` to give the parallel-batch settle-and-independent-lowering rule a closing leaf, and refined in `75b6a9b` (2026-06-10). Obligation 4 (the cancellation swallowing-handler three-channel suppression and its `V8a` Deps edge) was grafted on in `75a9bcd` to supply the `@`-query per-site routing witness that `V17a`'s four-site coverage claim requires. The over-bundling is therefore an accretion: two separate coverage-closing fixes each attached an independent obligation to V13c rather than to a dedicated leaf.

## Solution approach

Split the two off-topic obligations — the `tool-calls.md` §Concurrency parallel-batch assertion and the `cancellation.md` `@`-query swallowing-handler routing assertion — out of `V13c` / `V13c-T` onto a new leaf pair (or two), leaving the free-phase loop and ceiling-#2/#4 obligations in `V13c`. Retarget every cross-reference to `V13c` for the relocated obligations in lockstep: the coverage-matrix `tool-calls.md (TOOL)` row, the `V17a` / `V17a-T` four-site routing list (`` `V14a`, `V13c`, `V15a`, `V9i` ``), and `H7a`'s Deps plus its permitted-code / golden-derivation reference set. Keep the CIO-4 "a parallel batch counts as one slot" round-counting clause in `V13c` — it is round-counting, not batch concurrency. Drop `V8a` from `V13c` Deps if no surviving obligation references it.
