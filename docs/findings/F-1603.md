## Problem

The *Generated-output-in-src limitation (acknowledged soft surface)* sub-section of the `id="audit-scope"` paragraph in `audit-resolution.md` closes with a future-work disposition clause: "widening the inventory-closure audit's family-set to subsume this check is a PIC edit deferred past loom 1.0." This is phase-2 roadmap commentary embedded inside a normative section — it records what a later spec edition might do, not a constraint a loom-1.0 implementer must satisfy. The preceding "but PIC does not require it" phrase already conveys the not-mandated disposition, and the closure rule earlier in the paragraph already forbids widening to a sixth family, so the clause is redundant and goes stale once the corpus advances past 1.0.

## Solution approach

Delete the trailing clause "; widening the inventory-closure audit's family-set to subsume this check is a PIC edit deferred past loom 1.0" from the *Generated-output-in-src limitation* sentence in the `id="audit-scope"` paragraph, terminating the sentence at "but PIC does not require it."
